Autobot Jazz (Botcon 2008 Exclusive)

Allegiance: Autobot
Size : Deluxe (comes in a 6-pack with Goldbug , Grimlock , Optimus Prime , and Starscream w/ Razorclaw )
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Milky white, white, black, clear tinted plastic, and some red, blue, metallic silver, and orangish yellow
Rating : 7.6

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Cybertron Crosswise. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Cybertron Crosswise here .)

Unlike the other "Mirror Universe" Autobots, Jazz looks like, well... "normal" Jazz. Granted, I'm sure because this mold was never done as anything resembling Jazz before, so it's completely different color-wise from any other use of the Cybertron Crosswise mold, but still... it seems a bit of a waste to make "evil" Jazz look almost exactly the same color-wise as the normal good Jazz. That's not to say it's not a good color scheme, though-- white and black have always been excellent colors, and there's a bit more blue detailing on Jazz than I'm used to, which helps to spice up the color scheme a bit. The white painted on his clear tinted parts is noticeably lighter than the milky white plastic used for his other white parts, though, which is a bit disconcerting in car mode. He's also got the usual red/white/blue stripes in vehicle mode, and the "4" on his doors and front of his car mode. There are some minor changes to our typical lovable Jazz, though, and that comes down to his logos-- instead of "Meister Racing", it's "Massacre Racing". He's also got "Kill to Win" logos above his front wheels, and black lines have been painted above his front headlights to make it look like the headlights are eyes and the black lines are "eyebrows", so he's "glaring" at you as he charges forward. These are really nice touches, and so incredibly corny that they loop right back around to being really awesome.
Jazz has gotten a head remold, so that the head actually looks like Jazz in robot mode. It's a pretty spot-on representation of Jazz, with all the details and proportions right-on. His expression looks rather neutral, though-- I would have liked an "angry" or "mischevious grin" look molded onto his face to make it more obvious he's a bad guy. Jazz also has had the mold's Cyber Key changed, to a "normal Autobot" Cyber Key instead.
Botcon Jazz has a decent color scheme, plenty of paint apps, a good head remold, and some really nice changes to his logos in car mode. However, there's just not enough about him to really classify him as "Mirror Universe" Jazz beyond said logos, which I think is his greatest flaw. He just looks a bit out-of-place with the other darker-colored Mirror Universe Autobots.


Autobot Jazz Bio :
FUNCTION: Bodyguard/Special Operations
MOTTO: "Destroy it with style or don't bother destroying it at all!"
Autobot Jazz and his brother Ricochet serve as Optimus Prime's powerful and merciless bodyguards. While the Seeker unit is designed to hunt down and destroy targets, Jazz and Ricochet stay with Optimus Prime to protect him against any and all threats. Jazz is one of Optimus Prime's most loyal and trusted aides. He only wishes to serve the glorious future that Optimus laid out for the Autobots and he is completely convinced of his cause. Recently, Jazz intercepted a series of transmissions from a planet called Earth. While garbled, he managed to decode and translate enough to become enamored with the planet's culture. As a result, he gave himself a new design pattern with his own special modifications. Everything he does has to be with style, from talking to simply transforming. While Optimus Prime finds this behavior strange, he allows Jazz some latitude from standard Autobot military practice as long as he remains loyal to him.
