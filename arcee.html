Arcee (BotCon 2001 Exclusive)

Allegiance : Maximal [Subgroup: Wrecker] (although she has interchangable Maximal and Autobot orbs)
Size : Mega
Difficulty of Transformation : Hard
Color Scheme : White, chrome red, light pink, light blue, light black, and some dark blue, orange-yellow, and red
Rating : 7.2

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Blackarachnia Transmetal 2. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Blackarachnia Transmetal 2 here .)

Finally, Transfans get a toy of Arcee! (For those of you who don't know, Arcee was a female character in the original Transformers that, sadly, never got a toy.) Of course, this toy is really just a repaint of Blackarachnia Transmetal 2, but it's close enough- it even has a color scheme similar to Arcee's old form!
Arcee's beast mode is a spider. While a Transmetal 2 spider is cool nonetheless, the white and light blue in this mode don't mix together well with the whole "creepy spider" feel that this mold should have going for it. The only mold change apparent in this mode is the fact that, instead of a spark crystal that can switch between Maximal and Predacon, Arcee has a spark crystal that can switch between a Maximal symbol and an Autobot symbol! This is the first mold change made to a Botcon toy, even if it is a slight one.
In robot mode, Arcee's lack of paint detailing becomes more apparent. Coupled with her white skin, this makes her look almost like a prototype in this mode, and this is NOT good. For such an expensive toy as this, more paint detailing would have been appreciated. As with the beast mode, the white, light blue, and pink colorations do not fit with this toy. Another mold change to this toy is apparent in this mode as well: the stupid "extra bra" is gone, leaving behind the "armor bra" used on Blackarachnia Transmetal 2 in the show, which, in my opinion, looks much cooler. There's even an Autobot symbol engraved on the upper left corner of it, which is a neat reference to Arcee's roots as well!
All in all, it's nice to see a Botcon Exclusive that has some mold changes, but that's about all Arcee excels at. She still has the same problems as Blackarachnia Transmetal 2, plus the bad paint detailing and the odd white, pink, and light blue coloration. I recommend Blackarachnia Transmetal 2 above this toy, personally.


Arcee Bio :
FUNCTION: Valkyrie
MOTTO: "Looks are always deceiving."
Once viewed as an almost maternal figure among Autobots, Arcee has continued to find ways to improve herself since the days of the Great War. After a series of personal traumatic events, she secluded herself from her comrades, honing her combat skills and mental focus. Her mysterious reemergence with a new body has turned heads of friends and foes alike. Now a master of Crystalocution, by which she attacks her enemies at their fracture points, and the lost art of Tekkaido, Arcee continues to strike fear into her opponents. In her new form, she also wields deadly energy blades, localized deflector shields, and reportedly has precognizance of one's imminent death, thus making it difficult to gain her trust.
