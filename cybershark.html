Cybershark

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme : Cyan, Green-blue, and white, with spots of silver, red, and muddy brown
Rating : 7.8

Beast mode is a hammerhead shark, with a beautiful fading color scheme. Very nice, with no obvious clue hes a robot, except that his legs sort of stick out on his bottom side. There are practically no movable parts in this mode except for the front fins, which is kind of drag, but this is to be expected from a sea creature such as this.
Robot mode is very good, too, but his shark halves stick out a bit and interfere. His face looks more like that of a Predacons than a Maximals. His shark face can pop out of his missle launcher and he can hold it, but this doesnt seem to serve any purpose. He can shoot missles out of his belly. His metallic-shark belly looks very nice, and his tail scythe is cool-looking.
A nice-looking transformer, but with somewhat of a lot of extras in robot mode. His detailing is also only so-so.


Cybershark Bio :
FUNCTION: Ocean Attack
Just when the Predacons thought it was safe to go back in the water... Cybershark rises to the surface for a fearsome feeding frenzy! Like a turbo-charged torpedo speeding through the sea, Cybershark searches the ocean depths for Predacon enemies. Upon finding the enemy he launches his robotic hammerhead to knock'em out cold, then finishes the job in robot mode by filleting his prey with his switch blade tail!
