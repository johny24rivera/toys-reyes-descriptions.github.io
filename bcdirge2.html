Dirge (Botcon 2007 Exclusive)

Allegiance : Decepticon
Size : Deluxe (comes in a 5-pack with Bugbite , Dreadwind , Thrust , and Thundercracker )
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Blue, black, light orangish brown, and some white, light red, and silver
Rating : 9.5

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Classics Starscream. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Classics Starscream here .)

Dirge is the only "official" Classics Seeker repaint to not be completely accurate to his G1 self in vehicle mode. His actual G1 jet mode had slightly different wings, but since he shares the same mold changes as Ramjet , and Ramjet's wings are close enough to Dirge's, it's not a big issue for me (particularly if you consider how impractical/costly it would have been for the Botcon folks to remold TWO Seekers' wings). Dirge's blue is a bit lighter than it was on his G1 toy-- probably to distinguish it more from Thundercracker-- but otherwise the colors are spot-on G1, only with a bit more variety in robot mode. All of the colors come together very well-- blue, black, and light brown make for a great color combo, and the silver and red are used in just the right amount to break up the colors, and look great against the black and blue as well. I especially like how detailed his chest and waist are. The only complaint I have with Dirge when it comes to his colors is that he has the dull slavish-G1 white/red stripes down his wings, as opposed to the more dynamic stripes seen on the mass-released Classics Seekers.
Dirge is a great repaint of a great remold, and with the exception of the dull wing stripes in vehicle mode, I really don't have any complaints about him. My personal favorite of the Botcon 2007 exclusives.
