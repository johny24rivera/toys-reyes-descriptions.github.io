Mirage

Allegiance : Vehicon
Size : Basic
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Yellow, transparent blue, bluish green, silver-gray, and some black
Rating : 9.5

Vehicle mode is an Indy race car, and is a bit big for a basic. (Not that I'm complaining.) This mode looks very cool, with no extras whatsoever, unless you look very closely. The middle of the hands are visible from the back side, and the robot head can be viewed if you rotate his engine missles, but that's it. The detailing is pretty cool, and the yellow missles and laser attached to this race car make it look ready for battle. The only downside to this mode is that, besides the wheels, there are practically no other moveable parts- but hey, it's a car, what'd ya expect?
Robot mode isn't quite as good as the vehicle mode, but still looks very cool. The face looks quite futuristic and emotionless, like a Vehicon drone, and the laser on top of his head accents this. The missiles mounted on the sides of his arms are neat as well. This is the first basic I've seen with removable missiles! The downside, though, is you have to actually push them out with your finger- there's no firing mechanism. The missles, which are parts of his engine in vehicle mode, look a bit like hockey pucks. This, when coupled with his oversized lower legs and mask-like face, make this Vehicon look a little like a hockey player! There are downsides to this mode, though. There is a big extra- the front of the race car, which is attached to his back, is odd-looking. Although it doesn't get in the arms' way much, there is no locking mechanism, so it tends to get in the way when you move his legs. The second problem is his oversized legs. Although this may be a plus in your opinion, as it seems to resemble the old 80's line of Transformers, it isn't, in my opinion, because it the bottoms don't even slightly resemble feet, and look odd. The last problem is that his arms look like they're in a continual shrugging position, and it looks weird. However, the pros far outweigh the cons in this mode, so this mode ends up looking pretty nice.
A very nicely done Vehicon- great in both modes. Highly recommended.


Mirage Bio:
FUNCTION: Warrior
MOTTO: "Eat my dust!"
Mirage is a real trickster - and like most practical jokers, loves being devious. An experimental, high-speed weapon created by Megatron to breach the intelligence gap between Vehicon generals and their drones. Still, not very smart. Would be useless without Megatron's supervision. Enjoys playing tricks on fellow Vehicons. Can appear out of nowhere to attack Maximals. Sneakiest and most elusive of all Vehicons. Top speed of 300 miles per mega-cycle. Can instantly teleport up to 200 yards. Wields plasma mine blaster and dual missile launchers. Unpopular amongst fellow warriors.
