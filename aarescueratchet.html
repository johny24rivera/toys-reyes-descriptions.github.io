Autobot Ratchet (Rescue) [Autobot Alliance]

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation: Medium
Color Scheme: White, moderately dark red, moderately dark glittery gray, and some light milky gray, black, silver, transparent blue, dull golden yellow, and dark metallic gunmetal gray
Rating : 7.1

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the deluxe version of Revenge of the Fallen Ratchet. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the deluxe version of Revenge of the Fallen Ratchet here .)

Like the previous "Rescue Ratchet" movie redeco , this version of Ratchet now gives the G1 color scheme to the deluxe version of the mold. Generally, my comments about this color scheme are the same as any color scheme involving red, white, and black/gray-- it works fairly well, and though it's not the most original color scheme in the world, it's certainly effective on an ambulance and looks nice. Compared to the previous Rescue Ratchet movie deco job, though, I think the paint apps on this one are a little inferior. There's just a plain red stripe on the side of this version with "RESCUE" imprinted near the back end of said stripe-- certainly nothing really eye-catching or unusual. I'm also starting to miss the "heartbeat spike" in the lines on the sides of the toy, as that's become so big a part of Ratchet's look in recent years. One thing I do like about this version over the previous movie Rescue Ratchet is the increased amount of black, which helps give a bit more color variation and contrast to the toy, particularly in vehicle mode. The transparent blue windows, headlights, optics, and missile also look quite nice, and there's a bit more red on the toy as well (particularly visible in robot mode), which keeps him from being too one-tone with the white.
No mold changes have been made to Rescue Ratchet.
Rescue Ratchet is about what you'd expect from a G1-themed redeco of the mold, though they're still mixing it up a little from the Voyager class Rescue Ratchet deco, at least. Compared to that previous version, I like that this one has a greater amount of black and red (which is also a little darker for contrast on this version), but this one's paint apps are a bit more plain. Overall I'd recommend this over the original release of this mold, though.
