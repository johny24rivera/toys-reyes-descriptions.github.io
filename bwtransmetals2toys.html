Beast Wars Transmetals 2

After the explosion caused by Megatrons Transmetal 2 device (See the Beast Wars episodes Feral Scream part 1 and 2 for more information), some transformers were mutated into a half organic/half robotic, asymmetrical look. The beast modes are extremely jagged and menacing. This explosion was clearly not as beneficial as the previous one. This explosion took away the three-mode gift given to the original Transmetals and gave them the same shiny parts, but with a small plastic see-though orb somewhere on their bodies, each containing a Maximal or Predacon symbol. The Beast Wars enter a new phase- the Transmetal 2 phase!
