Iguanus

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Basic
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Yellow and brown, with a bit of orange, light blue, and red
Rating : 7.2

Beast mode is an iguana. Iguanus, by the way, was remolded from Megatron (Crocodile) . This is one of several repaints that is, in my opinion, better than the original. Some of his parts are remodeled, though, including his robot and beast faces, and the frill on his beast neck. This mode is nice-looking, although there is an odd yellow stripe down the middle of his back. The detailing is pretty good, too. The articulation is pretty much limited to only his mouth and front legs, though. His front legs have the robot arms on the back of them, which looks very abnormal, and his back legs are pretty small, and they cannot move by themselves. One of the major downsides to this mode is not obvious just by looking at it, or even after you first get the toy. After a while, the knobs that hold the tail in place may wear, and, because the tail activates the spring transformation, you may eventually have Iguanus permanently stuck in robot mode. Thus, it is ideal to be gentle with this toy.
Robot mode has some extras- his iguana legs, folded behind his robot legs, and his frill, although this doesnt get in the way much. The hardest part is getting his robot gun (its his tail in beast mode) off. You have to literally pull it off of the tail, and, after a while, the end of the tail wears a bit.
A nice repaint, and a pretty good basic toy overall. Recommended.


Iguanus Bio:
FUNCTION: Demolitions
A lizard unlike any other, his internal intelligence processors are specifically programmed for combat sabotage. If Optimus Primal installs an infrared detection dish near a Predacon outpost, Iguanus will alter it to self-destruct when fired. Body scales are actually titanium-plated armor, as is the deflective headplate that shields him from laser fire.
