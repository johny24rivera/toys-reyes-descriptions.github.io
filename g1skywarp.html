Skywarp (G1 Reissue, Toys "R" Us Exclusive)

Allegiance : Decepticon
Price : $35 (U.S.)
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Difficulty of Sticker Applications : Hard
Color Scheme : Light purple, black, silver, and some transparent orange and chrome silver
Rating : 6.4

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to G1 Starscream. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of G1 Starscream here .)

Move over, Starscream! Even though ol' Screamer is easily the most popular of the G1 jet "seekers" (a fandom name for Starscream and his many repaints), he's certainly not the best. That's reserved for Skywarp here. That shiny black and light purple scheme go together absolutely beautifully, and the silver goes well with the color scheme as well. The stickers, although in the same places as they are on Starscream, are subtly different, so it's not pushed in your face that "hey, this is just a repaint, here!"
When all's said and done, it still IS the boring ol' Starscream mold, though, so it has all the same features and flaws in that area that Screamer does.
Skywarp is pretty much the best you can hope from the Starscream mold, considering that the color scheme is one of the best. Still a rather bad toy in and of itself, though.
