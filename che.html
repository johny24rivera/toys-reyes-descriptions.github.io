Che

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Beast Rider Size
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Dark yellow, black, transparent dark green, transparent yellow-orange, silver-black, tan, and some pink and white (original); Transparent yellow, silver, transparent dark blue, black, light gray, and some yellow, white, and reddish pink (repaint)
Rating : 4.9 (original); 5.4 (repaint)

Che is the Beast Rider for Cheetor, and it reflects it pretty obviously. The Che Beast Rider is designed like a giant cheetah head, with small claw "battering rams" on the sides. There is a double-barrel missle launcher coming out of the mouth, and a wheel on each side. There is little difference between the two modes, which are named "pursuit mode" and "battle mode". "Pursuit mode" is the first picture shown above, in which the claws are tucked behind Che, and the top weapons are concealed. "Battle mode" is the second picture shown, in which the claws are rotated out to the front, and the top weapons are revealed. A claw behind the top weapons is available in both modes to hold any Transformer riding Che. The gimmick is also active in this mode; if you press Che's "head" down and let it go, Che will zoom over a flat surface for a while, his claws in front rotating around. A rather neat feature, but I would have prefered that the claws did an up-and-down movement to signify a "slashing attack", rather than the claws just rotating around in place, which looks rather odd. The claws also don't stay in any position when not activated, so it's hard to get them to stay the way you want them to (face-up, so to speak, instead of out to both sides and such). Since the two modes are so similar, it rather defeats the purpose of transforming Che, and I usually just leave my Che toys in "battle mode". One major downside to the Che toy is that the toy it was meant for, Cheetor, cannot fit in Che! Che's too small for Mega Beast Machines Cheetor , much less Supreme Cheetor . Only basics and some deluxes can fit on Che and still look right. The missile launcher can fire its two missiles individually, though, which is a definite plus. The original Che (shown on the left side of each pic) is colored very much like Beast Machines Cheetor, and is mostly yellow and green with silver-black spots. The Che repaint, however (shown on the right side of each pic), is colored more like the Battle for the Spark figure, Night Slash Cheetor . It's rather neat, too, since Night Slash Cheetor can fit on Che alright. The repaint is mostly black and dark blue, with some dark yellow. I really wish they would have stuck with black missles for the repaint, however, instead of making them an oddly bright yellow.
Che looks rather neat, but he isn't a very playable toy; he's more like an accessory. Not to mention that his transformation's a joke, and the Mega Cheetor can't even fit on him! Although Che was a rather neat idea, it could have been implemented much, much better.


Che Bio :
FUNCTION: Interceptor
Relentlessly hunted by Megatron and frustrated by Optimus Primal's beliefs, Cheetor swipes the blueprints of the Beast Riders technology and creates his own individualized transport. Encased in a unique Quintessian alloy recovered from the lower levels of Cybertron, Che is impervious to most artillery attacks. Razor-sharp battering rams slice through barriers while front-mounted galvaconductors emit 10,000-volt charges to neutralize any obstacle. When in pursuit mode and at full acceleration, Che can reach hypersonic speeds while maintaining incredible maneuverability. Only Cheetor can operate Che at its peak performance through the focus and balance of his spark energy interface, but allows his fellow Maximals to pilot it in his absence.
