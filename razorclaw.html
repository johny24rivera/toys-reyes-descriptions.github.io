Razorclaw

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Basic
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme : Orange, tan, and transparent purple with some brown sprayed on it(Original), Neon orange, maroon, and aquamarine (Repaint)
Rating : 5.8 (Original), 6.1 (Repaint)

Beast mode is a sand crab. A nice-looking mode from the top; however, his robot legs stick out of the back and make this look like a crab with two little legs. Other than that major drawback, this mode is pretty good, with nice detailing and color fading.
Robot mode has a lot of extras. His shell part of his crab mode turns into two halves that stick out, and his crab legs really get in the way and look odd. His crab tail can hook onto his stomach, but then he cant move his legs very much. His claws are nice, though, with several spikes in one claw and a laser gun sticking out of the other.
A nice transformer, although the transformation isnt very creative and his beast mode has two big extras.
(There is a repaint of Razorclaw that comes with a Beast Wars video. I am told that this Razorclaw is just as good, if not better, than the original, mainly because of the better color scheme.)


Razorclaw Bio :
FUNCTION: Undercover Attack
Capable of remaining motionless beneath a layer of sand, Razorclaw is one of the best quick attack specialists they've got! When enemies approach his hidden location, his powerful pincer claw shoots up through the surface to capture and impale victims with the claw's protruding cyber-spikes! Radar defeating shell armor allows him to stay cloaked from Maximal sensors. Blaster rifle in (his) secondary in claw fires disruptor particles, temporarily paralyzing his unsuspecting prey.
