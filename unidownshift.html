(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Energon Downshift. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Energon Downshift here .)

Oh, joy. Another lazy Universe exclusive "repaint". And, considering that it's at KB Toys, it's overpriced for a deluxe as well ($13 U.S. as opposed to $10). Universe Downshift is hardly different from Energon Downshift, and the few changes that were made make the toy uglier. First, the tan and bronze on the Energon version has been replaced with yellow. Granted, the yellow isn't too noxious of a color, but the tan was still definitely preferable. The nifty slanted metallic green pattern on the vehicle mode has also been replaced with straight black lines with a ridiculously large Autobot symbol on the front hood. Talk about a bullseye for Decepticon aircraft... The black stripes themselves aren't bad perse, but again, the green stripes on the Energon version did just look better. Other than that, except for a few very minor detail changes, everything else is the same as on Energon Downshift.
No mold changes have been made to Universe Downshift.
Universe Downshift is a waste of a perfectly good exclusive, really. He's inferior to Energon Downshift in every place that he's different, and he isn't that different to begin with. Recommended only if you REALLY want a version of this mold and absolutely can NOT find the Energon version for a reasonable price (which is unlikely).


Downshift Bio :
One of the few Autobots left behind on Cybertron after the evacuation, Downshift has been assigned by Optimus Prime to guard the entrances to the shafts leading to the Energon core of the planet in the city of Iacon. A loner by nature, and a veteran of many tough, secret missions throughout the galaxy, Downshift is perfectly at home on the desolate and damaged planet.
