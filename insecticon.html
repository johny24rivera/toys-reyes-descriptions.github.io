Insecticon

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Basic
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Sparkly dark blue and green, with a bit of white and red
Rating : 6.5

Beast mode is a beetle. Although nice-looking, his robot hands are clearly visible from any angle. His robot feet are also visible from his back side as well. His wings can also open up so he can fly.  What is nice is that he can stand up on his insect legs in the mode, unlike most other insect transformers. Unfortunately, except for the wings, there are pretty much no points of articulation in this mode, so you've basically got a cool-looking brick.
Robot mode is decent. He can stand up well on his big flat feet, but his insect legs get in the way, as well as his insect jaw. Also, his crossbow that can be put together (the two halves are inside the insect wings) is small and is very easy to lose.(As I did.) :( The detailing in this mode is only so-so.
Overall, okay, but nothing really seems to stand out in this transformer.
