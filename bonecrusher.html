Bonecrusher

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Hard
Color Scheme : White, black, and silver, with some gold and red
Rating : 7.0

Beast mode is a buffalo. This mode is very well crafted and extremely nice-looking. However, he doesnt have that many moveable joints in this mode, and his robot head can stick out a little bit from above his beast head.
Robot mode has a lot of extras, and his face looks a bit odd. Buffalo flesh parts stick out all over his body and get in the way, like his flesh plates on his legs, shoulders, and waist. He has no hand weapons, but he does have a missle that can shoot out of his mouth. His face is very long and odd-looking, and it cant face forward very well, as it bends back to face straight up often, without any locking lever. You pull his tail at the end of his head to fire his mouth missle. Other than that, a nicely crafted mode, with open hands and bulky feet.
A nice transformer; has an odd shaped head and a lot of extras in robot mode, though.


Bonecrusher Bio :
FUNCTION: First Strike Infantry
As stubborn as he is strong, nothing can stop the brawny Bonecrusher when he charges into battle to crack some skulls! Like a living locomotive with a one-track mind, all he wants to do is fight, fight, fight! Optimus Primal tries to get him to lighten up, if only to prevent him from overheating an internal coolant co-processor, but Bonecrusher would rather be out pounding his enemies into cyber scrap. His favorite attack strategy is to stun opponents with a dizzying head-butt, and then finish them off with a fast firing missile shot straight from his mouth!
