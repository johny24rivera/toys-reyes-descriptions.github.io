Tarantulas (Transmetal)

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Chrome gold, blue-black, purple, and some neon green, silver, yellow, and neon pink
Rating : 9.1

Beast mode is a metallized tarantula. A very nice mode, with cool legs, and a large, well-crafted back, with the only weird part being his legs in front of his mouth, which sort of look like feelers. The only extra in this mode is the chainsaw-blade under his feelers, which get in the way of feeler movement a LOT.
Vehicle mode is his spider mode with two wheels stuck out from under it to make it look like a motorcycle. This mode doesnt stay balanced, and its front wheel comes off easily, but this mode is still great if youre careful.
Robot mode is very evil-looking. It has his spider legs as an extra on his back, but these dont get in the way much and actually complement the mode, making him look all the more evil. The wheel-shoulder pads jiggle a little, however, and can get in the way of some arm movement.He has little neon green eyes everywhere, and his feet are transmetal gold, shaped into sort of claw-feet. His chainsaw-blade makes a great weapon- perfect for dissecting transformers, hehehe....
A very nice transformer, and looks exactly like the TV show bersion, except that his eyes are two normal ones on the figure and one long bar in the TV show.
