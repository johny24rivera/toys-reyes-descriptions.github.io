Night Slash Cheetor (Universe)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme : Dull gold-yellow, transparent red, dark brown, and some dark reddish brown, silver, black, metallic gold-bronze, metallic blue-silver, and glossy dark blue
Rating : 8.2

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Beast Machines Night Slash Cheetor. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Beast Machines Night Slash Cheetor here .)

Oh, come on. There's been so many versions of Cheetor over the years, you couldn't POSSIBLY expect there not to be a Universe version of him too, right? Well, Universe Cheetor is a pretty cool repaint, and a redeco of Beast Machines Night Slash Cheetor (though why they kept the "Night Slash" prefix, when there's no other Cheetor figure in Universe, is beyond me). The Night Slash Cheetor mold finally gets a paint job more suited to that of a real cheetah-- that is, he's primarily golden yellow now. It's by no means an eyesore of a color, though, since it's a nice dull yellow, so I don't mind it being a main color like I do with most other yellows. The maroon, black, and gray colors also help accent the main color nicely. As on most Universe toys, there are a plethora of various other paint apps on Cheetor, including some nice bronze paint apps on the wrist, a spray "worn" silver paint app on the front of his swords, and even little "freckle" cheetah spots under his robot mode eyes! That's amazing detail, considering how small his face is. My favorite paint apps, however, are the cool bluish "Energon Radiation" scars on the sides of his upper arms and legs-- they look REALLY cool, and are packed with jagged edges and details. But what's even cooler than that is that they look kind of like spots-- which is, of course, appropriate for a cheetah. My only complaint about the colors-- and it's a small one-- is that that little spinning chest-doohicky in the original Night Slash Cheetor's chest, instead of being chrome or some other shiny alternating color, is just plain reddish brown like its surrounding parts. I rather liked that rotating chest thing.... Oh, and his spark crystal has an Autobot symbol just painted over it, but that's typical on Universe figs anyways.
No mold changes have been made to Universe Cheetor.
Universe Cheetor is a pretty good repaint-- especially when it comes to his Energon radiation "spots"-- but overall, I think the original Night Slash Cheetor's scheme of black and orange just looked a bit cooler, even if there wasn't quite as much paint detailing. But if you can't pick up the earlier version, and you like beast TFs, then by all means, get this one.
