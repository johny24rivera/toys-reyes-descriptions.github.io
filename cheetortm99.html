Cheetor Transmetal (Fox Kids)

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Chrome gold, black, silver, silver-grey, dark red, and some aquamarine and light red
Rating : 9.5

Beast mode is a transmetal cheetah. This mode is extremely nice- in fact, I think the best Beast Wars beast mode. Only from a straight-on side view can you see the tip of the robot head poking out from Cheetor's beast stomach, but that's it. The detailing is very good as well, with hydraulic cylinders and metal plate details everywhere. The tail, which becomes a whip in robot mode, is crafted into a curved position, making it seem more realistic. The cheetah head has a big, noticeable line running down the middle of it, though (this is because they form the robot hands), and it subtracts a little from the "sleek" look of the head. The color scheme is almost entirely different from the original Cheetor Transmetal - the silver has been replaced by a fiery red, and some the blue replaced by black. This gives this Transmetal a very fiery look to him, and, although it does not resemble a cheetah's much, it still looks very neat.
"Vehicle mode" is actually just flipping out little rocket jets on the sides of Cheetor's stomach. Although this isn't much of a vehicle mode, it still looks pretty nice, and not weird-looking like some other Transmetal and Transmetal 2 vehicle modes.
Robot mode doesn't look quite as good as the beast mode, in my opinion, but still looks very nice, and definitely contributes to the "organic" robot mode that the Transmetals are going for. The color scheme takes on a bit more of a silverish tone in this mode, with the chest, upper arms, part of the face, and the upper inner legs being all silver, or at least partially silver. There is also some aquamarine on the face, and is a nice contrast to the red. There not that many extras in this mode, either. The only major one is the front cheetah legs and upper back attached to the back of Cheetor Transmetal's robot mode like a backpack. This doesn't get in the way too much, though. The only other extras are the halves of the cheetah head stuck on the bottom of the robot mode hands and lower arms, and while these don't get in the way, they look odd, especially when viewed from above. The only major complaint I have about this mode is that, overall, this mode seems to have too much of a "beefy and bulky" look to it- not much like Cheetor, in my opinion.
A very nice transformer in both modes. Recommended over the original Cheetor Transmetal, but only if you like the color scheme leaning away from a cheetah's.


Cheetor Transmetal Bio :
FUNCTION: Robotic Jungle Patrol
Following the massive quantum surge, Cheetor emerged as a cybernetically enhanced cheetah. Meanwhile, his robot form inherited the organic traits once attributed to his beast mode. Now Cheetor prowls the jungle with more agility and power than ever before. Flip-out cyber-thrust wings provide enhanced speed and limited flight while in beast mode, making him part jet, part mechanical cheetah.
