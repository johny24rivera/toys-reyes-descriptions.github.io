Clocker (Movie) [Target Exclusive]

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Scout
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme : Dull gray, black, dark metallic blue, and some transparent black, silver, and dull metallic red
Rating : 9.4

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Cybertron Clocker. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Cybertron Clocker here .)

Clocker's more realistic deco for the movie line may be rather absurd considering his vehicle mode is so obviously futuristic, but regardless, it's a nice color combo. The metallic dark blue looks very, VERY nice and is a great color, but the other colors complement it very nicely. Normally the dull gray and black would look boring together, but something about combining them with the silver and metallic dark blue really makes them feel appropriate, like Clocker wouldn't feel "complete" without those two duller, more realistic colors. The dull red used as accents not only contrasts nicely with its surrounding colors, since it's the only "warm" color on Clocker while all the other colors are "cool".
No mold changes have been made to Clocker-- a shame, since his Speed Planet Cyber Key really doesn't make any sense in the movie-verse. Why couldn't he have gotten a cool Sector 7 Cyber Key like Hardtop , darnit!?
Movie Clocker is one of the best paint jobs this mold has had, right up there with Botcon pre-BW Tigatron in terms of awesomeness. Plus, he's a lot cheaper to boot. Unless you already have Tigatron, get this version of Clocker if you only get it once.


Clocker Bio :
Before the war for the AllSpark began, Clocker was a professional racer on Cybertron. His speed and skill dazzled millions of fans, and for a while, he seemed unbeatable. He was on top of the world. Then war came, and there were suddenly no more races to run. He turned his skills to finding the AllSpark, stopping the Decepticons, and bringing peace to Cybertron. He dreams of the day the war ends, and he can race again alongside his Autobot friends with no limits on how far or fast he can go.
