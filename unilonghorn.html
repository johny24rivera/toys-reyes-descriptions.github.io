Longhorn (Universe)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Hard
Color Scheme : Pale flat blue, bluish black, gold-silver meld plastic, and some pale yellow, metallic orangish bronze, glossy muddy maroon, sky blue, pale yellowish gold, light green, and metallic purplish bronze
Rating : 8.9

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Beast Wars Ramulus. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Beast Wars Ramulus here .)

Longhorn's name reminds me more of a cow than a ram, but regardless, this is a pretty cool repaint. It's certainly an original paint job-- I never would've thought bronze, bluish black, blue, and gold-silver would have gone so well together, but they do. I think the blue should've been a TAD darker, but it's a really minor quibble. I also really like the goldish-silver plastic-- I've never seen it used on a transformer before, and it has swirled of both gold AND silver plastic in it, with both of the colors mixing together most of the time, but some parts are more silver and others are more gold, and the like. What I don't like about this plastic, though, is that's a little bendy, and feels a bit cheap when compared to the other kinds of plastic used on Longhorn. But what's really amazing about this toy, though, is that it has a TON of paint apps. They're everywhere on this guy! Nearly all of his fur detailing is colored bronze, he has maroon decoes on his chest and his wrist-blades, and other odds and ends all over the place. He's a beauty to look at, he really is. The "Energon radiation" pattern, by the way, is, I'm guessing the purplish spray paint app on his horns.
No mold changes have been made to Universe Longhorn.
Longhorn is a fantastic repaint of an already pretty-good mold-- he's got paint details out the wazoo! Just be forewarned of the slightly bendy plastic that comprises two-thirds of his body, though. If you don't have the original, by all means pick Longhorn up. Heck, even if you DO have the original, Longhorn's still worth serious consideration.
