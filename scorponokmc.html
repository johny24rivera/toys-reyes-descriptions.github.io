Scorponok Transmetal (McDonalds)

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Happy Meal Size
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Light purple, gold, and red, with some yellow
Rating : 2.3

Beast mode is a robotic scorpion. There arent many moveable parts in this mode; only his claws. This is kind of crummy looking and not a very cool beast mode; his robot legs are showing very clearly from any view except one from directly above. For a Happy Meal toy, the detailing is pretty nice, though.
Robot mode is defintely the crummier of the two. His hands are just engravements on the plastic on the underside of his scorpion claws. His face is engraved on the bottom of his beast face and doesnt look like Scorponok at all. His hips have a bolt running through them and look very odd. His only moveable parts are his shoulders and his upper legs. His stinger sticks out of his bottom and looks kind of odd; it doesnt look like it can be used as a weapon like the original Beast Wars Scorponoks tail.
A kind of weak transformer in both modes; not very recommended. If you have the money, buy one of the toys that are five bucks or above.
