Optimus Prime (Rally Rumble) [Activator; Autobot Alliance]

Allegiance : Autobot
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Moderately dark glittery red, moderately dark glittery blue, light milky brownish gray, and some silver, black, pale light metallic red, light sky blue, and yellow
Rating : 7.6

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the original Autobot Alliance Activator Optimus Prime. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original Autobot Alliance Activator Optimus Prime here .)

Like most redecos of Optimus Prime these days, Rally Rumble Optimus Prime is more a repositioning of his primary colors (no pun intended) and changing their shades a bit as opposed to a totally new deco job. The main plastic color changes are that both the blue and red have been made a bit paler (and more glittery-looking) compared to the original Autobot Alliance Activator version of the character. Personally, I think that this aesthetic choice doesn't really look any better or worse than the initial version, it's just... different. I do like the layout of the colors slightly more on this version, though, with the red being a bit more predominant on the cab while the blue is relegated to the back half, thus visually separating the two "parts" of the vehicle mode better. The light milky brownish gray-- unfortunately-- hasn't changed at all from the previous release of this mold, and is still a pretty dull, boring (and slightly dirty-looking) color. The flames on the initial version have been changed to stylized yellow-and-blue lightning bolts on this version, which certainly fit the whole "racing" theme of this version a bit more, along with the "01" numbers on the sides of the cab. The silver paint app above the light blue cab windows also looks great and contrasts well with both of the surrounding colors. In robot mode-- with the exception of having lightning bolts on his chest as opposed to flames-- Rally Rumble Optimus Prime looks almost entirely the same as the original Activator version given the predominance of the light brownish gray, so my comments on that mode haven't really changed for this version.
No mold changes have been made to Rally Rumble Optimus Prime.
Rally Rumble Optimus Prime is a decent enough re-release of the mold for those who want something a "little different" than the standard Optimus flames, but the lightning bolts, racing numbers, and the increase in the amount of red on the figure are the only real differences between this version and the previous. Of the two color schemes, I think I like this one SLIGHTLY more, but only just. It's so similar there's no reason for someone who already owns the original to get this one unless they're Prime completists.
