Fox Kids' Classic Beast Wars Repaints

In celebration of the re-releasing of Beast Wars Transformers on Fox Kids, Hasbro re-released some classic Beast Wars action figures. To keep the collector's value of the "old" Beast Wars that they were repainting the same, they switched the color scheme for the new ones, as you can see in the pictures above. The color schemes are generally more wild and "fruity" than the originals. The quality of the toys is also a little better than the originals as well.
