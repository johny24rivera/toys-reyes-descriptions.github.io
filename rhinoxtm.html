Rhinox (Transmetal)

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Hard
Color Scheme : Chrome aquamarine, gray, dark brown, and some green-blue, orange, and light green
Rating : 9.2

Beast mode is a metallic rhinoceros. He has some robot hands and his chest under his upper belly, but this is still a very nice beast mode, with a well-proportioned head, body, and legs. His horn is very large and menacing, and the detailing is exquisite, with several wires and tubes running all over his gray metallic skin.
Vehicle mode is a rhino with tank treads for back legs and skis for front legs. Gives some speed to Rhinoxs bulky body and looks like a cross between a snowmobile, a rhino, and a tank. His actual tank treads cant roll, though. A decent vehicle mode overall.
Robot mode is large and muscular. His face looks like that of a sumo wrestler's, though. His only extras are a small backpack of parts on his upper back and his rhino head halves on his shoulders, as well as some small stomach panels hanging off his sides. His horn becomes a sword. He can stand up very well in this mode, and he has a nicely detailed main body.
A very nicely done transformer; highly recommended. Transformation may hurt your fingers, though.

FUNCTION: Strategic Defense
Following the quantum surge, Rhinox emerged in the form of a massive mechanized
rhinocerous. He soon discovered that his new beast structure gave him an added
conversion: a heavy-treaded all-terrain vehicle. To a big, powerful and slow Maximal like Rhinox, the enhanced mobility is like a dream come true. He perfected new battle moves like pulling wheelies and devastating Predacons at high speed. Also, a new animalized robot mode improves his fluidity and skill on the battlefield.
