Rattrap (Transmetal)

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Hard
Color Scheme : Gray, brown, chrome red, creamy gray, with touches of blue, neon green, and neon orange
Rating : 7.6

Beast mode is one cool-looking rat. With little bands of metal running around his head and a big fat rump with one extremely articulated tail, this mode is cool, although theres a lot of extra junk under him. However, this can be ignored, because it doesn't get in the way much, if at all.
His vehicle mode is a rat rover. His back legs tuck in to become rolling wheels, to turbo engines come out of his butt, and his front legs fold back to become little wheels. Pretty awesome, in my opinion, the best Transmetal vehicle mode.
His robot mode it the only reason this guy didnt get above an 8.0. Theres a lot of extra junk on his back, and he looks just a little too mean and muscular for a wimp like Rattrap. All these extras get in the way and make Rattrap a tad top-heavy. However, his rat tail whip is kinda cool and he has actual fists.
I suggest you keep him in beast or vehicle mode. Hes cooler that way- but that's just my opinion.
