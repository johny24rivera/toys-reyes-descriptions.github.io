Acid Storm

Allegiance : Decepticon
Size : Deluxe
Series : Classic/Generation One
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Jungle green, black, and some dull pastel blue, transparent amber, dull yellow, dark muddy brown, bright red, silver, and dull light muddy greenish brown
Rating : 9.3

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Classics Starscream. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Classics Starscream here .)

All of the "classic six" Seekers have now been repainted from this mold now, so who else is there? Why, random-colored Seekers that appeared briefly in the background in certain episodes of the G1 cartoon, that's who! Acid Storm here is the "green Seeker" seen in the background in a few episodes of G1, though just making Starscream green would have made for a fairly boring toy-- in addition to being green, Acid Storm's got a fairly cool jungle camo deco. The two different shades of muddy brown look quite nice against the green, and really help differentiate Acid Storm from the other Seekers-- something that's particularly important given how many uses this mold has had by now. Black and the dull pastel blue make for excellent contrast colors, one being substantially darker than the main green color while the other's much lighter. I particularly like how much the blue looks like the color of rain, which fits given Acid Storm's bio lists him as a "rainmaker". There are a few, very minor, things I'm not all that fond of when it comes to the paint job, however. For one, his robot arms are completely bereft of paint apps, which makes them look rather dull against those rather dynamic-looking wings. Secondly, putting FOUR Decepticon symbols on Acid Storm's vehicle mode-- two on the main wings and two on the vertical tail wings-- is a bit overkill, really-- two is definitely enough. Still, as said before, those are really minor quibbles.
No mold changes have been made to Acid Storm.
Acid Storm is an inventive thematic repaint that adds a new "Seeker" to the typical six that are repainted/remolded from the Starscream mold. Although he gets major props for originality, I don't think he tops a few of the other uses of the Seeker mold, though he outdoes or ties some of the others-- overall, I'd put him in about the middle as far as ranking how good each of the Starscream repaints looks in comparison to the others.


Acid Storm Bio :
A member of an obscure squad of Decepticon air warriors known only as "The Rainmakers," Acid Storm is known as an individual only to his teammates and a very few others. He is highly intelligent, eager, and dedicated utterly to the Decepticon cause. He has a talent for marshalling vast amounts of data, as well as a way with words that would make him a powerful speaker. However, he prefers to remain anonymous, as part of his team. His hyperion3 blasters can seed natural clouds with a highly destructive form of acid rain.
