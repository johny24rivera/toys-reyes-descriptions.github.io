Wreck-Gar (Combiner Wars)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Legend
Difficulty of Transformation to Robot: Easy
Difficulty of Transformation to Armor : Medium
Color Scheme : Black, light orange, light orangish yellow, and some silver, moderately dark red, and blue
Rating : 7.7

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to legends Combiner Wars Groove. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of legends Combiner Wars Groove here .)

Wreck-Gar is a motorcycle. Groove is a motorcycle. Ergo, Groove has been remolded into a Combiner Wars version of Wreck-Gar-- at least that's the logic. And color-wise Hasbro did an excellent job. The light orange and light orangish yellow is a great basic color scheme, with the light orange being a GREAT shade of the color, and both of them complementing each other quite well. There's also a fair amount of black-- on the wheels, obviously, but also on the motorcycle seat and as the base plastic for the windshield and a few other minor parts. Red is another fairly prevalent color, used as paint on the center areas of the wheel hubs as well as on the robot shoulders, and on some rather neat yellow/red decals on the robot knees that replicate some similar details on the original G1 figure. For accent colors, there's some silver on the headlights and face, and there's some really nice blue paint on the windshield. The blue is an excellent contrast color against what is otherwise a very "warm" color scheme, and I honestly wish there was just a bit more of it, as a bit TOO much of Wreck-Gar is in the orange area of the color wheel, in my opinion. Still, it all looks good. The problem is, what with the very lean physique, the more "squat" motorcycle-- the mold just does not say "Wreck-Gar" at all. It's too much of a "color-slap" done just because Wreck-Gar is a fairly well-known TF motorcycle rather than because the design fits him.
Thankfully, Wreck-Gar at least has a new headsculpt, which is fairly well-done for the size, with a bit of faux little "motorcycle front" details on his forehead, along with a black painted beard and little beady red dots for eyes on a silver face. His packaging says he combines with Sky Reign , but he does no such thing-- there's been a few halfway "fan" modes, but Sky Lynx's torso mode has no space for Wreck-Gar's chest armor mode.
Combiner Wars Wreck-Gar has a great color scheme, and by itself it's my favorite color scheme this mold has been in. That said, it fits the character very poorly outside of general alt-mode and the new headsculpt, and for Wreck-Gar fans I'd recommend the Autobot Alliance or upcoming Power of the Primes versions instead, depending upon whether you want him to combine or not. I'd only recommend this version for completists.
