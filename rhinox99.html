Rhinox (Fox Kids)

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Dark red, light red, dark brown, pale greenish blue, and some yellow, gold, and blue
Rating : 6.6

Beast mode is a rhino. The color scheme in this mode, is, in my opinion, much better than the original . The pale greenish blue is already a nice touch, but when you add a bunch of black stripes on his back similar to the original Dinobot's brown ones, WOW! What a color scheme! One of the downsides to this mode, however, is that there is very few points of articulation; mainly just the mouth and the back legs.
Transformation to robot mode is not so actually hard as it is to try to position the "flesh plates" so that they don't get in the way. That is the major downside to the robot mode; the large flesh plates on the waist and arms hamper movement a lot, and get in the way. Other than that, this mode is nice, and is one of the few deluxe Transformers with two weapons; a rotating "chain gun o' death" and a sword. The "mutant head" splits in the middle and folds out to reveal the robot head, but the head plates don't swing all the way out, unfortunately, so it's better just to leave the mutant head like it is. The color scheme changes greatly in this mode, revealing the robot inside to look somewhat like a fire-type transformer, with the fiery reds and oranges dominating his robot parts.
A better quality toy and a better color scheme than the original Rhinox. Recommended over the original.
