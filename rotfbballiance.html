Bumblebee (Alliance)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : "Cheese" yellow, black, transparent dark blue, and some silver, light sky blue, dark metallic blue, bright orange, dark gray, and light red
Rating : 9.6

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the original deluxe TF1 '08 Bumblebee toy. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original TF1 deluxe '08 Bumblebee toy here .)

Alliance Bumblebee is basically Bumblebee, now with more black stripes! (Really just a way to keep him on the shelves while also keeping him "new".) Nothing's really new about his color scheme-- it's still basically the same it's always been. Compared to the original RotF deluxe version, most of the dark gray in robot mode has been replaced with a more movie-accurate black, and the yellow is a bit more golden, both of which make him look a little better. In vehicle mode, his two stripes now go down the top of his roof too, and there are additional stripes (with the N.E.S.T. Global Alliance logo) along his car doors. I quite like the stripes on the top of the mode-- more contrast, and all that-- but the stripes on the car doors look odd since they're ONLY on the car doors-- they really should've stretched the side, from one wheel to the other. He doesn't have any license plate paint apps (either in his car mode or on his crotch in robot mode), and his rear tailpipes aren't painted, but other than that the basic details are painted.
Alliance Bumblebee has the same head and arm mold changes that the original RotF deluxe version has-- he has no mold changes unique to this release, though.
Alliance Bumblebee is a nice, somewhat movie-accurate version of the guy if you somehow managed to miss the other versions of the mold, and has a few nice extras touches to boot. It certainly helps that it IS a good mold, even if Bumblebee is really starting to saturate the market. My personal favorite release of the character in the RotF line, barely edging out the Human Alliance version due to value.


Alliance Bumblebee Bio :
When Bumblebee discovered Dirge lurking as a sleeper agent among the N.E.S.T. Global Alliance air command, he partnered with the first Autobot he could find. Now, he and Armorhide are tracking the Decepticon flyer from airstrip to airstrip, hoping to catch him unaware long enough to ground him permanently.
