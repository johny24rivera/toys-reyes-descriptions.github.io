Optimus Prime & Ultra Magnus (Cybertron; CostCo Exclusive)

Price : $30
Overall Rating : 8.5

Yeah, okay, this isn't much of a review. But that's because this "exclusive" is just two previous Transformers toys repackaged. The Ultra Magnus is the exact same as his Sam's Club Universe version, to check out that review here . The Optimus Prime is the same as his Robots in Disguise version, but with a black sash, woo, THAT'S certainly worth buying another one. See the review of his RiD toy here . Nicely-priced package if you don't have either of these toys, but if you do, then this is definitely a big pass. No meaningful changes at all to the set.
