Nightglider

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Basic
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme : Chrome bronzish purple, grey, tangerine, dark blue, and some red
Rating : 6.9

Beast mode is a flying squirrel. This mode is very nicely detailed when viewed from above, with such things as holes in the wings, and a nice mix of fur and metal. However, the main problems with this mode is that there are almost no moveable parts. It would be nice if the wings could flap, or something to that extent. Another major problem with this mode is the undercarriage robot parts on his underside, which makes the main body of the toy somewhat bulky, and if viewed from any angle but above, odd-looking.
Robot mode is very much like the Optimus Primal (Bat) design, with the main body in front, and large wings on the back, sticking out both sides. These wings make it hard for the toy to stand up, however, although they do add to the fierceness of this toy. He has a small sword for a weapon, with a medieval-style design. His spark crystal is right in the center of his chest, you can't miss it.
Overall, a nice toy in robot mode, although beast mode has a lot of extras.


Nightglider Bio :
FUNCTION: Intelligence Operative
MOTTO: "Do not go gently into that good night."
Nightglider is the most skillful Maximal spy. A creature of the night, he operates best alone. Can virtually escape detection - emits an electromagnet cloaking field, is soundless in flight, disappears in subdued light or shadow. Electro-disruptor can cast illusions, altering his physical placement and appearance for up to 10 minutes. Devoted to the Maximal cause; despises Predacons. Wields serrated Energon sword.
