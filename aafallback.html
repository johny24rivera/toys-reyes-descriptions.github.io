Fallback (Autobot Alliance)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Light tan, dull brownish red, black, and some dark charcoal grya, dull silver, dull orange, and clear plastic
Rating : 8.5

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Revenge of the Fallen Brawn. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Revenge of the Fallen Brawn here .)

Fallback is an homage to the G1 toy Outback, which is appropriate, given that Outback was also a redeco of the original G1 Brawn toy. Anyways, Fallback here roughly homages Outback's color scheme, but isn't too slavish to it. A very light tan-- almost white-- makes up most of his vehicle mode colors. That by itself would be rather boring, but there's several black and silver paint apps on that mode, which really help to add some contrast. That said, however, his alt mode still is a a bit duller than I would've liked it to be. In robot mode, however, his colors really pop, with the dull brownish red contrasting excellently against both the black and the light tan. It gives Fallback a "muddy" look, which works well with his original "Outback" name. The silver and dull orange paint apps-- particularly on his chest-- really help to add that finishing touch and really make him rather pretty to stare at. There's also dark charcoal plastic parts here and there, but they don't really contribute much to the color scheme, and might've well just have been black. Because Fallback is from the "Reveal the Shield" subline, he has a rub allegiance symbol on his front vehicle section-- rub it to reveal he's an Autobot.
No mold changes have been made to Autobot Alliance Fallback.
Autobot Alliance Fallback is a pretty cool repaint of the Brawn mold, and a bit more visually interesting overall-- the brownish red is especially nice-looking. I wish his vehicle mode had a bit more color variety, but overall I'd recommend him over RotF Brawn.
