Fractyl (BotCon 1997 Exclusive)

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Basic
Color Scheme : Gold, silvery white, and dark green, with some red
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Rating : 4.9

Beast mode is a green pterodactyl. Looks very nice from the top, and has a very nice color combination to it. However, the silver lever does stick out a bit on his back and there are his robot leg extras on the underside, which kind of get in the way a bit. His robot hands are also clearly visible on the back of his beast legs. His mouth can open and close, though, which is a nice feature, and the beast legs can move too. That's it for articulation- the wings can't even flap. The detailing isn't too good on this transformer.
Robot mode has a nice coloration to it, but also has a big extra that always seems to get in the way; the wings. There is no place to tuck and fold the wings, and it doesn't seem to fit in with the robot. His robot parts are very nice-looking, though, and have a nice shine to them. His gun is very small, and it can fall out of its storage space very easily, making it easy to lose.
A very nice repaint of Terrorsaur , one of the ones that is better. Recommended over Terrorsaur if you're willing to pay the high price for him.


Fractyl Bio :
FUNCTION: Geochemist
MOTTO: "The power of this planet is our salvation and our curse."
While Megatron pushes him to be a warrior, Fractyl is more concerned with solving the problems caused by the strange planet's surplus of Energon. The other Predacons either ignore him or ridicule his apparent fascination with the energy source, despite the fact that his work could provide them with the means for ultimate victory over the Maximals. While his research has only temporary success, he is confident that he will eventually resolve their recurring overload problems, thereby earning the others' respect.
