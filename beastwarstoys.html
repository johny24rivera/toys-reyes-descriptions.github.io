Beast Wars Transformers

The toys that single-handedly saved the Transformers brand from extinction after "G1 exhaustion" with a wild new take on Transformers. These Transformers, for the most part, have two modes; robot and beast. Although their beast modes have some lines in them and some extras, as they progressed, the transfomation got more creative and complicated. Most of the basics are transformed by a single push of the lever.
