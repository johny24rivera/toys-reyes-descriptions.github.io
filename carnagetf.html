Carnage (Marvel TF Crossover)

Difficulty of Transformation: Hard
Color Scheme : Light red and some silver, metallic gunmetal gray, black, yellow, white, and dark gray
Rating : 7.2

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the Marvel TF Venom. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the Marvel TF Venom here .)

Given Venom's visual resemblance (somewhat) to Carnage, it was fairly inevitable that the black symbiote would get repainted into the red one. And red is a bit more suiting of a color for a sportscar like this one, with the black flame outlines in vehicle mode looking quite fitting. (I wish they were more than just outlines, though, and had some actual filler colors like orange or something.) The black and silver/metallic gunmetal paint apps also go quite well with the red, though the dark gray plastic in mech mode seems a little out-of-place. Replacing it with black would have helped Carnage's contrast colors quite a bit more, methinks. My main complaint about the color scheme has to do with the shade of red, though-- it's a two shades TOO bright. A slightly darker shade would really have made the color scheme seem less obviously "toyish", not to mention I don't think "traditional" Carnage is quite this light of a shade of red anyways. I also think it's a shame that the head has been unchanged-- granted, Carnage shares many facial features with Venom, but he's a considerably skinnier, guy than Venom (and I'm not exactly a Spider-Man aficionado, here). Having that thick head mold doesn't work quite as well, though I suppose I can still understand why they did it this way.
Contrary to what most pictures of Carnage show, he actually DOES have a mold change, albeit a subtle one-- the molded-in "VENOM" license plate has been changed to now read "CARNAGE". It's not much, but worth noting. (It's also worth nothing that it has no paint apps, unlike Venom's.)
Marvel TF Carnage is an easy pass for pretty much everyone except completists and really big fans of the character that'll buy any plastic version of him. It's a fairly poor mold in mech mode to start with, but although red fits a sportscar a bit more, it's too light of a shade, the head mold doesn't fit Carnage all that well, and although he has some nice contrast colors, he doesn't look nearly as visually interesting as Marvel TF Venom.


Carnage Bio :
The evil of Carnage is matched only by his thirst for power. By melding his alien symbiote with the war-suit given to him by Doctor Doom, he can greatly enhance his already impressive strength and achieve ultimate devastation. He can even disguise himself as a muscle car, making him a formidable foe for the likes of Thor and Iron Man.
