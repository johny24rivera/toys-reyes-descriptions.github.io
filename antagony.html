Antagony (BotCon 1998 Exclusive)

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Mega
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Black, lavender, and some dark brown and silver
Rating : 7.2

Beast mode is a black ant. Although this repaint's color scheme does, in my opinion, look slightly better than Inferno's , it still doesn't stand up any better in beast mode. It still sags on its legs a lot, and its back and front legs are bulky somewhat. The middle ant legs can snap off somewhat easily, too, and the detailing is only so-so in this mode. Articulation is great, though, as the mouth, antennae, and front and back legs can all move.
Robot mode is extremely wicked-looking, even for a Predacon. Her body is almost entirely black and lavender, with some silver showing on the head and guns. Her face has the same demonic grin that Inferno's does, which adds to the wickedness of this toy even more. Her two guns are nicely crafted as well, but the only drawback is one doesn't really fire anything. One of her guns is her ant pincers and antennae stuck together, and the other is a missle launcher; the ammo is on her legs. Her ant bulb opens up to reveal her turbine engines, giving her the gift of flight as well as fright. Again, like Inferno, her waist hinges don't fit together well, and her middle legs and bulb get in the way some.
A very nice repaint; I didn't think they could make anything more demonic-looking than Inferno, but they did.


Antagony Bio :
FUNCTION: Chemical Warfare
MOTTO: "Prepare for armageddon."
Antagony is somewhat of an enigma. Soon after her mysterious arrival on the Beast Wars planet, she displayed the uncanny ability to accurately predict significant events. She explains away her clairvoyance as more intuition, but the other Predacons feel it is something more than that - almost as if she's from a time yet to be. Her prowess in battle is further enhanced by the ability to project limited-duration hard light holograms that serve to confuse her opponents. Antagony's primary weapon fires bolts of corrosive acid which instantly begins to dissolve Maximal armor upon impact.
