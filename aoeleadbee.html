Bumblebee (Leader; AoE)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Leader
Difficulty of Transformation : Hard
Color Scheme : Dull yellow, light gray, transparent light blue, black, and some silver and metallic bronze
Rating : 6.3

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the original DotM leader Bumblebee toy. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original Dotm leader Bumblebee toy here .)

As is pretty obvious if you're familiar with Bumblebee's new design for Age of Extinction, this is NOT, in fact, his AoE body, but rather his Dark of the Moon leader class toy, recolored slightly and released. The big downside? It doesn't come with the huge Mechtech backpack that was its main selling point in the first place , and what was largely the reason to forgive this toy's other flaws. That aside, focusing on the color scheme itself, 'Bee hasn't changed much-- the most obvious change is that his stripes in car mode are now AoE-accurate, with a large single stripe down the center with a "border" around the stripe, instead of two separate stripes like in DotM. He's also got some nice black trim on the bottom of the sides. The yellow plastic on him is also a bit more... yellow, with less orange in the mix-- it honestly looks a bit more plain and duller as a result. His transparent plastic and black parts and most of the black paint is unchanged. What is apparent in his robot mode is that there's more gray-- which, honestly, the movie toyline did NOT need. He's got considerably more gray paint on the faux chest piece, and much of his legs and arms are in a lighter gray than the DotM release of this mold. On the flipside, some other bits on his legs and arms are now in black plastic, which helps add a bit more visual contrast there, even if it's not a lot. There's a yellow paint app on Bumblebee's crotch, which looks odd against all the light gray around it-- more should have been added, there. Other than that, some of his paint apps from robot mode have actually been removed, like on his upper legs, toes, and license plate. At least his facesculpt is still painted nicely, though.
Other than the removal of the Mechtech backpack, no mold changes have been made to this version of Bumblebee.
AoE Leader class Bumblebee is a VERY hard sell, even for big Bumblebee fans and completists. Why? A. He's not AoE-accurate; B. He's more expensive than the DotM version, but comes WITHOUT the big electronic backpack that was the big selling point for the DotM version; and C. His plastic colors overall are less eye-catching and more plain, with fewer paint apps. Overall it just seems like something very little effort was put in and almost feels like it should have been some obscure discount store exclusive and not a mainline release. I'd skip this and get the DotM Leader class version instead, any day of the week.


Bumblebee (Leader) Bio :
There is a time for stealth and a time to attack. With the Autobots abandoned, exiled, and hunted by new enemies, it's good to have a warrior like Bumblebee around. The Autobot scout knows when to keep quiet, and when to go on the offensive.
