Snapper

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Basic
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Red, dark green, and some yellow and white
Rating : 7.7

Beast mode is a snapping turtle. This mode looks nice on the top, but his legs look sort of odd, and they are too long proportionally. His legs and mouth can all move, which is decent articulation for a basic. This mode is also nicely detailed too.
Robot mode is a very nice one. Part of his shell flips out to reveal a very neat triple-barrel cannon. My only two complaints are that is feet look like theyre pointing the wrong way and his shell in the back sort of gets in the way. Other than that, this is one fine robot mode, that is very balanced and doesnt have a lot of extras.
Highly recommended among the basic Beast Wars transformers.
