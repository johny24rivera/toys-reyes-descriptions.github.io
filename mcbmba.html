Blackarachnia [Beast Machines] (McDonald's)

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Happy Meal Size
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Reddish pink, transparent light red, dark yellow, and some black
Rating : 2.7

Beast mode is a technorganic spider. This mode might have looked nice, if the legs weren't positioned every which way. There's no possible way to get all the spider legs to touch the floor or even look like they're all touching the floor. Pretty bad planning, there. Also, from a back view, Blackarachnia's robot head and waist clearly show- the spider abdomen doesn't cover it up at all. On the other hand, the detailing is quite good on this toy, especially for a Happy Meal toy. The spider face looks pretty cool as well.
In robot mode, Blackarachnia is actually quite show-accurate. Her bulb on her rear is a bit too large proportionally, however, as are her back spider legs. Her main body trunk is pretty nice, though, and well-detailed, both mold- and paint-wise. My main problem with this mode is that she looks a little... chunky... in this mode, and Blackarachnia is anything BUT chunky on the show.
A so-so toy in robot mode, but a really bad beast mode. Only recommended for completists.
