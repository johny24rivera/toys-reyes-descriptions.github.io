Side Burn (Deluxe, Redeco)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Hard
Japanese Name : (Super) Speedbreaker
Color Scheme : Red, dark dull red, off-white, sparkly orange, transparent tinted plastic, chrome gold, and some chrome silver, light black, white, silver, metallic blue, bright yellow, sparkly yellow, and chrome yellow
Mold History : Side Burn was a completely new mold created for Car Robots (which subsequently became Robots in Disguise).
Rating : 7.9

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the original deluxe Side Burn. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original deluxe Side Burn here .)

"Super" Side Burn's color scheme follows a very simple formula: take the original deluxe Side Burn's color scheme, replace the blues with reds, and the silvers with golds. Add a few touches here and there, and you're pretty much done. So, "Super" Side Burn is pretty much a more vibrant, fiery version of his former self. I think this actually suits Side Burn's rebellious character on the show. The gold tires and engine look pretty darn cool, too. What's really cool about this Transformer, though, is that it's the first (at least to my knowledge) Transformer with an "official licensed" vehicle mode! Right on the package, it identifies Side Burn as a "Dodge Viper GTS", with the little logo and everything! Side Burn also has a small Dodge Viper GTS logo on his front bumper, which is really cool too.
No mold changes have been made to this toy from its previous release, so it retains all the features and flaws of its predecessor in that respect.
I like this version of deluxe Side Burn a little better than the previous incarnation of him, due to the more fitting color scheme, as well as the Dodge symbol on the front, which is just cool!
