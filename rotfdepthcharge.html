Depthcharge (RotF)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Scout
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme : Pale light blue, black, chalky light whitish blue, and some dull greenish tan, dull metallic gold, metallic gunmetal gray, and sky blue
Rating: 9.4

Depthcharge's vehicle mode is a stealth boat, and is pretty darned solid all around. Although the rear is hollow on the bottom, other than that there's no robot extras whatsoever in this mode, and the proportions are par excellence . What's particularly impressive is the sheer amount of mold detailing on this figure-- he's got ALL the appropriate details for a stealth boat, which is great considering his small size. Panels, guns, a communications tower... it's all there. And although I think the blue-and-whitish-blue camo scheme in this mode is pretty good, I wish a few more paint apps had been spared to highlight some of these excellent details.
Depthcharge's robot mode is also quite impressive. His proportions are great for the most part, fitting the movie aesthetic quite well with some fantastic little mold details on his limbs and chest. (His head also has some great detailing, but it looks VERY evil-- not very fitting for an Autobot.) The sole exception to this are his lower legs, which are a bit absurdly large, with the front of the boat mode poking up pretty far from where his knees are. The added black and a bit of tan/gold help to break up the blue a bit and give him a pretty good color scheme. His articulation is also superb-- he can move at the neck, shoulders (at four points, including a point at the base of his missile launchers), elbows (at two points), thumbs (yes, thumbs-- ON A SCOUT TOY), waist, hips (at two points), and knees. There's nothing to really get in the way except the boat points above his knees, so you get him in some awesome poses. (In addition, Depthcharge has a third "attack" mode where you combine his legs together so he can "sail" on them, but it's really goofy-looking and really fan mode-y.)
Depthcharge may have some funky lower legs, but other than that he's an incredible scout design and one of my favorites from the Revenge of the Fallen toyline. I like Sonar's color scheme slightly better, but you owe it to yourself to pick up at least ONE version of this mold, as it's fantastic, posable, great-looking, and fun and simple to transform.


Depthcharge Bio:
Depthcharge is a shrewd military strategist. Autobots and Decepticons alike are accustomed to ground and air-based combat, but ocean tactics are unfamiliar to them. Depthcharge uses this to his advantage, choosing a stealth boat mode to spy on Decepticon forces from the nearest port. Fast and smart, Depthcharge never shies away from battle-- but he prefers to be armed with the element of surprise.
