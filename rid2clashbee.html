Bumblebee (Clash; Warrior; RID 2015) [Toys "R Us Exclusive]

Allegiance : Autobot
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Milky yellow, black, transparent blue, light milky gray, and some light sky blue and silver
Rating : 8.1

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the original RID2015 warrior Bumblebee toy. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original RID2015 warrior Bumblebee here .)

Bumblebee's "Clash" toy is meant to be representative of his appearance after a brief upgrade at the end of Season 1, where he gets energized silver/light blue armor. The base plastic colors are more or less the same as the original warrior class Bumblebee toy, with the black and the light milky gray plastic in mostly the exact same spots. (The sole exception to this is that the gray plastic near the upper robot back has been changed to black this time around.) The yellow plastic is also in the same spots as well, though the plastic itself is a bit milkier and less flat in terms of the shade. Honestly a flatter yellow works better given the RID2015 "semi-cartoony" feel, though this is a really minor change so I can't say I mind it that much. The differences here are with the paint apps. There's a lot of light sky blue used on Clash 'Bee-- on the chest, eyes, and central headcrest, as well as right below the knees on the lower legs in robot mode. In vehicle mode, there's some on the headlights and two stripes on the hood that oddly combine into one large light blue section right before the front windshield. They should've kept it as normal stripes, if you ask me. The front and side windows have been painted black now, though the rear windows still are transparent blue, along with two stripes on the top of the hood, which is kind of a weird choice given the other color selections in vehicle mode. Silver has been added to the lower chest, lower arms, and on most of the lower legs, with some black on the knees and feet. It makes the robot mode as a whole look a little better than the original, especially on the legs and chest. That said, compared to the show, there definitely should be some silver to make Bumblebee look more "armored up", and his head should be gray/silver as well.
No mold changes have been made to this toy, which is a real shame as it makes it look really inaccurate in robot mode. In the show he gets a full-blown new head design, extra weapons, and some minor mold detail changes akin to Optimus Prime's look at the end of Season 1. He also got some nifty Decepticon Hunter weapons-- but no dice here, he still has his same ol' sword.
"Clash" warrior Bumblebee might be a buy if you like the added blue and silver on the toy or just love this mold so much you can't get enough of it, but the lack of mold detailing to make him actually look in robot mode like he does in the Season 1 finale sort of defeats the purpose of this redeco-- some more silver/gray really should've been added as well. As a "variant" color scheme it's alright, but it doesn't represent the design it's supposed to well at all.

Review by Beastbot
