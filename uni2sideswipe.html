Sideswipe (Universe 2.0)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Deluxe
Series : Classic/Generation One
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Black, white, red, and some silver, clear plastic, yellow, dark translucent black, and transparent light blue
Rating : 9.6

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Universe 2.0 Sunstreaker. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Universe 2.0 Sunstreaker here .)

Sideswipe's colors are very accurate to his G1 toy . Though red, black, and white isn't a very original color scheme at this point, it still looks nice, and just the right amount of each color is used (particularly in robot mode). The shade of red used-- which has an ever-so-slight swirly, glossy quality to it-- also look fantastic even by itself, which makes the almost entirely red vehicle mode much more attractive than Sunstreaker's yellow. The silver and yellow accents also really contrast well with the other colors, and using a very dark translucent black for the windows in vehicle mode was a nice idea-- they look like they're clear, but still heavily tinted, thus hiding any robot bits that you may have been able to see through them. Sideswipe's red paint also meshes very well with his red plastic, which is a big plus, particularly in car mode. I also like the black stripes along his sides and the clear-silver headlights, they both really look nice and add something extra to the vehicle mode.
Sideswipe has one mold change-- the head is new and designed to look like his G1 self, and is a very well-done sculpt. However, this mold was actually designed in advance for Sideswipe, so Sideswipe actually has an alternate transformation from Sunstreaker, even though no mold changes in those respects have been made! It basically ends up with him having a G1-accurate chest and a quite accurate robot mode, despite sharing 95% of his mold with Sunstreaker. An utterly brilliant move, I must say. As far as looks go, I think the design is slightly better than Sunstreaker's, as the front hood for the chest meshes with the surrounding parts better than Sunstreaker's chest.
Universe 2.0 Sidewipe is a fantastic mold in a fantastic color scheme, and even if you already have Universe 2.0 Sunstreaker, he has an alternate transformation and a new head, thus making his robot mode partially "new" as well. Highly recommended, even if you already have Universe 2.0 Sunstreaker.


Sideswipe Bio :
Sideswipe is a natural-born fighter. He's had little formal training in the arts of war, yet he has an instinctive nature that makes him one of the most dangerous warriors among the Autobots. He is not as self-centered or cold blooded as his brother Sunstreaker, but the two are nonetheless close. When they fight side by side, they are truly a force to be reckoned with.
