Tread Bolt (Universe 2.0)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Voyager
Series : Classic/Generation One
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme : Moderately dark blue, black, light milky gray, and some bright orange, moderately light brown, yellow, dull "cheese" yellow, transparent dark brownish amber, and silver
Rating : 9.5

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Classics Voyager Jetfire. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Classics Voyager Jetfire here .)

Tread Bolt (I'll never understand why they keep giving that name to a jet ) is a partial homage to the G1 micromaster of the same name, as G1 Treadbolt was a primarily dark blue stealth bomber. It's not a perfect fit by any means, but I'll take any obscure Micromaster homages I can get. Tread Bolt's color scheme seems largely inspired by the Blue Angels, being dark blue with some really dynamic, slanted stripes along the edges of his wings in vehicle mode. He also has "Air Defense" emblems on the main body of his jet mode, which are similar to the "Air Force" emblems on G1 Tread Bolt. The light gray, black and brown serve as excellent secondary colors (which are considerably more visible in robot mode), and add quite a bit of a variety to Tread Bolt's color scheme beyond just the blue and yellow. He also has plenty of paint apps, pretty much all of which are well-placed-- the bright orange details on his robot legs and thrusters really contrast well, and I love love LOVE the silver-painted helmet in robot mode. Unfortunately, there are a few minor paint apps I would've changed-- I don't like the dark blue face, for one, it just looks odd. I'm also not fond of where they placed the Autobot symbol-- it's right behind the cockpit in vehicle mode, with some mold detailing indents basically blocking out the eyes on the symbol, which makes it look pretty weird. Another color choice I'm not fond of is the transparent brownish-amber used for the missiles and the cockpit windows-- it's just an ugly color.
No mold changes have been made to Universe 2.0 Tread Bolt.
Universe 2.0 Tread Bolt is a great mold with a great color scheme. A few minor things, such as the head and the transparent plastic color, I would have done different, but overall he's well worth the $20 price point and looks a lot better than any other color scheme this mold has received. Highly recommended.


Tread Bolt Bio :
Tread Bolt is a ghost. Though he is an aerial ace, his real talent lies in stealth. Special sensor-refractive armor renders him invisible to all but the most sophisticated detection technology. Silent, super-precise lasers let him take out enemy spotters before they can shout a warning. More than a few Decepticons have been blown to pieces before they ever even heard him coming. He has alienated a lot of his fellow Autobots, because when he's not sneaking up on Decepticons, he creeps around scaring the heck out of anyone he can find.
