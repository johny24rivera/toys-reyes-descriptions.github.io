Cyclonus (McDonald's)

Allegiance : Decepticon
Size : Happy Meal Size
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Dark purplish blue, gray, light bluish white, light orange, and some lime green
Rating : 1.7

Vehicle mode is a futuristic helicopter. This mode is certainly reminiscent of the "official" Armada Cyclonus toy, but for some reason, the cockpit resembles Cyclonus when he's plugged into Crumplezone, and not Cyclonus when he's solo. Regardless, this mode is pretty crummy- but then again, so was the "official" Cyclonus' vehicle mode as well. The body of the helicopter is too large, the wings are too small, and his arms are clearly visible under them. His head is also just right THERE in plain sight, which ruins any look of authenticity this mode might have had. And although there's excellent mold detailing, not much of it can be easily seen, since the only paint on this entire toy is on the robot face. Cyclonus' gimmick is pretty cool, though- roll him along on a flat surface and his rotor spins faster and faster until it finally takes off. Granted, you really have to try hard to get this thing to go THAT fast, but it can happen. Only drawback to this is that the rotor is a seperate piece, and can come off easily. Cyclonus has one Powerlinx port on the top tail end of this mode, and a Decepticon symbol in front of his cockpit windows.
Basically all you do to transform Cyclonus into his robot mode is pull his helicopter tail forward to become his legs and pull up on his head a little. As such, the two modes are very similar. The huge rotor on the top behind his head just looks goofy in this mode, however. His chest also sticks out way too far for a Transformer, and looks ridiculous compared to the straight feet.
Cyclonus has a cool spinning rotor gimmick, but that's about it. Unless you're a completist, pass.
