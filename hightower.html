Hightower (Landfill Part)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Japanese Name : Build Cyclone
Color Scheme : Red, brick red, light black, light gray, silver, and some black, neon orange, and transparent tinted plastic
Mold History : Hightower was a completely new mold created for Car Robots (which subsequently became Robots in Disguise).
Rating : 7.9

Vehicle mode is a crane. Hightower is extremely kiddy-looking in this mode, even more so than his similarly-structued team member, Heavy Load . It is much more "square" in design than a real crane, not to mention the fact that the base part is MUCH bigger than on an actual crane. The crane hook appendage is also much shorter than on an actual crane; much, MUCH shorter. You really can't envision the crane picking up anything much higher than it upper body, which kinda limits its functionality. The hook is also molded shut, so you can't hang anything on it- nuts, that would've been kinda cool. What's kind of cool is that several people have come up with a "gunner" sub-mode for Hightower; flip the hook part of the crane arm in and the gun part out, and you have a cannon! That's pretty neat, but it still doesn't make this mode much better. THe robot feet are also very apparent on the back side, and the robot face and hands are also a little obvious on the upper body and in the middle of the sides, respectively. The front bumper also looks pretty crummy, since there's a pretty large gap between the two bumper plates.
Hightower's robot mode is awesome. Sure, detaching and than re-attaching the upper part of the crane is a pretty cheap part of the transformation, but at least it's effective. Hightower is rather short for a deluxe, but he makes up for it in bulkiness. His upper arms and lower legs are very bulky, giving him a "tough" look. For such a bulky toy, he's also pretty articulated in this mode- he has three points of articulation for each arm, and four on each leg (two of them being in the feet, which is very rare for a Transformer). However, his feet are also very large and floppy proportionally, making them look a bit dorky. At least his large feet and lower legs give him very good stability in this mode. His face is also pretty well done, with a "visor" for his eyes. He has a very stern look, which adds character. The upper part of the crane on Hightower's back may be viewed as an extra in this mode, but it doesn't get in the way much at all and it doesn't look that bad. Hightower has not just one, but two weapons for his robot mode; a really large and long gun made out of his crane arm, and a very small gun that comes out of a container in the driver's seat. These two guns contrast with each other very nicely.
Becomes either both the arms or one of the legs for Landfill , depending on the configuration.
A rather kiddy vehicle mode, but a rockin' robot mode. Recommended over Heavy Load, but only slightly more- they're both still neat toys in robot mode.


Hightower Bio:
MOTTO: "Stay alert; danger is everywhere."
Hightower is an excellent marksmen, storming into battle with a cannon in one fist and a magnum in the other. He likes to make things hot for Megatron with fire weapons and flame throwers. He is very alert to dangerous situations and often guards the Construction Team leader, as well as providing the solid support and advice of a seasoned warrior.
