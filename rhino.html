Rhino

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Happy Meal Size
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Grey, red, and some silver, purple, and orange
Rating : 2.5

Beast mode is a rhino, obviously. (By the way, Rhino has no official name, he is simply called Rhino.) Rhino's robot legs are very visible in this mode, and subtract from the overall look of the beast mode. Although the red lining inside Rhino's front beast mode legs gives away that he's a robot in disguise, this doesn't look too bad. Not too bad of a beast mode for a McDonald's toy, but still relatively crummy.
Robot mode is a big disappointment, though. This mode looks more like a dumb karate fighter in a grey suit than a robot in disguise. He has an awkwardly long body, and his arms are very slim and don't look very nice. His back rhino legs stick out a bit, and make this robot seem even weirder. His legs and head are the only decent part of this mode. His legs are each articulated at the waist, which is rare for a Happy Meal toy, and his head doesn't look too different from Apocalypse's , a villain on X-Men.
Beast mode is okay, but robot mode is very crummy, even for a Happy Meal toy. Not recommended.
