Optimus Prime (Battle Steel) [Gravity Bot]

Allegiance : Autobot
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Dark sparkly blue, swirly metallic gunmetal gray, and some silver, black, light metallic silvery blue, light sky blue, and red
Rating : 5.0

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the original Gravity Bot Optimus Prime. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original Gravity Bot Optimus Prime here .)

Like many of the late RotF line Optimus Prime repaints, the Gravity Bot redeco's chief difference from Optimus Prime's traditional colors is replacing the front red section with a silvery-gray color, and the changing of the colors of the flame deco apps. I have to say, although it makes Prime a bit less colorful, the shade of gray plastic they used for Optimus Prime's front half of his vehicle mode is pretty good looking. Normally gray plastic on TFs looks pretty dull, but this is a shade dark enough and "swirly" enough, with enough metallic flakes in it, where it actually looks rather metallic. It fades right into the silver flame deco pattern coming off of it perfectly. The light red flames on the front end of the vehicle mode are a tad light for my taste, but they still contrast with the gray fairly well and give Optimus a "warm" color given that the red plastic is now gone from his mold. Other than these changes to the front half of the vehicle mode, Battle Steel Optimus Prime is pretty much the same as the original Gravity Bot when it comes to colors and paint apps.
No mold changes have been made to Battle Steel Optimus Prime.
Battle Steel Optimus Prime is about on par with the original Gravity Bot in terms of color scheme. Although the replacement of most of his red with gray makes him less colorful, it's a really nice, attractive shade of gunmetal gray and goes well with the other colors. The flame deco on the front still provides him with a bit of red, anyways.
