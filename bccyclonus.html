Cyclonus (Botcon 2002 Exclusive)

Allegiance : Decepticon
Size : Ultra
Difficulty of Transformation : Hard
Color Scheme : Silver-gray, chrome deep purple, dark sparkly watery blue, pinkish purple, purple, dark metallic purple, metallic purple, transparent red, and some white, silver, light red, red, brownish purple, and metallic turquoise
Rating : 8.9

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Beast Machines Ultra Jetstorm. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Beast Machines Ultra Jetstorm here .)

The classic G1 character of Cyclonus is resurrected for Transfans, this time as a purple-and-silver repaint of Ultra Jetstorm. This toy has practically every shade of purple you can THINK of on it, and its color scheme definitely hails from the old Cyclonus toy, with some dark blue thrown in for a little more contrast. The "metallic" look of most of the toy is also definitely a plus. Unlike the Ultra Jetstorm toy, Botcon Cyclonus doesn't seem to have the widespread chrome chipping problem, thankfully. At least, it doesn't yet.... Cyclonus has two Decepticon symbols emblazoned on his wings, and a Decepticon spark crystal. However, the spark crystal is not transparent with a symbol etched on the back of it- instead, it's solid with the allegiance symbol painted on the front. Not quite as cool of an effect, but it's still neat-looking. However, one thing noticeable about Cyclonus is he doesn't have as much paint detailing as Ultra Jetstorm- something you would have expected MORE of in a Botcon toy, not less. And there's no new paint applications (with the exception of the Decepticon symbols), just the same old ones in different colors.
No mold changes have been made to Botcon Cyclonus, so he retains all the features and flaws of his predecessor in that area.
Cyclonus is a pretty cool toy, with a neat color scheme and minus the chrome chipping problem of Ultra Jetstorm. However, the less paint detailing and lack of new paint apps cancel those positives out. So, I can't say I like Cyclonus any more or less and Ultra Jets. Recommended if you like the exclusives, but otherwise, don't bother.


Cyclonus Bio :
FUNCTION: Saboteur
MOTTO: "Loyalty and compassion cannot coexist."
After exploiting Megatron's sinister attempts to ensnare his Spark in a Vehicon body, Cyclonus escaped Cybertron in hopes of fulfilling his proud Decepticon heritage. Powerful, subtle, and loyal to the extreme. Capable of drawing strength from his enemies' attacks through bleedback receptors mounted in his fists. Highly maneuverable in jet mode due to dual fusion-powered engines and an advanced multi-wing design. In both modes, Cyclonus is armed with close-range oxidating pulse cannons and independently targetable incendiary missiles. Vulnerable only to leaders that would exploit his skills for their own evil purposes.
