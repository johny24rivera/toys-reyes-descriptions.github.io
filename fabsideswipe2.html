Night Blades Sideswipe (Fast Action Battler)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation: Easy
Color Scheme : Dark milky metallic gray, pale light grayish green, dull grayish green, bright lime green, and some silver, black, metallic bluish green, and moderately light red
Rating : 7.0

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Battle Blade Sideswipe. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Battle Blade Sideswipe here .)

Night Blades Sideswipe certainly is adequately named, that's for sure. He has a very "night-ish" color scheme, with the most noticeable color contrast between the light green used for his windows, front grill, tailpipes, and side doors (with a nice "fade" pattern on the latter) and the dark metallic gray that makes up most of the base plastic of the toy. Both color shades are very nice-- the light green is a nice "neon"-esque color like you'd see on a heavily customized tuner, while the dark gray has a bit of a "metallic swirl" action going on, which helps it escape the boring look of most other shades of gray plastic used on Transformers. The silver down the center of the vehicle mode also adds a bit more of a dynamic touch, and the metallic blue-green used for the headlights and red for the taillights helps give Sideswipe a wider color palette. He also has a rather nice "targeting reticle over an Autobot symbol" license plate. Why it's that way is beyond me, but it looks cool regardless! In robot mode his color scheme-- although okay and still more interesting than this mold's original color scheme-- is definitely a step down from how he looks in vehicle mode, however. The green used for most of his robot parts is a rather blah, dull grayish green-- whether of a slightly lighter or darker shade-- and just doesn't contrast well enough with some of the other colors like the silver on his legs and face, for example. He also doesn't have nearly as many paint apps in this mode, unfortunately.
No mold changes have been made to Night Blades Sideswipe.
Night Blades Sideswipe is an EXCELLENT repaint in vehicle mode, looking like he came right out of a Pro Tuner customization shop. The light green and silver contrast very well with most of the other colors, and he's got paint apps to spare. Unfortunately the green used on his robot parts is a bit uglier of a shade, but overall this is definitely a color scheme I wish they had used on other (better) Sideswipe molds than the Fast Action Battler.
