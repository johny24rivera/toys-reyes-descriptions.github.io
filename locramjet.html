Ramjet (Legends of Cybertron; Botcon 2005 Hasbro Giveaway)

Allegiance : Decepticon
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme : Off-white, black, moderately light chalky blue, and some silver and yellow
Rating : 7.6

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Legends of Cybertron Starscream. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of LoC Starscream here .)

LoC Ramjet is a big ol' nod to Universe Ramjet , in both that he's a repaint of a Seeker mold and in color scheme. LoC Ramjet mixes it up a little, though, in that he has decidedly more black than his Universe counterpart, and no gray. The gray looks really good against the white, especially on the face-- the black face around the white helmet makes Ramjet look really evil, almost like he has no face at all! I'm not fond of the blue and the yellow-on-white, however, just like I wasn't fond of it on Universe Ramjet-- it looks a little too loud and... un-Decepticonish. The blue is of a slightly less obnoxious shade than it was on Universe Ramjet, though, which is good.
Given that he's a free giveaway from Hasbro just for coming to the convention (although you can buy him for anywhere from $10-25 online, depending on where you look and how lucky you are), Ramjet features no mold changes, and with the exception of a slight alteration to his chest app, his paint apps are a strict pallet-switch, with no new paint apps when compared to LoC Starscream.
LoC Ramjet is an okay repaint-- the black parts look pretty nice, though I don't like how un-Decepticony his color scheme is. LoC Starscream definitely looks better in the color department, not to mention he's cheaper, too, so there's no real reason to get Ramjet unless you're an LoC completist.
