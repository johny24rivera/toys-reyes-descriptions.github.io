Frostbite

Allegiance : Decepticon
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Hard
Color Scheme : Pale pearly blue-white, pale arctic blue, pale purple, bluish black, and some silver, glossy violet, dull gold, greenish gold, deep purple, sky blue, and light red
Rating : 8.7

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Beast Wars Jawbreaker. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Beast Wars Jawbreaker here .)

I'll be straightforward about this guy; Frostbite has one of the best paint jobs of any Universe toy, and that's a HUGE compliment. Frostbite looks absolutely fantastic; the dull whites, purples, and blues just go together incredibly well to create exactly the look that Frostbite's name implies; a cruel, arctic-themed Decepticon. I mean, on a hyena, of all creatures, you don't think an arctic scheme would work, but it really does. Frostbite, as is typical with Universe toys, has a ton of paint detailing on him; nearly every mechanical mold detail on him has some paint on it, and given that he's a Transmetal 2 repaint, that means that he's got a LOT of the stuff on him. (The Energon radiation apps are the purpleish spray apps on the top of his beast mode, I think...) Many of the colors are used only once or twice (like on the eyes, chest, or upper legs), giving him a more unique, asymmetrical, twisted look than he already has. (And, of course, since this mold is a Decepticon now, it certainly works.) Unfortunately, he DOES just have a Decepticon symbol painted over his spark crystal, which looks kinda cheap, but that is common among all Universe figs, so I can't fault Frostbite in particular here.
No mold changes have been made to Frostbite.
Frostbite is a definite step above his mold predecessor Jawbreaker, and even Jawbreaker had a pretty good color scheme. The arctic scheme, along with the plethora of mold and paint detailing on Frostbite, makes him one of the best Universe repaints yet. Highly recommended if you like beast TFs.
