Skywarp (Leader) [Combiner Wars]

Allegiance : Decepticon
Size : Leader
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Black, purple, and some silver, transparent orange, metallic pumpkin orange, transparnet cherry red, and metallic gunmetal gray
Rating : 7.7

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Generations Leader Jetfire. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Generations Leader Jetfire here .)

Skywarp is a fairly by-the-book redeco as far as the many Skywarp redecoes over the years have gone; as is traditional, he's mostly black with a fair amount of purple and silver highlights, and a few minor accent colors. The purple used on Skywarp is a little darker than on most Skywarps, but only a touch; it still contrasts quite well against the black and looks great as part of the color scheme overall. Also as expected, Skywarp has a fair amount of silver, particularly in robot mode, where his chest and waist are painted almost entirely silver. He's also got some silver lines contrasting against the much fatter purple lines on his main wings, and silver and black lines against solid purple tailfins-- it's a minor change, but it generally looks good, though I wish the silver stripes were a bit fatter on the main wings. Another slight change from the usual is that circles on Skywarp's chest and waist are painted a nice gunmetal gray, a color that both contrasts and complements the silver paint surrounding them-- this is a nice little extra to break up the silver on a toy of this size. As is usual for Seekers, Skywarp has transparent orange plastic used for his cockpit. He's also got a little transparent red plastic for his eyes, but the light piping doesn't really work, so it doesn't really show up well. The faux jet nose chest details in robot mode have an appropriately orange cockpit (a nice metallic shade of the color, too), but the details around it are painted purple! The nosecone isn't purple in vehicle mode, so this choice was rather odd, and I feel that purple paint could've been better used elsewhere, like on the entirely-black undersides of the wings, which are pretty dominant in robot mode.
Skywarp has the same mold changes made to the Leader class Jetfire mold as Thundercracker , but has no unique mold changes himself.
Combiner Wars Leader Skywarp is pretty much your typical expected Starscream redeco, with the best overall color scheme of the "usual trio" of Seekers that doesn't break the mold in terms of color scheme and paint apps, even if there's a few minor differences here and there (like the gunmetal gray and purple on the chest in robot mode). If you only get one Leader-class Seeker, I'd make it this one, but Seekers tend to be better as a squadron. Since getting three Leader class toys of one mold may be a bit steep for some, I'd thus only mildly recommend him, given the flaws of the mold and the many smaller, more affordable options for the character.


Skywarp (Leader) Bio :
Skywarp is the only known Cybertronian who can teleport. The Decepticons want to make him a super weapon. He just wants to be one of the bots. Skywarp would rather use his power for pranks than in battle. One day Megatron may lose patience and turn him into scrap. Until the Decepticons can learn how to replicate his ability, his potential is too great to give up on.
