Prowl (Deluxe, Redeco) [RiD]

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Hard
Japanese Name : (Super) Mach Alert
Color Scheme : White, dark metallic blue, gray, silver, tinted clear plastic, and some chrome gold, gold, red, transparent red, transparent blue, light orange, black, light black, dull orange, and yellow
Mold History : Prowl was a completely new mold created for Car Robots (which subsequently became Robots in Disguise).
Rating : 8.4

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the original deluxe RiD Prowl. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original deluxe RiD Prowl here .)

Out of the three Car Brothers redecos, Prowl is the one whose color scheme has changed the least. He still retains the same basic color scheme of dark blue and white- however, there's much more dark blue on him this time around, and gold has been added as well. This makes his mode look quite a bit nicer, even though it's still the same color scheme overall. His gold tires and his gold-traced shoulder pads in robot mode look very nice indeed, as well as his transmetal gold Autobot symbol on his chest. In his vehicle mode, the Japanese lettering has been replaced by English this time around- the word "Police" graces his hood and side doors. His Japanese name, "Mach Alert", is also on his side doors as well.
No mold changes have been made to this toy since its last release, so Prowl retains all the features and flaws of his predecessor in that area.
It's amazing how much "Super" Prowl's look is improved upon from the original, while still using the same basic color scheme. Recommended over the original.

Review by Beastbot

Second Opinion by VBBN

Rating : 8.5

This time around, Prowl gets a color scheme consisting of a dominating blue in color, with white as a secondary color and chrome gold bits to add a bit of RID flare. In my opinion, this is the best version of the RID Prowls we have gotten (Original deluxe, this one, and Spychanger ). The blue is much more interesting and pleasing to the eyes, and is a bit more appropriate for Prowl here. The only color from the original Prowl that isn't seen here is the chrome red on the chest in robot mode, now replaced with chrome gold. The chrome silver tires are chrome gold now as well.
Althought the mold has no mold changes, it does have a slight deco change, he has POLICE: Mach Alert on the sides of the car and "Police" across the hood. I liked the Japenese writing better, but in no way am I counting the paint change against the mold. Better to keep the molds unique.
On a final note, the doors are on very weak ball sockets on this guy it seems, both of mine snapped.
Mildly recommended, as it is a decent mold and better than the original prowl.

Review by VBBN
