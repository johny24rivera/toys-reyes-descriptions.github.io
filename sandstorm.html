Sandstorm (BotCon 1999 Exclusive)

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Mega
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Silver-black, gold-orange, and some neon green, black, and dark purple
Rating : 7.8

Beast mode is a scorpion. This repaint of Scorponok keeps all the same features, including the exaggerated proportions, unfortunately. The stinger looks way too big and bulky in proportion to the rest of his body, as does his claws. His scorpion legs also look way too small. This is almost made up for, however, by the extremely cool color scheme; the silvers and orange-golds make this look a bit more like a real scorpion. The little action feature in this mode is that, if you press up on a lever on his tail, his stinger will swing forward, which is neat.
Robot mode looks very nice, although it still has the same exaggerated proportions of the stinger and claws. The problem with Scorponok was that the spring that was used to launch the cyberbee wore out after a while; this toy fixes that problem, however, which is a nice pro. The other claw fires two missles when you lift it, and while this is cool, can get kind of annoying, since you can set it off pretty easily. The scorpion legs that hold this mode together can wear out after a while, however, like all plastic does, and after this toy is more than a few months old, the body and stinger seperate a little too easily. Again, the color scheme works nicely with this mode, although I think the "mutant head" looks better than the robot head.
A nice transformer; recommended over the original, if you want to spend the extremely expensive price for it.


Sandstorm Bio :
FUNCTION: Covert Ops Commander
MOTTO: "When destiny cries out my name, I must answer!"
Although Predacon in name, Sandstorm seems to resemble a Maximal of old. A courageous commander of Predacon resistance forces, Sandstorm demands nothing less than utter commitment and total loyalty from his troops. He was instrumental in bringing together the warring factions of the Maximals and Predacons against the tyranny that inhabits Cybertron. His quick decisions and fair judgments are his foremost attributes that instill respect from both Maximal and Predacon alike. In both beast and robot modes, Sandstorm is paired with a cyber-bee infused with the Spark of a deceased comrade. Sandstorm's weaponry includes twin shock-concussion missiles which launch from his claws and a deadly electro-arc sting which erupts from his tail, disabling any foe within his immediate vicinity.
