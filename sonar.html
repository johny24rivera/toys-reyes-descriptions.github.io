Sonar

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Basic
Difficulty of Transformation : Hard
Color Scheme : Chrome reddish pink, blue-gray, silver, gold, and some spots of neon green
Rating : 7.1

Beast mode is a metallized, mutated bat with huge claws. Her face is very menacing, and she has metal wings with some clumps of fur still showing on them. Her wings cant flap in this mode, though, and her robot legs sort of stick out on the bottom. Her articulation is also very limited in this mode, basically involving only her mouth and hind legs.
Her robot mode doesnt have as many good points as it does bad. Her transmetal orb is under a piece in her chest. Her claws look even larger in this mode than they did in beast mode. Her wings sort of look small in comparison to the rest of the body. Her legs are a little short in comparison to her arms, but this is no big loss. Her bat legs are very odd-looking, making it look like she has four arms. Her elbows cant move either, which is a minus.
A nice transformer, although kind of weak in robot mode. She is organic on one side but metallic on the other, which has a nice fusing effect. Her transforming isnt hard for the most part, but it is very hard to get her legs flipped through his body. I would just take them off when transforming.
