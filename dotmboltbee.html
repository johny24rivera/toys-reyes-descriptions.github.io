Bumblebee (Bolt; DotM)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size: Legion
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Glittery dull yellow, black, and some metallic silvery blue, silver and metallic gunmetal gray
Rating : 7.5

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the original RotF Legends class Bumblebee. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original RotF Legends class Bumblebee here .)

"Bolt" Bumblebee is another of those "barely-repaint" repaints of the toy. His basic color scheme of yellow and black stays the same on this toy and in the same places, so there's no real changes there. His windows are a bit darker than on most versions of this mold, and he's got a few darker gunmetal gray paint apps on his robot mode face and legs, but the major change here is the addition of the "lightning bolt" decals on Bumblebee's front and sides, at the cost of his stripes (his front grill details are still painted a gunmetal gray, though). These lightning bolts form a pretty cool pattern, particularly with the thin lines of silver strewn throughout the black bolts-- the silver lines are also present on the big Autobot symbol on his hood, as well. That said, I don't think they're all that better than Bumblebee's usual stripes, just a little different.
Bumblebee's right hand has been remolded to be able to hold Cyberverse-scaled weapons; otherwise, no mold changes have been made to this version.
Bolt Bumblebee, taken by itself, is a pretty decent Legends figure, and the "bolt" paint apps are fairly nice. That said, the rationale for this release puzzles me-- it's of a mold that's been repainted (and in similar colors) several times already; it's not part of a store-exclusive multi-pack where presumably having a Bumblebee in the pack was a part of the deal; it's done on the inferior (and, as of this movie, now inaccurate) version of the Legion class Bumblebee mold; and it was released at the same time as the "normal" DotM Legion class Bumblebee (it was technically part of wave 2 of the size class, but both waves were released at the same time). It's just pointless, and there's many other versions of the character at this size class that I'd recommend over this one.
