K-9

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Yellow, white, black, and blue.
Rating : 7.1

Beast mode is a german shepherd. Although he is a repaint of Wolfang , some parts have been modified that make this repaint better than the original. He is slightly bigger than Wolfang, with a longer back and longer tail. Almost no extras except for his robot hands on his belly and the back of his back legs, which are smooth and painted white and blue, like most of his robot mode. There is not many movable parts in this mode, however; only his back legs can move.
Robot mode has a lot of extras, which is why this didnt earn a higher rating. There are little flesh plates that stick out of his midsection, his front feet stick out of his upper arms, and his beast face plates are large and limit movement. However, his mask is cooler-looking(at least in my opinion) than Wolfangs, and bigger, as is his shield and gun that fires grappling hooks. The detailing is also very good, too.
A very nice toy, although it has a lot of extras in his robot mode.


K-9 Bio :
FUNCTION: Guard Duty
Placed in charge of guarding his fellow Maximals from a surprise Predacon attack, K-9 is always alert thanks to his lightning-fast reflexes and ultra-sensitive canine nose. While other Maximals plot defense strategies, K-9 stands guard to survey the surrounding area for signs of Predacon spies. But even if he encounters an entire attack force, he can hold them off with his fast-fire missile launcher until reinforcements arrive. When it comes to combat, K-9's bite is definitely worse than his bark!
