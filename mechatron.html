Mechatron

Allegiance : Vehicon
Size : Beast Rider Size
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Transparent red, light red, dark blue, light black, black, silver, silvery-black, and some bright yellow and dark blue (original); transparent violet, yellow, tan, black, dark gray, purple, and some light red and pale yellow (repaint)
Rating : 5.8 (original); 5.2 (repaint)

Mechatron is the Beast Rider for Megatron, and it reflects it pretty obviously. The Mechatron Beast Rider is designed like a giant dragon head, with small claw "spark extractors" on the sides. There is a double-barrel missile launcher coming out of the mouth, and a wheel on each side. There is little difference between the two modes, which are named "pursuit mode" and "battle mode". "Pursuit mode" is the first picture shown above, in which the claws are tucked behind Mechatron, and the top weapons are concealed. "Battle mode" is the second picture shown, in which the claws are rotated out to the front, and the top weapons are revealed. A claw behind the top weapons is available in both modes to hold any Transformer riding Mechatron. The gimmick is also active in this mode; if you press Mechatron's "head" down and let it go, Mechatron will zoom over a flat surface for a while, his claws in front rotating around. A rather neat feature, but I would have prefered that the claws did an up-and-down movement to signify a "slashing attack", rather than the claws just rotating around in place, which looks rather odd. The claws also don't stay in any position when not activated, so it's hard to get them to stay the way you want them to (face-up, so to speak, instead of out to both sides and such). Since the two modes are so similar, it rather defeats the purpose of transforming Mechatron, and I usually just leave my Mechatron toys in "battle mode". Unlike Che , which cannot hold the toy it was made for, Mechatron can easily have the Beast Machines Megatron held still on its rear platform. This, needless to say, is pretty necessary for a Beast Rider built for a specific toy. (Although why Megatron modeled his Beast Rider after his dragon mode, which he hates, is beyond me.) The detailing on this toy is also pretty good, and a bit better than Che's. The mechanical tubing on the upper dragon jaw is really neat! The missile launcher can fire its two missiles individually as well, which is a definite plus. The original Mechatron (shown in each above pic on the left) has its color scheme very similar to Beast Machines Megatron, which is rather neat. I especially love the color scheme on the sides. The dark blue and black go REALLY well together. The silvery-gray detailing pattern on the upper sides could not look cooler, and is definitely a nice aesthetic detail. The Mechatron repaint, however, is not done nearly as well. First off, its color scheme does not represent Beast Machines Megatron at all, which is rather odd. Second, the color scheme just doesn't go that well together, period. Although the purple and black are nice, the tan and the rather bright yellow just completely clash and throw the whole thing off. The silvery-gray detailing on the original Mechatron has also been dulled down into just dark gray detailing here, and it doesn't show that well over the dark purple coloration (although it still looks rather neat).
The original Mechatron is a pretty neat toy visually, although its play value is limited because, after all, it is only an accessory, and its transformation is a joke. The Mechatron repaint has a bad color scheme, and still retains all the flaws of his predecessor's design. Still, I think Mechatron is the better of the two different Beast Riders, and I'd recommend him if you're into accessories.


Mechatron Bio :
FUNCTION: Assault Chariot
Megatron created the Beast Riders technology to hunt down the remaining inhabitants on Cybertron and capture their sparks. His personalized transport, Mechatron, is unstoppable and fashioned from his sinister recycling projects. Though purely mechanical, Mechatron resembles his master's dragon mode, due to a failed attempt by the despot to erase the organic beast from his very being. This eerie battle chariot is programmed to shred through or bombard anything that gets in its path. Twin "claw" flame-throwers debilitate enemies from large distances, and its central fusion cannon nullifies all resistance. Mechatron is outfitted with an upgraded spark-capture claw that can tear out the entire laser core of any Transformer defender.
