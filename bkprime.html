Optimus Prime (Cybertron; Burger King Toy)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Kids' Meal Size
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Dark blue, moderately light red, and some black
Rating : 2.3

Prime's vehicle mode is... alright, but it's pretty darn obvious where his head and robot arms are hidden. Granted, it's pretty darn obvious where his head is hidden on the mainline toy, but that's besides the point. It's a real shame that his windows are the only thing that's painted on him, as he really could use something to break up all the red. The mold detailing is pretty good for a Kids' Meal toy, though, and although the proportions are a big kiddy, they aren't too bad. I just hate how obvious his transformation is.
Prime's robot mode has pretty good proportions for a Kids' Meal toy, but it really looks more like G1 Prime than Cybertron Prime , given that the front of the vehicle becomes the chest and not the shoulders. He really can't move in this mode much at all, though, and it bares repeating that he really, REALLY could use some more paint detailing.
Burger King Prime is probably the best of the BK Cybertron toys, but that's really, really not saying much. Only get it if you are a severe Prime completist or are too poor to purchase any real Transformers.
