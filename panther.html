Panther

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Happy Meal Size
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Black, silver, and silver-white, with some blue and red
Rating : 2.3

Beast mode is a panther. This robot has no name, and is referred to only as "Panther". This mode looks awkward, the tail no more than a stub. His main body looks very chunky and wide, his underside making him look like a panther that had his underside glued to a sheet of metal. His legs are articulated, however, which is rare for a Happy Meal toy.
Robot mode looks even more awkward than the beast mode. His main body looks decent, but his appendages are seriously messed up. His "hands" are just backwards-facing feet, as well as his robot feet. This mode is very poorly constructed.
So-so in beast mode, but is awkward-looking, especially in robot mode, like he took someone else's appendages and stuck them on his body in the wrong places.
