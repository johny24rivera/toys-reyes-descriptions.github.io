Beast Wars Transmetals

After the alien destuction device explosion (See the Aftermath Beast Wars episode for more information), the wave rearranged the body structures of some of the Beast Warriors bodies, and gave them a new shiny look. This also resulted in the transformers having three modes; beast, vehicle, and robot. These robots have a more robotic look in beast mode and a more organic look in robot mode. These toys are, in my opinion, the best line of Beast Wars Transformers.
