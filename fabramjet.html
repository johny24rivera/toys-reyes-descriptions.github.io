Claw Slash Ramjet

Allegiance : Decepticon
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Very light gray, black, dull brick red, and some silver, charcoal bluish gray, and dark milky grayish brown
Rating : 6.0

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Battle Blade Starscream. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Battle Blade Starscream here .)

Alright, finally a Fast Action Battler-only Movie character (even if it is just a repaint)! Ramjet is an homage to the G1 character of the same name, what with being a repaint of Movie Starscream and having similar colors to his G1 counterpart. The light gray, by itself, doesn't look all that different from Movie Starscream, but the addition of dull red and black really contrast well with the main color and make the toy infinitely more interesting-looking, even ignoring the G1 colors. Ramjet certainly has a wider variety of colors than FAB Starscream, especially in robot mode-- no large patches of only one color here! His black-painted face with the red optics also looks really, REALLY evil. I also particularly like the paint apps on his wings in vehicle mode-- the black and gray bordering on the dull red wings looks really nice, and the silver Decepticon symbols on the wings are just icing on the color scheme cake.
No mold changes have been made to Claw Slash Ramjet.
Claw Slash Ramjet's colors look quite good together are a definite improvement over the rather bland-looking Battle Blade Starscream. Too bad the mold itself isn't much to look at, or I'd recommend this toy.


Claw Slash Ramjet Bio :
Ramjet is crazy, even by Decepticon standards. He's got missiles, lasers, cluster bombs, and even vibro-claws sharp enough to cut duratanium armor, but he'd rather just smash head first into his enemies. A lifetime of head injuries has left him a little muddy in his thinking, but he is super tough.
