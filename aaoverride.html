Override (Autobot Alliance)

Allegiance: Decepticon
Size : Legend
Difficulty of Transformation: Very Easy
Color Scheme : Dark blue, black, and some silver, metallic gunmetal gray, pale metallic yellowish green, pale metallic gold, and red
Rating : 7.2

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to RotF Legends Sideways. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of RotF Legends Sideways here .)

Override is a fair rarity among Transformers redecos nowadays-- it's a repaint that both isn't A. a same-character redeco and/or B. an homage to a previous character. Nope, Override here pretty much stands on his (her?) own. The dark blue-and-black color scheme would be pretty boring by itself-- though the shade of dark blue is a particuarly nice one. However, the metallic yellowish green stripes in vehicle mode (and paint on the upper legs of the robot mode) really help to add some color contrast and decoration in there, along with the silver on the windows, grill, and face, and the pale gold on the headlights. There's also some darker gunmetal gray used for some mold detailing near the rear of the vehicle, but they're dark enough where they don't stand out quite enough from the dark blue for my liking. All this said, I do wish Override had a bit more spread-out paint detailing-- perhaps some more paint apps on the arms in robot mode, which have plenty of mold detailing that needs highlighting. Override's taillights are also sadly unpainted, as well. The toy has "enough" paint, but it's almost all centered on the vehicle front/chest and windows.
No mold changes have been made to Autobot Alliance Override.
Autobot Alliance Override is a pretty nice redeco of the RotF Sideways mold, though a few more of those yellowish-green paint apps (particularly on the arms) would have really made it pop. Still, it's definitely the best paint job I've seen this mold get.
