Bumblebee (Bolt) [Gravity Bot]

Allegiance : Autobot
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Black, metallic gold, and some dark milky gray, light sky blue, red, silver, light metallic bluish silver, and metallic bronze
Rating : 6.2

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the original Gravity Bot Bumblebee. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original Gravity Bot Bumblebee here .)

Bolt Bumblebee is a bit different from your typical "inverted colors" Movie Bumblebee repaint. Yes, he's mostly black, and yes, his primary accent color is of the yellowish kind. However, his lightning bolts and robot mode helmet are made of an EXTREMELY nice-looking metallic gold, and really work well with the black. Plus, Bolt Bumblebee also has some metallic bronze, silver, and metallic bluish silver as well, making him look very metallic indeed as far as the accent colors go. Even the lesser-used accent colors, such as a bit of light sky blue and red, really pop on the few places they're used and stand out greatly from the black and other colors.
No mold changes have been made to Bolt Bumblebee.
Yes, he's "just" a Gravity Bot repaint, but Bolt Bumblebee has an outstanding deco job, with all of his paint colors-- mostly metallic-- really working well against the black and other basic colors. Plus, gold lightning bolts are just schnazzy. Definitely recommended over the original Gravity Bot Bumblebee; I wish this same gold coloration had been applied to more of the various Movie Bumblebee repaints, that's for sure.
