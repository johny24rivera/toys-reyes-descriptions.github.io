Buzzclaw

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Basic
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Transparent orange and pink, green, and purple, with a bit of yellow and sparkly blue
Rating : 6.4

Beast mode is a mantis with reptilian feet. Looks very nice in this mode, although his robot legs can be easily seen under his tail. Press the tab on his back, and his mantis arms will spread themselves out, making a "slashing attack", although it is really kind of weak.
Robot mode has a lot of extras. His reptilian feet and big and stick to the bottom of his hands, and his tail mantis hands and wings stick out in the back, although the wings are probably supposed to. You can rectify this by taking off his backpack and using it as a weapon, while taking the tail off and using it as a shield (pictured above). While the shield looks okay this way, the weapon just looks rather... odd. Other than that, an okay mode, with a mean-looking face, and great detailing.
A basically okay fuzor, but not that recommended it you see others you dont have. Also, dont confuse his name with Buzzsaw , the deluxe non-fuzor hornet (and the basic Beast Machines wasp).


Buzzclaw Bio :
FUNCTION: Saboteur, Quick-Attack Specialist
Part mantis, part lizard, Buzzclaw has all the makings of a ferocious but sly warrior. In beast mode Buzzclaw crushes his enemies with spiny pincers and venomous mandibles while a powerful tail lashes out to land his surprised prey on their backside. As a flying robot Buzzclaw generates lethal ion discs from his forearm-mounted stilettos, paralyzing his victims and rendering them helpless. Generally stoic and silent, when it comes to the business of battle Buzzclaw is arrogant, resourceful, and intent on the job at hand.
