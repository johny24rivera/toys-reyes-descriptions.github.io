Vice Grip (BotCon 1998 Exclusive)

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Basic
Difficulty of Transformation : Hard
Color Scheme : Black, silver, and dark purple with some slightly lighter purple
Rating : 6.7

Beast mode is an earwig bug. (I'm not sure what it is.) Looks very menacing, with a color scheme almost exactly matching that of Antagony's. Not all that many extras if seen from above. His robot legs can be seen under the body from a side view, however. Articulation in this mode is pretty limited to the tail, however, since his claws can open and close, and the parts of his insect mouth.
Robot mode is very organic-looking. One of the downsides to this mode is that the insect legs on the arms can get in the way some, and his hands don't really look like anything resembling hands at all. His claw weapon is very nice-looking, but the lever that moves the claws is in his hand in this mode, making the movement look rather awkward. When you move it while in his hand, one of the claws stays put while the other claw and the main body of the weapon move to one side. Legs and trunk are fine, though.
A nice repaint of Powerpinch , actually better than the original, but, unfortunately, just as hard to transform.


Vice Grip Bio :
FUNCTION: Reconnaissance
MOTTO: "The search often begins with yourself."
Vice Grip is one of Megatron's most loyal and dependable troops. He understands the value of a mission and does his utmost to complete it successfully. This drive, coupled by his extraordinary tracking abilities, is well received by the Predacon leader. His comrades have deep respect for him and often look to him for guidance and advice. In insect mode, his rear pincers can easily crush unsuspecting Maximals, and then finish them off with a high-voltage electric surge. I robot mode, the pincers convert to a hand-held pulse weapon that discharges an explosive ball of electricity capable of shorting out Maximals in a twenty meter radius.
