Sideswipe (OTFCC 2003 Exclusive)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Hard
Color Scheme : Red, tinted clear plastic, white, black, flat silver, and some sky blue, chrome red, yellow, shiny gold, and metallic gunmetal gray
Rating : 8.4

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to RiD Prowl. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of RiD Prowl here .)

OTFCC Sideswipe is meant to be the same character as the classic G1 character of the same name, and it shows, both in his red-and-white color scheme and in his alt mode. In fact, his alt mode is almost a spot-on representation of his G1 alt mode- if you take away the boosters, of course. Like his Exclusive brother Sunstreaker , Sideswipe has a mold change made to him from the original RiD Prowl mold- the sirens are gone, with a red sticker in its place. (There's a sticker there instead of red paint because the white plastic on this toy isn't made to hold onto paint very well.) While this does make him more accurate to his G1 counterpart, this DOES mean that the boosters can no longer be held onto his sirens, or on his back in robot mode as a jet pack. If you ask me, it really wasn't worth the trouble, since you now HAVE to have his jet boosters in only one place in each mode or you have to put them aside.
As for Sideswipe's color scheme, it's pretty nice- I like red a bit more than Sunstreaker's yellow. However, the same complaints surface here- although Sideswipe has plenty of color contrast in his robot mode, his vehicle mode is a little boring, given that it's almost all either red or black. More paint detailing in this mode would have made it look better.
Sideswipe is a pretty decent repaint, even though I like the Super RiD Prowl version better. This one's vehicle mode is a bit "blah", and I don't like the fact that the police sirens have been removed. Still, it's a great tribute to a G1 character, and worth picking up if you can afford it. Recommended slightly over his brother Sunstreaker, both because I think red looks better than yellow and you'll probably find this one for cheaper, since it's not a Hotel Exclusive as well.


Sideswipe Bio :
FUNCTION: Warrior
MOTTO: "I don't break the rules, I bend them-- a lot."
Sideswipe is nearly the equal of his twin brother, Sunstreaker, in the combat arts, but far less cold blooded. Relishes a fight to the finish with an opponent, and isn't above using dirty, underhanded tactics to come out on top. In robot mode, his arms can act as powerful pile drivers and can deliver up to 4 tons of force per impact with five impacts per second. Double-barreled multi-purpose launcher mounted on right arm fires dual incendiary missiles and doubles as a melee weapon, which can create twin fiery sabers. Shoulder mounted weapons ports fires blinding magnesium-phosphorous flares visible for 18 miles. In car mode, rear mounted rocket boosters allow Sideswipe reach speeds in excess of 450 mph for up to 2 minutes.
