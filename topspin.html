Topspin (2008 Collectors' Club Exclusive)

Allegiance : Decepticon
Size : Scout
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Light red, transparent cherry red, transparent pale dark blue, silver, and some black
Rating : 6.5

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Energon Stormcloud. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Energon Stormcloud here .)

Topspin continues with the "red and blue" colors that have been used on the "freebie" Club Exclusive limbs each year up to this point, though his red is noticeably lighter than the other primarily-red limbs. What's also odd about Topspin is that although he has transparent parts, he's not MOSTLY transparent. He has the same transparent cockpit and Energon parts that Stormcloud has, along with a head, lower legs, and lower arms that are transparent. However, his main body and helicopter tail are all a solid color. This isn't a bad thing in and of itself-- it's just going to look really out-of-place when we finally get the final part to the combiner and combine all of them, since all three of the other limbs are primarily translucent (which, in my opinion, is the primary thing that made them all so unique). The dark blue does contrast quite well with the light red (though I wish there was more of it), and the transparent red is a very nice-looking "cherry" hue. He's also got quite a few silver paint apps on his body, all of which look great and help to add a bit more variety beyond just "red and blue".
No mold changes have been made to Topspin.
Topspin's color scheme isn't bad (though the red could have been dulled a bit)-- my main complaints about him regard the fact that, firstly, when have you ever seen a blue-and-red helicopter? And, secondly, he's NOT mostly transparent like the other Club exclusive combiner limbs, which is going to make him look out-of-place when we can finally combine all of them and takes away from his uniqueness. My least favorite out of the exclusive combiner limbs.


Topspin Bio :
MOTTO: "By your will, Alpha Trion."
SPECIALITY: The ability to manipulate Transformers sparks
WEAPONS: Energon plasma weapon: Twin Blasters
UNIQUE QUALITY: Has the unique ability to move a spark from body to body and can even split a spark into two or more pieces without destroying it.
Something happened to Topspin a long time ago; something he only remembers as pain, and terror, and a landscape of twisted, shifting metal lit by the strobelight chaos of laser fire through rolling smoke. It is the last time Topspin remembers feeling anything.
Eons later he found himself drifting, alone, though the vast darkness. Lost for years, he slowly ran out of energy, and his systems shut down one by one. Eventually, his inert body plunged through a wormhole and ended up on one of the transit pads in Axiom Nexus.
Handed off by processing authorities to Alpha Trion, he awoke to find himself staring into the face of another natural leader. Never before had he met a being with such overwhelming force of personality, or such deep conviction. Here was the leader that would guide him to what he sought.
Alpha Trion saw instantly what Topspin was; after all, he'd been waiting for someone like him since arriving in Axiom Nexus. He saw in Topspin the fruition of all his grand goals, and immediately set his plans into motion. He promised Topspin all he could want and more. Telling the plain truth for perhaps the first time in decades, he told Topspin that the reward he sought for so long waited just beyond the veil of the bleed, on Alpha Trion's home world.
