Megatron [Legends] (Movie)

Allegiance : Decepticon
Size : Legend
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Moderately light milky gray and some black, light milky gray, dark red, silver, and dull metallic bronze
Rating : 7.0

Megatron's futuristic aircraft alternate mode is, surprisingly enough, the best-looking alternate mode out of any of the Movie Megatrons. There's no bunch of robot mode kibble underneath the wings or the rear section, the the wings are much better-proportioned (read: larger) than they are on the larger versions of Megatron. The robot head is still a little obvious, however, sticking up backwards from the main body of this mode, and the robot hands are rather obvious, serving as the "side nosecones" of the mode. The color scheme of gray and black is also really dull, even if the few bits of bronze and silver do spice it up a little (particularly in robot mode). The mold detailing is VERY good, however, especially on the bits that are utilized in robot mode. It never ceases to amaze me how much detail they can cram in on even these small toys...
As a side effect of having the best vehicle mode, Legends Megatron has the worst robot mode out of the Movie Megatron toys, though it's still rather good for its size. He's a bit of a hunchback, as his middle nosecone from vehicle mode just folds behind his back for this mode. His legs are his biggest weak point in this mode, though-- they're made up of the vehicle mode wings, so they look pretty flat proportionally, and the large feet look a tad dopey compared to Megatron's otherwise frightening visage. Otherwise his proportions are great for a toy of its size, though, and the paint and mold detailing ont he chest, face, and arms really make the figure in this mode. As expected for a Legend-class toy, his articulation is rather limited-- he can move at the head, shoulders, waist, and hips, but that's it. Luckily, most of these joints are ball joints, so he can move a bit more than the average toy of this size.
Legends class Megatron is one of the best Movie Legends toys, with easily the best-looking vehicle mode of all the Megatron toys and a robot mode that, although not great, isn't that bad for a toy of this size, either. If you tend to like little toys like this, then by all means purchase this sucker.
