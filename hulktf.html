Hulk (Marvel TF Crossover)

Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Dark jungle green, dark metallic bluish gray, dull metallic gold, and some black, dull dark green, and silver
Rating : 9.3

Hulk smash, but Hulk tank smash better! Hulk have to thank Iron Man and Stretchy Guy for making Hulk Tank for Hulk... Anyway, Hulk really like tank mode-- nice detailing, and looks like it make lots of booms! Panel detailing all over, especially on treads, which Hulk especially like. Even battle damage on front! Using gold and dull blue to accent Hulk's natural green also make for good color scheme, Hulk think. Hulk does have two complaints about Hulk's tank mode, though-. First, tank has no real back! Hulk can see robot waist right there, plain as day! Plus, turret can't rotate! How Hulk supposed to smash things that attack him from side!? GRAAAAAAAAAAAAAHHH!!!!
As good as Hulk think Hulk's tank mode is, Hulk's robot mode even better! Since Hulk already on subject, though, Hulk cover the few things he NOT like about robot right off bat. First, Hulk's head look like Frankenstein! Hulk not THAT old yet! Second, tread panels on bottom of arm tend to come off pegs easily, and some other tread panels on lower arms not peg into anything-- that probably Hulk's biggest thing that makes Hulk mad about Hulk tank, in fact. Other than that, though, Hulk LOVE robot mode! Good proportions, make Hulk look big and beefy with huge shoulders, like Hulk really is! Hulk also love chest design-- beefy and with rivets, Hulk really like! Hulk also not really have any big tank extras, big plus! Hulk also have really good artc-- articl-- movement. Hulk can move at neck, shoulders (at two points), elbows, wrists, at base of thumb, at two points along four fingers of each hand (which are each one piece), hips (at two points), knees (at two points), and ankles! That lot of movement, Hulk can get into lots of cool smashing poses to smash stuff in Hulk tank! Hulk can also turn at waist, but then Hulk snap back into original position, because of stupid "super punch" gimmick that not really work well.
Hulk love Hulk tank-- though not quite perfect, Hulk think it definitely best of first wave of Marvel Transformers, with really neat robot mode! Hulk-- and Beastbot-- highly recommend!
