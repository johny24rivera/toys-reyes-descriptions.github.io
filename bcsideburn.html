Side Burn (2011 Collectors' Club Exclusive)

Allegiance: Autobot
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Moderately dark glittery blue, white, transparent amber, and some black, silver, light metallic blue, yellow, and dark red
Rating : 8.8

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Classics Rodimus. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Classics Rodimus here .)

I never would have envisioned Rodimus redecoed into an updated version of Side Burn from Robots in Disguise, but for the most part it works very well. The original Side Burn was a curvy sportscar and this is a more angular one with a very Rodimus-y spoiler, but if you can ignore that, it's clear that the Collectors' Club people worked very hard to make this as Side Burn-esque as possible without remolding anything. The dark glittery blue used for Side Burn's main color is a great shade of the color, and combined with the white and black it really pops, particularly in robot mode where there's more of the secondary colors. The transparent amber goes decently enough with the blue as well, though if the light isn't that strong it can blend in with the black interior seat detailing behind the windows. Given that Side Burn is a high-end collectible toy, he's got tons of paint apps-- the light metallic blue flame paint apps on his hood and sides are practically replicas of those on the original toy, and look fantastic. Secondary details like the headlights, hubs, and hips are also painted quite well, with the taillights being the only glaring paint app missing here. I particularly love how a lot of the robot mode paint apps were specifically designed to make the Rodimus sculpt as Side Burn-like as possible; the silver-and-red-striped chest engine, the slightly asymmetrical paint apps on the arms... it all looks great, particularly how subtly the paint apps on the head make it look so obviously Side Burn's.
No mold changes have been made to Side Burn.
Side Burn is a pretty great use of the Rodimus mold, obvious spoiler aside. The color combo already looks pretty good, but when you combine that with the paint apps painstakingly applied to make this toy look like a clear update to RiD Side Burn, it makes the toy that much better. Recommended, tied with Shattered Glass Rodimus for my favorite use of the mold.


Side Burn Bio :
FUNCTION: Speedy Knight
MOTTO: "Think you can keep up? I doubt it!"
Side Burn is a born exhibitionist; if it's loud, fast, or haphazard, you can't bet that he's tried it. Then bragged about it at a local Energon pub. Upgraded with a new Spark engine, Side Burn is now topping land speeds once thought to be unreachable and still hasn't found his limit. Although he'd be more than happy on the track, watching the world streak by his windshield in an indecipherable blur, Side Burn knows that his speed will someday provide a key advantage in the war against the Decepticons. Even if Prowl and his other friends in the newly-formed Autobot military don't share his outlook.
