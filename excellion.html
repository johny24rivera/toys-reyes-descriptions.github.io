Excellion

Size : Deluxe
Homeworld : Speed Planet
Cyber Key Code : d7p5
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme : Pale red, charcoal gray, transparent blue, and some light orange, silver, moderately light glossy red, metallic bluish silver, dull metallic gold, and dull sky blue
Rating : 9.5

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the original Cybertron Hot Shot. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original Cybertron Hot Shot here .)

Excellion-- named the same as the Japanese version of Cybertron Hot Shot-- is a redeco homage to G1 Hot Rod . It's certainly fitting, given that in several aspects this figure highly resembles the G1 Hot Rod toy in ways besides the color scheme, such as the "wings" on the shoulders in the robot mode and a similar transformation. Granted, the wings are blue on Excellion instead of yellow like on Hot Rod, but Cybertron Hot Shot's wings were already yellow, so keeping them the exact same color would've been rather redundant. Otherwise, the color scheme is spot-on G1 Hot Rod, with a great mixture of red, orange, gold, and silver. The nice gold flame paint apps on the front of the vehicle mode are especially wicked-looking, and the colors go together especially well in robot mode-- I just love that shade of orange, especially. (A slightly darker, more "pure" shade of gray would've looked a little better, however.) When it comes to the red in vehicle mode, however, I do have what is my only major complaint about the colors used-- the red paint used on the side doors and part of the roof is of a decidedly lighter, brighter, and more glossier shade than the red used for the plastic parts. It's quite noticeable, and throws off the unity of the vehicle mode a tad.
No mold changes have been made to Excellion.
Excellion is an excellent redeco of Hot Shot, and quite frankly are the colors this mold should have had in the first place. I have a bit of an issue with the mismatched red colors in vehicle mode, but other that he's an A+ repaint of an A+ mold. Highly recommended, even if you already have the original Cybertron Hot Shot.


Excellion Bio :
An outsider among the Autobots because of his brooding attitude, Excellion is one of the very few Autobot warriors who the Deceptiocns think of with any respect, but that respect is earned. Excellion has made an art of speed and stealth, moving back and forth across enemy lines as if he were invisible. More than once, he has retrieved vital information from within a Decepticon stronghold, or quickly and quietly disposed of a particularly annoying enemy agent.
