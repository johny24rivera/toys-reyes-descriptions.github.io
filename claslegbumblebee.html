Bumblebee [Legend] (Classics)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Legend
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Yellow, black, and some silver, red, and dull sky blue
Rating : 5.5

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Legends of Cybertron Hot Shot. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Legends of Cybertron Hot Shot here .)

Odd that they made a Hot Shot repaint of Bumblebee considering that he got his own deluxe toy in Classics, as well.... Well, anyways, they certainly painted up the toy to look pretty spot-on Bumblebee-- a yellow and black little car has always been Bumblebee's forte, and it certainly isn't changed by this toy. The yellow is a fairly bright color, but the black keeps it from being too overpowering. Silver is used on the headlights and the robot face, but truth is it doesn't really provide enough contrast against the yellow like the black does. The dull sky blue and red paint apps really provide a nice contast to the yellow and help to vary the color scheme a bit, but they're only used on the chest in robot mode, unfortunately. And that's my biggest complaint with Legends Bumblebee-- he just doesn't have enough color variation. If he had more paint apps of the blue or red variety, he'd look considerably better, especially in vehicle mode where pretty much all you can see are yellow and black.
No mold changes have been made to Legends Bumblebee.
Legends Bumblebee is a fairly dissapointing repaint of the second-worst Legends class mold (at least as of this writing, 4/07). The actual color scheme isn't all that bad, but he badly needs more paint apps and color variation to have something else besides all that yellow and black. About on par with LoC Hot Shot in terms of recommendation, but he falls short of the LoC Excellion repaint .
