Alpha Trizer (BotCon 2014 Attendee-Only Exclusive)

Allegiance : Cybertronian Knight
Size : Voyager (comes in a 3-pack with Apelinq and Flareup )
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : White, purple, and some light milky gray, silver, moderately dark dull red, and dull periwinkle
Rating : 8.4

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Prime Thundertron. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Prime Thundertron here .)

Like the other toys in this "Knight" 3-pack, Alpha Trizer is an update/homage to a previous BotCon exclusive-- however, unlike Apelinq and Flareup, his original concept was never produced. Originally 3H-- the folks who ran BotCon before FunPub-- had a concept for redecoing Beast Machines Snarl into Alpha Trion, but it never came to fruition for various reasons. Since Alpha Trion has already had a toy made by FunPub, enter Alpha Trizer-- kinda like Alpha Trion, but different. Alpha Trizer certainly has Trion's colors-- a medium purple and white are his two main colors, and they go together extremely well, contrasting with each other quite nicely. The purple is a particularly eye-catching shade. There's also a fair amount of dull red on Alpha Trizer-- again homaging Alpha Trion, with most of the red appearing on bits of the robot legs, robot shoulders/lion claws, and a few spots on the lion and robot head. There's also some nice silver on appropriate parts like the rear claws and stripe down the sides of the mane, as well as on his face and upper robot legs. In places where silver paint wasn't used for whatever reason, a light gray plastic was used instead for a few parts like the front claws and some minor connector pieces. It's a blah gray, but at least it's not a plastic color that's used much. Last but not least, there's a bit of periwinkle used for the eyes in both modes-- a really nice shade that contrasts against the white and purple very nicely, and is also in keeping with the Cybertronian Knight emblem-- complete with wings-- on the left side of Alpha Trizer's "cape". The paint apps are also varied up considerably, with few parts on Alpha Trizer looking too "one-tone"-- the sole exception to this being the rear body of the lion mode, where there's a bit too much unpainted purple. The white sword could also have used some silver paint, as well. I do love the paint details that hearken back to Beast Machines Snarl's details, like the ovals on the upper legs and the accentuated fur on the lower arms. (And let's not forget they're both lion-formers with beards!)
No mold changes have been made to Alpha Trizer, though like most other BotCon exclusives, the electronics have been gutted from his weapon (not that the light would have made any sense on this toy anyways, with there being no transparent plastic).
Alpha Trizer is a really nice treat for long-time BotCon fans, finally bringing a great idea to fruition, yet not just slapping the "Alpha Trion" name on him now that Alpha Trion has an actual existing toy. It's a good color scheme on a decent mold, though I wish FunPub had done a bit more to differentiate the face from Thundertron's-- it's a bit weird to see the face of the Star Seeker captain in the Prime series and the leader of the Knights here have basically the same face. Recommended, as long as you're part of the somewhat niche "BotCon-only character fan" audience it's targeting compared to some other exclusives.


Alpha Trizer Bio :
FUNCTION: Knight Grand Master
MOTTO: "Understanding one's role in the grand scheme is the key to balance, harmony and transforming the future."
As the wise and mysterious spiritual leader of the Autobots and Maximals, Alpha Trizer has experienced many pivotal moments in Cybertronian history. Although he shares the wisdom that he has acquired through these experiences, he doesn't seem to share many personal details. No one seems to know his true age or origins, but it's obvious that he has seen more than your average 'bot. A source of inspiration and insight, Alpha Trizer serves as the conduit to the Oracle/Vector Sigma and a connection to the very heart of Cybertron. This connection has obviously made him the 'bot he is today, but what came before?
Alpha Trizer puts his faith in the Oracle and the Allspark, claiming that he has seen its wisdom firsthand. If there is one spark in particular that he would like this wisdom to reach, it would be Ginrai. He sees a lot of the leaders of the past (and of himself) in the unyielding Knight Commander and hopes to help bring a sense of balance to tame the lion within him. He knows that Ginrai sees his Zen-like approach as passive and naive, claiming he has no time for the old bot's riddles or waiting for the day when "all will be revealed". As the saying goes, can a cheetah change its spots? Alpha Trizer believes with all his spark that it can.
