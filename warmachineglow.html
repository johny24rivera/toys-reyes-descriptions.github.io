War Machine (Sportscar) [Marvel TF Crossover]

Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme : Black, silver, moderately light milky gray, brick red, and some moderately light red and glow-in-the-dark off-white
Rating : 9.4

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the Marvel TF Iron Man sportscar. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original Marvel TF Iron Man sportscar here .)

As you'd expect from a War Machine redeco, the color scheme for this toy is primarily black with some silver/gray highlights. The silver paint looks particularly good, and is quite striking in the double racing stripes running down the top of the vehicle mode-- they really help to add some excellent contrast to that mode. The shade of glossy gray plastic used for parts of the mech mode is pretty close to the silver in overall coloration, so it fits pretty well with the overall scheme, if not quite as well as the painted silver. That said, just those two colors would make War Machine rather monochromatic (and he does look a bit like that in mech mode); however, the brick red paint used for the windows in vehicle mode looks REALLY nice, both in terms of adding another color to the mix and in terms of how well it fits with the overall color scheme without getting gaudy. War Machine also has a bit of glow-in-the-dark off-white paint added to the figure-- on the headlights, the triangle "power reactor" on the chestpiece, and on the eyes in mech mode (the taillights are painted light red). It's not nearly as much as the other figure in this wave, but it still glows quite well and is a neat feature, even if you can't exactly do much with it. It also isn't really used enough to make it a contributing factor to the color scheme, either, but it does contrast nicely with the black and silver where it's used.
No mold changes have been made to the sportscar version of War Machine, but it does have the fixed knee-pegs that later iterations of the Iron Man sportscar mold also have.
The War Machine sportscar is an awesome redeco of one of the best Marvel TF Crossover molds. The silver is used in rather dynamic paint apps (particularly in vehicle mode), the brick red really helps to add a bit more color to him (again, particularly in vehicle mode), and the glow-in-the-dark bits are a nice touch. Highly recommended-- and recommended over every other iteration of this mold as of this writing (6/12).


War Machine (Sportscar) Bio:
Most versions of the War Machine armor aren't known for their speed, but this one makes up for it. Designed with high-speed battles in mind, this armor combines the latest in firepower with the acceleration of a top-of-the-line super car. Nothing stands in the way of War Machine when he's got the pedal to the medal!
