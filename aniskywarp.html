Skywarp (Animated)

Allegiance : Decepticon
Size : Voyager
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Charcoal black, royal purple, and some transparent orange, light pasty purple, pale light metallic purple, silver, pale metallic gunmetal gray, and dark metallic purple
Rating : 7.9

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Animated Voyager Starscream. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Animated Voyager Starscream here .)

As is expected with a Starscream repaint from any line, Skywarp here is an homage to the G1 Seeker of the same name . However, like the other Animated Starscream "clones", Skywarp's color scheme differs in some subtle ways when compared to his G1 self. Yes, his primary color scheme is still black and purple, but the silver has been all but taken out of the equation, with only a few bits of gunmetal gray on his chest and some silver on his waist. Instead, three different shades of purple have been used, and the all work marvelously well against the black. The light metallic purple is used mostly on the front of the jet/chest area, while a moderate royal purple is used for most of the robot mode connector pieces, and a dark metallic purple is used for some jet mode accents. They're all different enough from each other to justify being there, and really help add to Skywarp's overall color variety even though they're technically the same color. As is traditional, Skywarp also has an orange cockpit, and this orange cockpit translates to orange robot mode eyes and missiles as well, which is an excellent accent color and goes well against both black and purple. However, I'm not fond of the replacement of Skywarp's traditional "pure" black with a more impure, charcoal black. It doesn't look BAD per se, but a pure black would've looked a lot better and made the contrast stand out even more.
No mold changes have been made to Animated Skywarp-- which is a shame, as he really could've used a remolded expression. Starscream's smirk doesn't work well for this fraidy-cat character. There is also quite a few reports (and this includes my own) of Skywarp having exceptionally poor quality control. Not when it comes to his paint apps, mind you, but his joints-- many of them are EXTREMELY loose, particularly his ankle ball joints which pop off pretty much if you just touch them. Luckily this isn't a universal thing, but it is something to watch out for.
Animated Skywarp is a great redeco of the Starscream mold, though I would've much preferred a purer black to help bring out the contrast more. That aside, I'd still recommend it easily over the original Animated Voyager Starscream-- just be on the lookout for quality control issues.


Skywarp Character Bio :
FEARFUL SUPER PILOT
"They're headed right for us! Run!"
Skywarp wouldn't call himself a coward. He'd call himself smart. So what if he runs away from combat? Fighting is dangerous! All those missiles and lasers could really hurt someone! As a clone of Starscream, he's got all the power and skill of the leader of the Decepticon air forces, but none of the courage or ambition. He could easily be one of the most dangerous Decepticons of all time, but since he screams and runs away the second a shot is fired, he's mostly just a joke.
GALACTIC POWERS & ABILTIES:
- Gets shot in the back more than any other Decepticon
- Uses his teleportation ability to run away faster
- Even just seeing a picture of Megatron gives him nightmares
