Crosshairs [Target Exclusive]

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Scout
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Pale tan, moderately dark brown, charcoal black, transparent black, and some light electric blue, pale tannish yellow, silver, dull red-orange, and gray
Rating: 9.4

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Energon Strongarm. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Energon Strongarm here .)

The first thing that comes to mind when I look at Crosshairs is the Botcon 2005 exclusive Fallback -- the two are quite similar in their overall color schemes, even down to the light blue accents (which in Crosshairs' case, are prerequisites since he's in the "Allspark Power" part of the line). The major difference between the two is that the placement of the light tan and the dark brown are reversed on Crosshairs, with Crosshairs being overall lighter-colored than his much more expensive mold partner. Personally, of the two I like Crosshairs' color placement slightly more, though I think both look great and suitable for a jeep. The light blue accents on both toys are in roughly the same places, and contrast very well against the other colors, though Crosshairs has a little more blue than Fallback. I wish Crosshairs' charcoal black parts were a more solid black, however, as that almost always looks better in my opinion. I also don't think transparent black plastic looks all that great, so I think Fallback's cool blue-colored weapon wins out over Crosshairs' easily in that department. Crosshairs also sports a cool desert camo deco on the the front half of his vehicle mode, which is pretty well-done and fits well with the overall color scheme without making the toy seem too busy.
No mold changes have been made to Crosshairs-- in fact, this mold has been used so many times now it's starting to noticeably degrade. On my toy, at least, the pegs that hold the arms together are so shallow now they tend to come undone if I bend the arm and then straighten it out again, so that can obviously get rather annoying.
Crosshairs is a great redeco of a great mold, though I still think the Target Exclusive Strongarm released earlier in the assortment has a slightly better color scheme overall. Recommended if you don't have any other version of the mold and can't find Strongarm, though.


Crosshairs Bio :
Armorer and weapons master for the Autobots, Crosshairs is used to dealing with some of the most dangerous technology in the galaxy. Long use has taught him to be painstakingly methodical, but has also caused him to be so entrenched in his systematic organizational methods that no other Autobot could ever hope to find anything in the vast armory on Cybertron. Crosshairs is so careful that he will not issue a weapon unless the requester fills out forms detailing  in triplicate  his need.
