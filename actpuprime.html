Armor Up Optimus Prime (Activator)

Allegiance : Autobot
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme : Dark glittery blue, light red, and some black, yellow, light metallic silvery blue, metallic gunmetal gray, and light sky blue
Rating : 6.5

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the original Activator Optimus Prime. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original Activator Optimus Prime here .)

Armor Up Optimus Prime here is a repaint homage to Star Convoy, a Japanese Prime whose trademark paint app was a yellow "T"-like shape on his upper chest-- just like Armor Up Optimus Prime sports. Beyond that and the now-silver (and rather cool-looking) paint job the head has gotten, though, it's hard to tell what exactly has changed between this version and the original Activator Prime. Indeed, he is a "barely-repaint", but there's a few more changes than it would at first appear:
- The black used on the windows is shinier and now pastel like on the original release.
- The entire vehicle headlights are now yellow, as oppposd to just the round bits in the center.
- He has a silver Autobot symbol on his left shoulder.
- Both of his sirens are light metallic bluish silver, as opposed to the orignal Activator Prime's sirens, where one was light red and the other was light blue.
- This Prime's upper legs are the same blue as his lower legs, as opposed to being gray.
- The entire lower chest and hip area are painted gunmetal gray, minus the headlight yellow paint apps.
- Armor Up Optimus Prime's red is a SLIGHTLY lighter shade than the original Activator Prime's.
- The dark blue plastic on this version is the same shade, but has glittery bits in it as opposed to being a flat shade.
- Most of the connector pieces (primarily seen on the back of the robot mode) are blue, not gray.
Still, almost all of these changes are minor and make Armor Up Optimus Prime more of a variant than a bonafied actual repaint. I understand Hasbro not wanting to have Prime stray too far from his iconic colors, but this is still waaaay too similar to the original.
No mold changes have been made to Armor Up Optimus Prime.
Armor Up Optimus Prime looks SLIGHTLY better than the original overall, I think, but that doesn't change the fact that he's a "barely-repaint" who really could have used some more variance in his colors. The mold is pretty bad in vehicle mode as well, making this a hard recommendation unless you're THAT big of a Star Convoy obsessionist.


Armor Up Optimus Prime Character Bio :
In preparation for a massed attack on the Decepticons, Optimus Prime orders his Autobots to upgrade their armor. Working with Autobot Ratchet, he mounts improved armor plating on his frame, giving him the toughness he needs to withstand even the most powerful fusion blast.
