Tarantulas Transmetal (Fox Kids)

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Silver-gray, black, and chrome silver, with some red, neon orange, and neon green
Rating : 8.8

Beast mode is a transmetal tarantula. This mode is very nice, with practically no extras. The only major extra is the little "chainsaw" weapon that Tarantulas has- this is stored under his mandibles. This gets in the way a bit, and makes the middle of the tarantula head pop up sometimes. The other, less noticeable, extra is the vehicle mode wheel under the back of the abdomen. This doesn't get in the way, however, and actually complements the abdomen with its addition, looking like a "spinning spinneret". The detailing to this mode is very good, and complete with a crafted "turbo engine" on the top of the abdomen. The color scheme for this version of Tarantulas Transmetal, however, is a bit too drab in my opinion. There is not much color to this mode, or the other two modes, for that matter. It is mostly just silver, gray, and black. While the red does complement a little, it doesn't help much, and the neon green looks odd in its placement. The color scheme makes this 'bot look more like a transmetal wolf spider than a transmetal tarantula. Still, an incredible beast mode, though.
Vehicle mode is merely flipping out the wheel and chainsaw weapon under Tarantulas and pushing his spider legs up to prevent interference. While this isn't much of a vehicular mode, it still looks very nice, especially when complemented by the turbo engine. This mode is a bit unstable, however, and can't stand up very well, although the extra spider legs (which look odd in this mode) may help a little.
Robot mode is very good. The most noticeable extras in this mode are the spider legs, but this makes the mode look all the more scarier. The only other major extras are the panel that covers Tarantulas' robot head in beast mode, and the halves of the wheels from the vehicle mode. The head panel just looks odd and sticks out a bit, but the wheel halves on the shoulders make Tarantulas look a little more buff, although they are a bit wiggly. The rest of this mode is practically flawless in its construction- the legs are nice and pointy, the arms have little spider-like claws on the ends of them, and the chainsaw blaster fits perfectly in its hole in the hand. Again, however, I think the color scheme hampers the overall effect that this mode could have had. The lime green looks odd next to the grey, and the face looks like Tarantulas has a mustache.
A very nicely constructed toy, but the color scheme is slightly on the drab side, in my opinion. The original Tarantulas Transmetal is recommended over this one.
