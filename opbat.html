Beast mode is a bat. This is an extremely poor mode. Although this toy has a fierce face, its wings canÂ’t flap or move at all, and there is a lot of extras on the bottom. WIth the exception of the mouth, there are no movable parts whatsoever in this mode, and the color scheme is mostly just one color.
Robot mode looks like a warrior/knight. He has two small swords, which have some jagged holes or edges in them, put otherwise smooth. His upper arms look odd, though, considering they are on a gear that turns them, and his bat wings stick out a lot, as does his bat face on his chest. The detailing is a bit better, and the color scheme more varied in this mode.
Only worth getting if you think the coolness of the robot mode overturns the non-playability of the beast mode.
