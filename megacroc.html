Megatron (Crocodile)

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Basic
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Light green and dark green, with some gold, white, and red
Rating : 6.9

Beast mode is a crocodile. The detailing is pretty good in this mode, but the articulation is pretty limited. Only the mouth and front legs can move. Also, the robot hands are clearly visible on the back of the front feet, and the back legs are too small proportionally. One of the more serious flaws of the toy, however, is not apparent by looking at it, or even after you buy it. After a while, the tab that holds the tail in place may wear a bit, and, since the tail is what activates the spring-loaded transformation, you may eventually be stuck with Megatron in robot mode.
Robot mode is okay, but has some extras. His crocodile back is sort of large. Also, his crocodile feet stick out of his legs and make them look awkward. His tail can become a gun, though, and his face is very nicely detailed. It is a bit hard to take the tail off of his back, though- you really have to pull sometimes.
A nice start for the Beast Wars, but not the ideal toy.
