Robot mode is even worse than beast mode. His face doesnt look anything like that of Dinobots , and his arms are just little etches in the plastic on the inside of his chest plates. His dino head folds back to reveal his robot head and doesnt hide itself at all.
Very crummy-looking, even for a Happy Meal toy.
