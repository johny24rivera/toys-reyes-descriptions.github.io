Armordillo

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Basic
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Orange, dark brown, and grey, with some red
Rating : 5.8

Beast Mode is an armadillo. Although you wouldnt exactly run in fright from an armadillo, the way this guy looks you might. Almost no obvious clue this is a robot in this mode. To be blunt, however, this mode is kind of boring, since there are practically no points of articulation except for the front two legs and the end of the tail.
Robot mode is slightly disappointing, though. The transformation is simple; pull up on his tail and hes done. Your baby brother could figure this one out. The shell in the back is quite large proportionally, and it gets in the way some. The guns are sort of corny. Theyre just feet with a chainball or laser attached to them.
All in all, a good transformer, but not the best.


Armordillo Bio :
FUNCTION: Desert Combat
To reduce thermal energy intake, infrared vision allows Armordillo to battle at night, when the desert battlefield has cooled. His back bears the burden of a built-in suit of armor that is virtually impenetrable. Enemy artillery bounces off like pebbles, while his own hidden laser weapon causes devastating damage to Predacon attackers.
