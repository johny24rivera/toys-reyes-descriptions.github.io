Bumblebee w/ Sam Witwicky (Human Alliance; DotM)

Allegiance : Autobot
Difficulty of Transformation : Hard
Color Scheme : Black, "cheese" yellow, light milky gray, dull greenish gray, moderately dark blue, and some silver, white, dull metallic bronze, and light red
Rating : 9.7

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the original RotF Human Alliance Bumblebee toy. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original RotF Human Alliance Bumblebee toy here .)

For the second outing of this mold for the Dark of the Moon line, Bumblebee gets his typical "stealth" reverse color scheme that his repaints have often been lately. Black is now his main color in vehicle mode, with his stripes now being yellow. In vehicle mode he looks quite nice, though to differentiate them a bit more I wish his stripes weren't of the EXACT same style as the first usage of this toy's. What really makes the mode pop, though, isn't the black or yellow stripes, but rather the non-translucent blue used for the windows, which really contrasts well with all of the other colors. Granted, the fact that it's NOT translucent plastic means that you can't see the Sam Witwicky mini-figure inside Bumblebee-- and that Bumblebee's eyes don't shine with light piping in robot mode-- but considering how well it looks, I really don't mind. The silver paint apps on the wheel hubs, stripes, tailpipes, and parts of the robot mode all also really look swell. The one thing I don't like about this color scheme, though, is how prevalent the orange-yellow is in robot mode, along with all that dull milky light gray. Neither color goes all that well with the other, and all that yellow in robot mode makes Bumblebee look a little too similar to how he looked originally-- not to mention, if he still looks about half (or more) yellow in robot mode, how is that "stealth"? A lot of paint apps are used up to make Bumblebee more yellow in robot mode as well, whereas he really could've used some more silver paint detailing on some parts like his shoulders, instead.
No mold changes have been made to Human Alliance Bumblebee (or Sam Witwicky).
DotM Human Alliance "Stealth" Bumblebee has some excellent color placement and paint apps in vehicle mode, but unfortunately in robot mode he loses a bit too much of the black for a shade of yellow that doesn't look all that great to begin with. Recommended over the original RotF Human Alliance Bumblebee, primarily because of how great the alt mode looks.
