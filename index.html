Model Commentary
On all Toy Reviews, Beastbot will give an overall rating to each toy. The rating system is based on a 1.0 - 10.0 system, 1.0 being absolutely awful, 10.0 being perfect in every way. Note that it is next to impossible for a transformer to recieve a 10.0 rating; thus, any rating over 9.0 is extremely good.

Any reviews that have an asterisk after them also have a "second opinion" review, written by VBBN.

Unless otherwise stated, all pictures without the white (or yellow) wall & posterboard background (which were taken by me) are courtesy of bwtf.com.
