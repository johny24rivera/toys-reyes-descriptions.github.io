Elita-1 (Autobot Alliance)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Black, silver, and some pale light metallic pinkish purple, light gray, white, and transparent dark blue
Rating : 6.7

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Revenge of the Fallen Chromia. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Revenge of the Fallen Chromia here .)

At long last, the last of the three biker sisters from Revenge of the Fallen gets a toy! Only... it's just a repaint of Chromia. Although they were both motorcycles, Elita-1 was most definitely an entirely different design from Chromia in the movie, and it really irks me that she's relegated to a repaint when she had a different design commissioned. Putting that aside-- well, even the COLORS are wrong. Elita-1's design in the movie was mostly purple-- this one clearly isn't. Again, really bad move. Beyond that fact that she has the same face as the other Biker Sisters (like she's supposed to), an unusual robot body structure, and that she's a motorcycle, there isn't anything this toy has in common with the Elita-1 movie design. It's a shame, because it really colors my appreciation of the toy. I mean, the color scheme itself is fairly nice-- the black/silver/light gray color combo is a bit overdone, but the really nice pale metallic pinkish purple contrasts extremely well with the other colors. It really makes the color scheme, in my opinion, which is why I wish there was more of it-- particularly near the back half of the vehicle mode or below the waist in robot mode. From some angles, it's still a bit too "two-tone" with the black and silver/gray being a little too dominant. The transparent dark blue plastic also helps to give the scheme a bit more color, though it's used so rarely it really doesn't mattter much in the grand (color) scheme of things. Elita-1 has a couple of nice silver "Cyberglyphic" paint apps that help at least a little to match her up with her sisters, but otherwise there really isnt any purely decorative paint apps in terms of those that aren't pinkish purple. (One nice little nod is that her license plate reads "3L 174", a "133t" play on Elita's name.)
No mold changes have been made to Elita-1.
Autobot Alliance Elita-1 is a decent enough repaint-- roughly on par with this mold's previous usage as Chromia, just judging the mold and color scheme by itself. Unfortunately, it completely falls flat at representing the character it's supposed to, being a repaint of another character when it was supposed to be a completely original design, and has the wrong colors compared to the movie model to boot. Such a wasted opportunity...


Elita-1 Bio :
Though she is a sister to Arcee, Elita-1 has more in common with Sideswipe. She and the swordsman team up whenever possible-- especially if it means they can get away somewhere nice to do a little Decepticon hunting. This time, the two Autobots have found themselves up against Axor, who should present just enough of a challenge to make things interesting.
