Skywarp (Universe)

Allegiance : Decepticon
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Hard
Color Scheme : Black, moderately light purple, gray-white, silver, and some transparent light red, orange, yellow, deep metallic violet, dark gray, and white
Rating : 9.4

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Beast Machines Deluxe Jetstorm. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Beast Machines Deluxe Jetstorm here .)

The fifth (!) iteration of the Beast Machines Deluxe Jetstorm mold, Skywarp, is most decidedly the best. For one, he doesn't have the "sticky, flimsy arms" problem that Storm Jet and Jhiaxus had; and secondly, his paint job just freaking rocks. Black and purple have always been a favorite color scheme of mine, and Hasbro just made it even more awesome with all these added paint apps. The gray, used as a secondary color as it is, mixes well with the black and purple; the silver just rocks all around; and the orange makes a VERY nice contrasting color in the back, while keeping this toy from being "just another black repaint". There's also a few colors- white, yellow- and dark gray- that are used on just one or two places, giving you the impression that extra care was put into this repaint to give it all that more pizazz. (Although yes, Skywarp's spark crystal just has a Decepticon symbol painted over it, like the other Universe figures.) I have no complaints whatsoever about the paint job- it's just that good, and a great homage to G1 Skywarp .
No mold changes have been made to Universe Skywarp.
Universe Skywarp is a great mold with an outstanding paint job. Although the mold does need to retire after this toy- after all, this is its fifth color scheme- it does make a great Skywarp. Mildly recommended if you have a previous version of this mold; otherwise, highly recommended.
