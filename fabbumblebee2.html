Rally Rocket Bumblebee (Fast Action Battler)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation: Easy
Color Scheme : Yellow, black, dull milky bluish green, and some silver, light gray, dull light blue, metallic bluish green, light red, and metallic silvery blue
Rating : 7.7

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Plasma Punch Bumblebee. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Plasma Punch Bumblebee here .)

Out of all the FAB repaints, Rally Rocket Bumblebee is definitely the least "repainted". Yellow is still is primary color, though it's a noticeably lighter, more primary shade of yellow than the orangish yellow used on Plasma Punch Bumblebee. There's more black to offset the brighter color, though, particularly in vehicle mode. The black wide stripe down the top of Bumblebee looks particularly cool, and in keeping with his new racing-themed deco, this version of Bumblebee has really cool back-to-back "BB"s on his hood and on his car doors. It's a really slick logo, and quite creative as well. In robot mode, Bumblebee's secondary colors are two different shades of blue, one being pale light and the other having a greenish tint. It's not something I would've thought went well with yellow, but in these small amounts it works surprisingly well while still letting Bumblebee keep his characteristic "yellow and black" color scheme. One of the few things I do not like about this new scheme, though, are the yellow tires. I mean... yellow tires? Seriously? His missile is also colored a dull light blue, which just doesn't cut the mustard as a decent color for a projectile. It should have been transparent, or something.
No mold changes have been made to Rally Rocket Bumblebee.
Rally Rocket Bumblebee is an ever-so-slight improvement over the first use of the mold, but his color scheme is overall so similar to Plasma Punch Bumblebee's I wouldn't bother with this version if you've already got the original unless you're either a completist or a huge Bumblebee fan. Rally Rocket Bumblebee's color scheme, though using a bit lighter shade of yellow, overall is more varied and the colors just mesh slightly better.


Rally Rocket Bumblebee Bio :
With most of the Decepticons taken care of, Bumblebee is ready to enjoy himself. The best way he can think of to do that is to show his human friends a thing or two on the race track. With his engine tuned and his new paint sparkling in the sun, he's ready for anything the other racers can think of. And if the Decepticons decide to attack, well, he's keep his plasma cannon polished just for them.
