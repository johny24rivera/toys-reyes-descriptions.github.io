Grimlock (Shadow Armor) [Tiny Turbo Changer]

In Series : 1
Identification Letter on Package: B
Allegiance : Autobot
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Black, dark purple, and some red and moderately light blue
Rating : 5.5

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the original TLK Tiny Turbo Changer Grimlock toy. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original TLK TTT Grimlock toy here .)

Shadow Armor Grimlock is basically Grimlock in what have traditionally been "evil clone"-ish colors for Transformers-- i.e., he's mostly black, particularly in beast mode, where he has no other paint apps or other colors beyond just red eyes (which is a shame, given all the molded-in details that are kind of washed out by the dark plastic color). In robot mode, however, his colors get more interesting, with some dark purple coloration along the line down his forehead crest, on his faceplate, and on his chest. I do wish the purple was just a titch lighter to contrast against the black better, but it still looks great. The moderately light blue eyes are also cool-looking, though they aren't teal like your typical "Nemesis" colors. Still, keeping in mind the budget/limitations of the Tiny Turbo Changer pricepoint, this is a pretty nice redeco.
No mold changes have been made to Shadow Armor Grimlock.

Review by Beastbot
