Galvatron (Cybertron)

Allegiance : Decepticon
Size : Leader (Super)
Homeworld : Giant Planet
Cyber Key Code : 165x
Difficulty of Transformation : Hard
Color Scheme : Black, very shiny metallic silver, light milky gray, and some light red, metallic violet, silver, metallic bronze, and transparent reddish orange
Rating : 9.3

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Cybertron Megatron. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Cybertron Megatron here .)

Cybertron Galvatron, especially when compared to Cybertron Megatron, is definitely one of the best improvements in a paint job a toy has EVER gotten. The silver used on most of Galvatron isn't actually chrome (so you don't have to worry about it chipping), but it gives off a very, VERY cool shiny metallic sheen. I've never seen a color this shiny used on a Transformer before that wasn't chrome. Gray is used on parts that undergo a lot of scraping together during transformation or in movement in either mode, so that the shiny metallic isn't scratched to make it look worse. I'm not sure why the front of the head is shiny silver while the back half is suddenly gray, but that's the only place where those two colors don't quite fit together. The black and red, when combined with all the silver, make Galvatron look like he's positively dripping with evil. Many of the accent colors fit in perfectly as well- the purple face is very Decepticon-y, and the metallic bronze goes very well with the silver. The red plastic could maybe stand to be just a shade darker, but other than that no complaints whatsoever with his color layout. I especially like the new paint app on the cockpit seat-- painting over part of the window makes the window almost look like a narrowed eye, VERY cool.
No mold changes have been made to Cybertron Galvatron.
Galvatron is an absolutely incredible repaint, I can't possibly see this mold getting any cooler. The color scheme quality gap between this toy and Megatron couldn't be greater. Highly recommended.


Galvatron Bio :
The rage Megatron feels after his defeat by Metroplex activates the dormant power within his Unicron armor. This vast energy draws power directly from the Giant Planet itself, feeding it into Megatron and changing him into the mighty Galvatron! A living engine of destruction, Galvatron lives only to conquer all and grind the Universe beneath his armored heel. His weapons and intellect super-charged by the living energy of the Giant Planet, there are none powerful enough to stand in his way!
