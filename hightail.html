Hightail

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Scout (Basic)
Homeworld : Earth
Cyber Key Code : s7n5
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme: Moderately light gray, black, dull slightly orangish red, and some silver, bright safety orange, yellow, and white
Rating : 6.4

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Lugnutz. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Lugnutz here .)

Hightail is a repaint of Lugnutz, but he's a "repaint" only in the sense that Galaxy Force Optimus Prime is as well. Namely, a few color swaps and a few paint apps have been switched around some, but it still looks really similar to the mold's original color scheme. The black and gray of Lugnutz are pretty much the same on Hightail, with maybe just shade or so difference. The main change is that the light orange on Lugnutz has been replaced with a dull red on Hightail that has just the slightest hint of orange in it. Needless to say, though it's a noticeable color change, it's not ENOUGH of one for a repaint. The spiffy gold flames on Lugnutz have been replaced with a rather dull curvy silver line on the robot chest, which isn't nearly as cool, if you ask me. A few other, more minor paint app changes have been made, such as the new white paint apps on the waist, but these are so minor as to not be very noticeable unless you compare the two toys side-by-side. One really cool thing about Hightail, though, is that he's the first Transformer (at least to my knowledge) to have his colors done after a sports team. His license plate says it's from Cincinnati, Ohio, and the license plate letters say "B-CAT 1". This is a nifty homage to the Cincinnati Bobcats, who have the same team colors as Hightail-- more than a coincidence, certainly, and pretty cool too.
No mold changes have been made to Hightail. However, the problem the back half had clipping to his front half in robot mode has been solved in this iteration of the mold.
Hightail is a lazy repaint of one of the line's worst molds-- there's really not much to recommend, especially since one of Lugnutz' few cool things about his color scheme, the golden flames, have been removed for a plain silver curvy line. Unless you're a big Bobcats fan, you probably should skip this one.


Hightail Bio :
Freedom. That's why Hightail came to Earth. Sure he's an Autobot, but that doesn't mean he has to follow Optimus Prime around like a puppy. Cybertron was just too small for him is all, and the wide-open spaces and long-straight stretches of pure blacktop on Earth are the perfect antidote. Hightail is happy to mind his own business, cruising the back roads and byways of America, looking for the next filling station where he can recharge and kick back with a cold can of oil. And if he meets any Decepticons along the way, well, he'll send'em crying back to Galvatron.
