Cheetor (Transmetal 2)

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Yellow, chrome purple, and grey, with touches of red
Rating : 7.9

Beast Mode is a seriously mutated cheetah. Although not as sleek as Cheetor Transmetal , he gave up that for a meaner appearance. He looks almost rabid, with pieces of metal sticking out of his fur here and there, a hunch on his back, with a very long tail and an extremely thin waist. He has a missle launcher, though, and is one of the few Beast Wars Transformers that can fire its weapons while in Beast mode.
His robot mode is somewhat disappointing, though. Its legs cannot straighten out much, and there is a noticeable gap between the back of his robot head and the starting of his "hunchback". This mode is very mean-looking and I just cant picture Cheetors voice coming out of it. The detailing is very nice, though. The spark orb is located under a panel on his left leg.
A basically good toy, although the robot mode could have been better.

FUNCTION: Robotic Jungle Patrol

Cheetor is experiencing some unusual growing pains. The lines between beast and robot have blurred, creating a new Cheetor even faster and more ferocious than ever before. But he must learn to control his new capabilities - instead of being controlled by them. Outfitted with a new high-powered missile launcher and leap-thruster, the new ultra-strong and somewhat unpredictable Cheetor strikes fear into the circuits of all Predacons he encounters.
