Elita-One (Movie) [Target Exclusive]

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Scout
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme : Charcoal black, glossy metallic pinkish bronze, light milky gray, and some silver and dark blue
Rating : 8.5

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Energon Arcee. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Energon Arcee here .)

I think the Energon Arcee mold has been repainted as an homage to pretty much every G1 female, now... Still, no matter how many times they repaint this figure, they always make it different enough from the others where each one is easily discernible from the other, and Elita-One here is no exception. Instead of making Elita-One pink and white like her G1 colors, they decided to make her vaguely reminiscent of her G1 self, making her a pinkish bronze and light gray-- a very good choice, as it makes her different enough from G1 Arcee's colors yet still close enough to her original colors to make you go "Oh, yeah I see it now" if you're told it's Elita-One. The colors themselves go very well together, though the light gray admittedly looks rather dull compared to the silver and metallic pinkish bronze. There's not too much of the light gray to make Elita-One look anywhere near dull, though, so no problems there. The silver doesn't contrast well on the light gray, however. It does look REALLY good against the pinkish bronze, though, especially the silver "Z" stripes on the upper legs. Elita-One's transparent Energon parts made been molded in a charcoal black-- they go quite well with the other colors, but a pure black would have looked a little better, I think.
No mold changes have been made to Elita-One.
Elita-One is one of the slightly weaker repaints of the Energon Arcee mold overall, but that's more of a testament to how well this toy's color schemes have been than Elita-One's actual color scheme itself. If you find the blue Arcee movie repaint of this mold I'd pick that one up instead, though it IS nice to finally have a toy of Elita-One after all these years, even if it is just a repaint.


Elita-One Bio :
As the first of the female Transformers robots, Elita-One gets a lot of attention, and loves every minute of it. It isn't just her incredible handling, or her fast idle that gets the attention either. She's a cunning fighter, and one of the best marksmen among the Autobots, able to blast Scraplets off a Decepticon at more than four miles. At short range, she's just as effective, generating an attraction field that can cause unshielded robots to overheat almost instantly.
