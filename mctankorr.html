Tankorr (McDonald's)

Allegiance : Vehicon
Size : Happy Meal Size
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Light black, light blue-white, light gray, dark sparkly blue, and some light red, translucent red, yellow, and silver
Rating : 4.0

Vehicle mode is a cybertronian tank. This mode is pretty nice and very show-accurate, especially for a Happy Meal toy. The only rather odd parts about this mode is that, for one, the large cannon is connected to Tankorr's head, not his shoulders, so he can't move his head while keeping his cannon straight. Secondly, Tankorr's robot arms are rather visible from the middle of his back tank treads, as they're a different color then the surrounding plastic. Other than that, though, this mode is well-done. There is plently of detailing as well, both mold-wise and paint-wise. There are all sorts of mechanical details etched into the chest and the sides of the treads, and this toy has quite an array of colors for a Happy Meal toy. Tankorr's "sunglasses" are painted quite well in this mode. Tankorr's allegiance symbol is visible on a small transparent red orb in the middle of his chest.
Although the transformation to robot mode is very simple, it is rather effective and show-accurate anyway. Even though this mode isn't quite as good as the vehicle mode, it's still rather nice, considering the size of the toy. Tankorr's feet are both simple and effective, although a bit small; basically, you just fold them into two halves so that they resemble short legs and feet. His chest is a bit large proportionally in this mode, however, and his arms cannot extend all the way out. Small tabs only allow the lower arms to come out halfway, which is rather odd- there is no need for those tabs at all, and it would have made this mode a bit better to do without them. There is also a telltale gap in the chest where a rod connected to Tankorr's neck pivots during transformation. It's rather detracting from the mode, and makes it more "toy-like". Still, the detailing and paint detailing are still quite nice in this mode, as they were in the vehicle mode.
A pretty-well done Happy Meal toy; but again, it IS just a Happy Meal toy, and as such, it's small and has limited playability. Still, this toy is my favorite of the Beast Machines Happy Meal bunch, and I recommend it if you enjoy collecting the little buggers.... and if you like McDonald's food, of course.
