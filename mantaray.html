Manta Ray

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Happy Meal Size
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Dark blue, light blue, mustard yellow, and some light brown
Rating : 2.7

Beast mode is a manta ray, obviously. One of the first things you notice when you look at Manta Ray (He has no real name; therefore, he is referred to as Manta Ray) is that his robot legs stick out and are very noticeable in this beast mode, even from a view directly above. This was very poorly done. Also, there are no points of articulation (with the exception of those used for transformation) in this mode. Although they could of made his manta ray top look cool, they made it look very dopey, with huge, emotionless eyes and rounded fins.
The transformation to robot mode is ridiculously easy. Just flip his head back and turn him around. Even though the idea behind transforming Happy Meal Transformers, which are made for little kids, is not to make it too hard, this toy carried that point too far. In robot mode, he looks a little better, but not much. His rounded, dopey-looking fins still stick out, and his chest looks a bit too large for this mode. His arms are decent looking, though, along with his legs.
Very crummy in both modes- they could have made this robot look a lot better than they did.
