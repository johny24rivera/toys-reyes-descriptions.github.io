Megatron [Beast Machines] (McDonald's)

Allegiance : Vehicon
Size : Happy Meal Size
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Transparent blue, red, light teal, and some white and red-purple
Rating : 1.4

Beast mode is a dragon. This mode looks pretty crummy, all things considered. For one, Megatron HAS NO ARMS! Not even any detailed into the plastic "wings" behind his main body! Rather poor planning in that part. His "wings" aren't even shaped like wings, they're just the halves of his cloak of his robot mode with wings painted on the inside. Megatron's head is also colored very oddly in this mode as well; that light teal is just nauseating when connected directly to the white and red. It's very out of place. The detailing on this toy is rather nice, however.
Robot mode isn't really a robot mode at all. It's just a half-cloak with Megatron's head sticking out of it. No moving parts at all in this mode, which kinda ruins the fun of it- not even arms on the cloak like there was in the show! There are little arms molded to the sides of the cloak, though. The head might have actually been menacing, if it wasn't for the fact that the dragon chin and mouth sticks out from the top of it, making Megatron look like a "conehead". Very bad indeed.
A pretty bad toy. I'd think twice about getting it even if you ARE a completist.
