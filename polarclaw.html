Polar Claw

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Mega
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : White, black, and red, with some yellow and dark blue
Rating : 7.2

Beast mode is a polar bear. This mode is nice, but looks small for a mega and has a lot of extras on his bottom side, making this bear look extremely fat. The detailing is very good, though.
Robot mode is very nice in the middle and bottom, but the top part isn't all that good. His front polar bear legs swing over his shoulders to form a bat launcher, which launches a small plastic cyberbat. The spring does wear out after a while, so it may not stay on after a while. His other leg opens to reveal a sort of gun-like structure; this looks extremely weird though. His chest, legs, and arms are nice and well-crafted, though, with a little bit of polar bear flesh on the underside of his fists. His face has two different faces, a regular one and a mutant one.
A nice Beast Warrior, but looks kind of odd on the top in robot mode.


Polar Claw Bio :
FUNCTION: Infantry Battle Commander
A foot soldier with a hunter's instinct, this ballistic bear is equipped to command front-line infantry battles against the evil Predacons. Along with his savage teeth and ferocious fangs, his most devastating weapons include a quick-firing robotic bat, which features sonar sensors to detect enemies and alert Polar Claw for combat at all times--and at all costs!
