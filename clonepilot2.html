Clone Pilot (Remold)

Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Black, off-white, dark greenish blue, and some red, clear plastic, silver, and pale yellow
Rating : 8.5

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the original SWTF Clone Pilot/Trooper. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original SWTF Clone Pilot/Trooper here .)

The remold of the Clone Pilot/Trooper mold is, quite honestly, the first Star Wars Transformer that will really catch your eye right on the aisle pegs. All of the other Star Wars Transformers have had fairly dull color schemes, necessitated by the need to be accurate to their movie colors. It's obvious they had fun with this one-- the vehicle mode has been painted to look like a tiger shark! A typical military style "shark face" has been painted on the front, while dark greenish blue tiger shark "stripes" and "gills" have been painted all over the wings and the top of the main body of the vehicle mode. The off-white is still the dominant color on the main body, just as it was on the original use of this mold, but there's enough stylized color variation to make up for this. The black and bits of red also contrast really well with the off-white and dark blue-green, as well.
There are two mold changes made to this version of the Clone Pilot mold-- the most noticeable is that the Mech mode head has been changed to resemble a Clone Commander more than a Clone Pilot (or so I've been told, at least)-- and, an increase in the "rank" of the toy aside, I think that head design looks better than the one on the first Clone Pilot. Also, the miniature figure that fits inside the cockpit has been appropriately changed to a Clone Commander figure, as well.
This new version of the Clone Pilot mold has a really snazzy color scheme that catches your eye very quickly among the other Star Wars TFs. The remolds also make the mold look a little better in robot mode, as well. Although it's not for everyone-- some might prefer a more realistic color scheme on a Star Wars vehicle-- I think this one surpasses the original release of the mold in everyway-- one of the best Star Wars Transformers $15 toys, in my opinion.


Clone Pilot (ARC-170 Remold) Bio :
This skilled pilot is part of the Republic's most elite military force. Using a cutting-edge morphing technology developed during the Clone Wars, he is able to engage his enemies on the ground, then quickly morph into ARC-170 mode and bring the battle into space.
