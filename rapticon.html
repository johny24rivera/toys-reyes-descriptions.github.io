Rapticon

Allegiance : Maximal (Dinobot)
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Dark blue, silver, pale sea green, chrome oily black, dark green, and some neon green
Rating : 9.5

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Dinobot Transmetal 2. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Dinobot Transmetal 2 here .)

Beast mode is a transmetal velociraptor. I can not stress how cool this color scheme looks on this beast mode; even though the color scheme on the original Transmetal 2 Dinobot was cool, this is much better. This mode looks much more like a killing machine than a heroic Maximal. I especially like the transmetal "oily" black, as it makes it look like it has a thin coat of oil over these parts. This mode does suffer from the same flaws that the original Transmetal 2 Dinobot had, however.
Robot mode is equally fearsome. Here, a couple things seem a bit different from Dinobot Transmetal 2. These things can be noticed from the beast mode, but it's easier to notice them in this one. For one, the joints are tighter (although this may just be my toy), and two, the Predacon spark crystal has been replaced by a dark purple Dinobot one. The picture on the box shows him in robot mode holding his tail as a weapon, but this seems to large for a weapon, and doesn't fit in his hand very well anyway.
A great repaint; in my opinion, one of the best repaints ever. Highly recommended.


Rapticon Bio :
FUNCTION: Guerilla Combat Specialist
MOTTO: "I have no true enemies, because no one will stand against me!"
Size, strength, speed and superior intelligence are all trademarks of Rapticon. As fierce and cutthroat in battle as he is with insults and one-liners, but Vehicons would much rather be on the receiving end of his verbal assaults than his physical ones. Reinforced tail can smash Vehicon weapons and bodies to pieces almost instantly. Enhanced vision allows him to eye the enemy from massive distances and immediately identify weak spots in their formations. Sonic roar can cause permanent damage to Vehicon radars and sensors. Devises and executes ambushes and raids with unmatched precision and success.
