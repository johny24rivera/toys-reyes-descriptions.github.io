Quickstrike (Fuzor)

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Basic
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme : Transparent yellow and brown, with spots of Light Blue and Red
Rating : 7.5

In beast mode, Quickstrike is a scorpion with a snake for a tail. Extremely cool-looking, with no obvious giveaways thats its a robot. Snake tail is extremely articuilated, with claws that snap together well. The color combination is nice, as is the detailing.
In robot mode, his claws become his feet, his right arm is his snake tail, and his left arm is his scorpion legs fitted together into a sort of giant claw. Robot head looks sort of like a gas mask with optics. (Thats the equivilent of eyes in humans.) The snake arm doesn't really look like anything but an extremely long, freaky arm. His other arm looks like extremely long fingers, too delicate to grab anything. WIth the exception of the arms, though, this mode is perfect.
All in all, a little freaky looking, but fuzors are supposed to be. A little poor in the arms department, though.
