Cliffjumper (Cyber Slammer)

Allegiance : Autobot
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Light red, silver, and some black, dark metallic blue, and charcoal black
Rating : 4.9

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the Cyber Slammer '08 Bumblebee toy. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the Cyber Slammer '08 Bumblebee toy here .)

Based on the mainline deluxe repaint of the same name, Cyber Slammer Cliffjumper follows the same general color scheme, right down to the black-edged silver stripes on the front of the car mode and the "charcoal" off-black used for the black paint instead of the "purer" black paint that is used on either of the Cyber Slammer Bumblebees. The metallic blue paint used for Cliffjumper's windows is noticeably darker than that used for Cyber Slammer '08 Bumblebee, which works quite well against Cliffjumper's pretty light shade of red. (In fact, Cliffjumper's red really could stand to be a few shades darker on this toy, all things considered, since it's so much more prevalent here than on the mainline toy.) There are a few noticeable discrepancies in the color layout between the mainline release of Cliffjumper and this Cyber Slammer version, however. For one, his lower arms are silver on the Cyber Slammer toy (which, in my opinion, looks better), as opposed to having charcoal black arms like the mainline release. Cliffjumper's stomach is also red with a bit of silver, as opposed to charcoal gray with blue (and in this case, I think the mainline release wins). Cliffjumper's dark colors really could've been a bit more prevalent, overall-- especially on his robot parts and on his back end, but he still looks rather cool regardless. (Oh, and the paint apps on his face still make him look rather ugly, "cute" Cyber Slammer or not, in my opinion.)
No mold changes have been made to Cyber Slammer Cliffjumper.
I think Cyber Slammer Cliffjumper looks better than Cyber Slammer '08 Bumblebee-- primarily because I'm not a big fan of the color yellow-- but Cliffjumper really could've used more of that charcoal gray to help offset all the light red. Recommended slightly over Cyber Slammer '08 Bumblebee, but I don't think Cyber Slammer Cliffjumper's as much an improvement color-wise as the mainline release is over the mainline '08 Bumblebee toy.
