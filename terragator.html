Terragator

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Basic
Difficulty of Transformation : Hard
Color Scheme : Dark green, dark blue, white, tan, whitish green, and some brown, red, and orange
Rating : 7.5

Beast mode is a crocodile with a shell on its back. This mode looks nice on the top, but doesnt stay together very well at all, especially his shell and tail. You can pull on his tail to make his mouth move up and down, but his tail comes off a lot when you do this. Nice detailing and paint job, though.
Robot mode is a very nice mode, with few extras. The only extra is his crocodile face and head on his right shoulder, which makes him look rather lop-sided. He has two weapons; he has a large shell shield that can cover over half of his body, and his crocodile tail and back feet flip out and upside down to become a rifle. His main body is well-crafted with no extras, and his face is extremely organic-looking.
A nice robot, although he doesnt stay together well in beast mode.


Terragator Bio :
FUNCTION: Swamp Warrior, Amphibious Assault
Most Transformers find that smelly, mucky environments clog up their circuitry. Not the half-croc, half-turtle Terragator. He prefers it. Terragator is most at home in the swamp lands and sewers. Part of the Predacon elite amphibious warriors, his specialty is land/water assault. In robot mode he wields an impenetrable shield, and his hand reveals an awesome weapon.
