Autobot Blades (Voyager) [Universe 2.0]

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Voyager
Series : Classic/Generation One
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Black, slightly dull red, and some transparent blue, white, and dark metallic blue
Rating : 9.5

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Cybertron Voyager Evac. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Cybertron Voyager Evac here .)

Blades here is an homage to the G1 Protectobot Blades, also a rescue helicopter who could combine with the other Protectobots to form Defensor. Obviously since this is just a repaint of a Cybertron toy, Universe 2.0 Blades can't combine, but he's an homage nonetheless. However, he's a bit of a re-done homage-- in fact, the only reason I'm sure he IS an homage to the G1 character and not just a name re-use is because of the bio. Blades has the same general colors as his G1 counterpart-- red, white, and black-- but they're in completely different amounts. Red is now the dominant color on Universe 2.0 Blades, as opposed to white, which is just an accent color. Black is also another main color, even though it wasn't used all that much on G1 Blades. Still, I think the changes are for the better-- the amounts of red and black go together very nicely on this toy, and the white stripes and paint apps work very well as accent colors, giving it a bit more of a "rescue" feel. Transparent blue windows and optics are also a nice touch and provide some great contrast against the red. There's also a nifty "Autobot Coastal Rescue" logo on both sides of the tail, which is a pretty cool detail. About the only real change I would've made is putting a bit more of the dark metallic blue paint on the figure-- it's used very sparingly on the chest and tail, but it's a really eye-catching color that should have been used in larger amounts, particularly on the vehicle mode.
No mold changes have been made to Universe 2.0 Voyager Blades.
Universe 2.0 Voyager Blades is a very dynamic-looking repaint of one of the best helicopter TF molds of all times, and even though it's not a slavish homage, it's a fairly obscure G1 homage nonetheless. What's not to recommend?


Autobot Blades Bio :
Autobot Blades carries powerful wire-guided missiles and a photon laser that can bore a hole through Decepticon armor at 200 yards, but he rarely uses either. He considers ranged combat to be unfair, which is funny because up close he's just about the dirtiest fighter there is. He'll use his rotors to kick dust and debris into his opponent's optics, then kick the poor guy right in the exhaust port, and keep on kicking him when he falls down. It's not that Autobot Blades likes to fight; it's just that he really, really likes to win.
