Scourge

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Chrome light orange, orange-red, purple, transparent green, black, and some light green
Rating : 7.9

Beast mode is a Transmetal 2 locust. This mode is very nicely done. The detailing is absolutely fantasic. One of his eyes is a regular locust eye, but the other one is more robotic, with several bumps and a robotic eye in the center. Lift up this robotic eye to see his Predacon allegiance orb. This theme continues all along the body, with one side or leg being being robotic, and the other more organic. There are details of wires and metal everywhere, especially on his abdomen. The over all look is nice, a very skinny and sharp look to it. It can stand on its insect legs, an ability that very few other insect Transformers can do. The only big problem with this mode is the wings- there's a button that you can push to make the wings flap up and down, and would be a nice effect if the button wasn't right in the path of the wings. Thus, they usually hit your hand then you press the button. One of the wings can fire a missle, but the other wing can't, which is odd.
Robot mode is not as good as the beast mode, but is still okay. It is very odd-looking, to say the least. His huge, backwards-bending legs make it had for him to stand up in this mode, and you can't really visualize him walking like anything more than a chicken. His main body is skinny, and small for a deluxe. One of his hands if nicely crafted and is no problem, but the other has almost all the extras on it, including four of the insect legs and the insect head! This makes this arm very hard to move with out one of these parts getting in the way. His two halves of his insect abdomen and his wings are attached to his back, but this doesn't get in the way often. The missles can be taken out and be put into his right hand to be used as swords.
A very nicely crafted Transformer in beast mode, but odd-looking in robot mode, with a lot of extras.


Scourge Bio :
FUNCTION: Special Operations Combatant
MOTTO: "A plague upon all their houses!"
Scourge is possibly the most despicable of the already loathsome Predacons. Delights in taking depravity to all-time-low levels. Obsessed with germ-warfare and lethal chemical development. Despised by peers for his inclination to test deadly viral and bacterial concoctions on fellow Predacons. Skilled in combat. Scourge wields proton cluster cannons with chemical warhead capabilites. Has electrostatic disruptors inside antennae; capable of leaping distances exceeding two miles with use of wings.
