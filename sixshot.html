Six Shot (Energon)

Allegiance : Decepticon
Size : Mega
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme : Dull mustard yellow, very dark purple, charcoal black, transparent light red, and some metallic purple, metallic cherry red, chrome red, and metallic bronze
Powerlinx ports : 1
Rating : 7.7

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Energon Shockblast. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Energon Shockblast here .)

Six Shot is certainly an interesting redeco of Shockblast. Yellow, purple, red, and black, in this combination isn't particularly my idea of a great color scheme for a transformer. However... I don't think it's nearly as bad as many people make it out to be. For one thing, the yellow used as the main color is a very dull yellow, and hardly gaudy. The purple is a delicious metallic purple, or a more dark purple that's only distinguishable from the charcoal black under somewhat strong light. (Though as for the black itself, I would've preferred a more pure black instead of this color-- it just looks a bit better on a Transformer, I think.) The red is also the same red used for Energon Galvatron , and looks REALLY great. Individually, all of these colors aren't bad. In fact, if they had made red or black the primary color and yellow the secondary color, I think this would've been a GREAT color scheme. It's just that using yellow as the main color doesn't fit well, and coupled with the hokey purple "camoflauge" paint apps used, it makes Six Shot stand out, but in a way that's hardly menacing. (I mean, really, a PURPLE and YELLOW camoflauge scheme? ...Huh?) The camoflauge patterns also take away from details on the gun-hand and the back of his other hand that could've been filled in with different-colored paint apps that would've looked a little better. Though on the plus side, I do think the red optic on Six Shot's face looks more menacing than Shockblast's green optic. And I have to stress again how much I love the metallic purple and red used on Six Shot.
No mold changes have been made to Six Shot.
Six Shot isn't as bad as much of the fandom has made him out to be-- he's not very gaudy, it's just that yellow should NOT be his main color, and the purple camoflauge patterns are pretty hokey. But I'd still recommend Shockblast over this guy, no question.


Six Shot Bio:
MOTTO: "I will destroy my enemy's spirit as soon as I crush his body."
Six Shot is a powerful and cunning Decepticon warrior. In tank mode, his treads can crush anything that ends up beneath them. His Energon-enhanced cannon is so powerful, it is credited with bringing down two Guardian robots during the Powerlinx Battles on Cybertron. Six Shot's armor is nearly impervious and he loves to bash Autobot metal in battle-royale fashion. His ultimate goal is to defeat Optimus Prime in hand-to-fist combat and he patiently waits for the day this opportunity will arise.
