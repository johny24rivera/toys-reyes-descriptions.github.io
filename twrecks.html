T-Wrecks

Allegiance : Maximal (Dinobot)
Size : Ultra
Difficulty of Transformation : Hard
Color Scheme : Brick red, tan, neon pink, neon orange, light blue, dark blue, silver, bronze, and some neon green
Rating : 8.0

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Megatron (T-Rex). For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Megatron (T-Rex) here .)

Beast mode is a tyrannosaurus rex. Although this guy has a rather lame name, he more than makes up for it with his great color scheme. His red color scheme is perfect for a T-Rex, and makes him look really cool. Don't get me wrong, Megatron (T-Rex)'s color scheme was cool too, but a purple tyrannosaurus just reminded me a little too much of Barney.
In robot mode, T-Wrecks keeps the same pros and cons of the original Megatron (T-Rex), with a few exceptions. For one, the neon orange does NOT fit in with the other colors at all, and makes me wonder what the guy who came up with it was thinking. This is a complete repaint, with no mold variations whatsoever, not even for a Dinobot spark crystal, which is kind of a let-down. Also, it doesn't make much sense that a leader of the good guys has a face exactly like MEGATRON, the main 'bot he's up against. I really wish they would have remolded the face a little to make this fact a little less apparent. The chest compartment seems to stick on the back plate a little firmer than did the original Megatron (T-Rex), but this could just be my toy.
A great toy, except for the neon orange part of his color scheme. Recommended over the original Megatron (T-Rex).


T-Wrecks Bio :
FUNCTION: Dinobot Commander
MOTTO: "The quickest way of ending a war is to win it!"
As a strong defender of the Maximal cause, this former attack specialist stationed in sector 17 of the Mirtonian constellation has returned to Cybertron. Reformatted into the charismatic Dinobot platoon commander, T-Wrecks, his fellow Maximals bestowed him the nickname "Big Rex" as a testament to his powerhouse presence on the battlefield. Serrated steel jaws can snap most materials in two, acidic breath instantly corrodes most alloys and dual hip-mounted launchers fire air-imploding projectiles.
