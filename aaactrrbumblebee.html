Bumblebee (Rally Rumble) [Activator; Autobot Alliance]

Allegiance : Autobot
Difficulty of Transformation: Very Easy
Color Scheme: Bright yellow, light pale milky gray, black, and some red, silver, light sky blue, and metallic gold
Rating : 7.9

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the original Autobot Alliance Activator Bumblebee. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original Autobot Alliance Activator Bumblebee here .)

Bumblebee's not going to be anything but mainly black and yellow for some time, it would appear, even when it comes to his many various redecoes. However, that said, this is definitely one of the more interesting repaints that keeps Bumblebee's same basic colors intact. The yellow is a much brighter shade this time around-- it might be a bit noxiously bright to some, but I think it fits on a "Rally" version of the car, and the black contrasts exceptionally well with it. Thankfully, there's considerably more black on this version, particularly in vehicle mode, so he has a lot of great contrasting. I also quite like the red border the black on his hood has, as well as the black/red mix on the "02" rally-esque numbering on his car doors and the creative "honeycomb" patterning seen on the sides as well. They all really help to make this toy stand out on the peg. Unfortunately, he's packaged in robot mode, which isn't as big of a departure in terms of paint apps. Sure, he has a miniature version of the black/red hood paint app on his chest-- which looks great-- but his main secondary colors in this mode remain a light gray (even paler on this version than on the initial Autobot Alliance Activator) with a bit of silver on the chest. Both of these colors are too light to contrast with the already-bright yellow, and so don't really fit in with the color scheme nearly as well as additional black or red would have. The light sky blue on the eyes still works quite well there, however.
No mold changes have been made to Rally Rumble Bumblebee.
I like that Rally Rumble Bumblebee has a bit more color & paint variations than the usual "slight redeco" of Bumblebee, but it's still definitely Bumblebee. If you have the original version of this mold, this version's close enough where anyone but completists can probably pass it up. However, if you don't, this color scheme looks mighty fine in vehicle mode (though not so much in robot mode-- too bright), and I'd recommend it over the original Autobot Alliance Activator.
