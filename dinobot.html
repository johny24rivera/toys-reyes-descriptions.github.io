Dinobot

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Light brown, black, and silver, with some blue and yellow
Rating : 7.1

Beast mode is a velociraptor. Looks very nice, but his robot legs are clearly visible from his upper chest and his beast legs are sort of oddly placed at the sides of his middle. However, he does look fierce, and the color scheme is very nice.
Robot mode is very nice, with few extras. His tail opens up to reveal a spinning shield and a sword. His feet bend to form his hands, which look odd, but nice. His feet have a bone image imprinted on them in some places, and his chest is his dino head. His only extras are his dino butt on his back and his small dino arms on his sides. He has a mutant face and a face you see on the TV show, although his mutant face can come apart easily. I really wish Hasbro wouldn't have put in the mutant face, though, because it gets in the way a lot, and it can't come apart all the way.
A very nice transformer and color scheme; recommended over Grimlock , his repaint.
