Waspinator (Transmetal)

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Black, chrome gold, red, translucent light green, and cyan, with some spots of sparkly red
Rating : 6.7

Beast mode is a metallic wasp. This beast mode looks a lot better than the original Waspinators beast mode, but that isn't saying a whole lot. His insect legs won't hold him up, and his back insect legs are clearly his robot legs, as they're much thicker than the other four. Still, the color combo is pretty good, as is the detailing. The jet cockpit for the vehicle mode also clearly sticks out of the bottom of the wasp head.
Vehicle mode is a bee-plane. This isnt much of a vehicle mode- a jet cockpit just flips out of his mouth and his wings bend and fold to resemble a jets wings. There is also a lot of extras on the bottom, especially his insect legs.
Robot mode is okay, except there is no locking mechanism, so his bee chest can be in several different places around the chest area. His insect legs stick out in this mode and get in the way. His face looks almost the same as the original except for the colors.
Not particularly great in any of its modes; the worst Transmetal, in my opinion, but still an okay transformer overall.
