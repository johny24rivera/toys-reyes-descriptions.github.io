Strafe (Autobot Alliance)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size: Voyager
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Moderately light gray, red, silver, and some transparent orange, pearly white, black, dark gray, light yellowish orange, and blue
Rating : 9.3

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Revenge of the Fallen Mindwipe. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Revenge of the Fallen Mindwipe here .)

Strafe is a light (very light) homage to the G1 Technobot of the same name, but this is really only apparent regarding some of his colors and his personality described in his bio. Strafe's main color is a fairly straightforward gray, but that hardly means his scheme's boring-- Strafe here has a LOT of paint apps, as well as different colors of plastic. With the exception of a large portion of the center top of his vehicle mode being gray, there isn't any large part of him that's the same color. Red and white are his main secondary colors, and both contrast excellently with the gray-- particularly the red. He also has a fair amount of orange, in terms of paint apps that help to break up large sections of gray as well as in terms of transparent plastic, which looks quite nice when it has light shining through it. He also has silver paint apps on his wings, which go well with the red, and, in a bit of a creative twist, make the wings look like they're slivers of a (very) large Autobot insignia. Normally, this would be brilliant, in my opinion-- however, what with the whole point of this "Reveal the Shield" subline being that their allegiances are NOT incredibly apparent, it seems a bit silly in this subline. (Speaking of the "Reveal the Shield" gimmick, the Autobot allegiance rub symbol for Strafe is on his right shoulder in robot mode-- rubbing it reveals, of course, that he's an Autobot.) Along with a bit of black and blue, it all makes for a pretty original scheme, if a bit on the loud side. Normally I'd like such a color scheme, but there's just one huge, major problem-- this is a stealth bomber . Complete with the half-Autobot insignias on the wings, the yellow, the red, and the white, this has to be the most UNstealthy color scheme for this vehicle mode I've ever seen, and very ill-fitting.
No mold changes have been made to Autobot Alliance Strafe.
Autobot Alliance Strafe has a color scheme that-- by itself-- is pretty original and eye-catching while also being a light homage to the G1 character. However, it doesn't fit a stealth bomber in the least, and the funky color scheme just doesn't suit the movie-stylized mold at all. I'd recommend RotF Mindwipe way over this version because of that, even though no matter what version you get, you're getting a fantastic mold.


Strafe Bio :
Most of the time, Strafe is a pleasant guy to be around, if a little bit jumpy. But if something startles him, it's a better than even chance that everything around him will end up with at least one hole burned through it. Loud noises, alarms, bacon sizzling-- pretty much anything can set him off. Other Autobots tend to stay away from him on the battlefield, preferring to let his spastic fight reaction burn itself out on Decepticons before the move in to capture the cowering survivors.
