Under Three McDonald's Beast Wars Toy

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Happy Meal Size
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme : Yellow, red, and black, with some silver
Rating : 1.1

Beast mode is actually not a beast, but just a lion's head with an obvious hinge sticking out of the top. Although the head is somewhat detailed, it features no colors except one, yellow, with no movable parts whatsoever, except the hinge, which is used to "transform" him. Still, you can't expect too much for a toy that is for kids under three....
Robot mode is a little better than beast mode, but not by much. Although there are still no moveable parts with the exception of the hinge, there is, at least, a recognizable robot body inside. This robot body is flat, and somewhat corny looking. The large extras of the lion head on the side make him look more like a flattened robot on pavement than an actual transformer.
Very corny, in my opinion. Not recommended unless you are a die-hard collector.
