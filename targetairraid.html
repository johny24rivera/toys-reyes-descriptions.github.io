Air Raid (Movie) [Target Exclusive]

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Scout
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme : Light milky gray, black, jungle green, and some light muddy brown and cherry red
Rating : 9.3

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Energon Skyblast. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Energon Skyblast here .)

Air Raid is the second repaint of the Skyblast mold for the Target Exclusive scout class movie line, and this time sports a very military-esque color scheme. The light milky gray and black, by themselves, aren't particularly interesting of a color scheme, but the jungle green makes all the difference and really goes well with both colors while spicing things upaÂ  little-- particularly with the black and muddy brown "blocky camoflauge" paint apps covering the main body of Air Raid. I mean, really, they're ALL OVER the place-- if it wasn't for the light milky gray parts, it really would be rather hard to pick out Air Raid among some foilage. I'm not as fond of the square camoflauge as I am of the more traditional "curvy" camoflauge patterns, however, more because of personal preference than anything else (not to mention that real plants and foilage aren't shaped like boxes). The bits of red look the best against the other colors, even though it's only used in three tiny places (at the base of each wing and then on the robot optics). It really adds some great contrast. Adding to the military feel is a painted "S7" logo on the bottom of the chestpiece, which looks cool as always and helps Air Raid to "blend in". There are a few paint apps Air Raid is missing that he really needs, though-- namely, colored Autobot symbols on his wings and camoflauge paint apps on the underside of his wings-- as it is, there's only paint apps on the topside ofhis wings.
No mold changes have been made to Air Raid.
Air Raid has a more interesting-- and slightly more realistic-- color scheme than Movie Skyblast , but I still think he falls ever-so-slightly short of the original color scheme used for this mold. If you can't find Energon Skyblast, though, this is a nice substitute, as it's a great mold.


Air Raid Bio:
Cybertron has laws, and the Decepticons have broken almost all of them. It makes Air Raid sick just thinking about it. Respect for the law is what separates sentient creatures from simple drones. The Decepticons make a mockery of themselves and their fellow robots by their disregard for the law. Not for much longer, though, if Air Raid has anything to say about it. He's spent centuries hunting the worst Decepticon criminals from one end of the galaxy to the other, and he's not stopping until every last one is caught and punished.
