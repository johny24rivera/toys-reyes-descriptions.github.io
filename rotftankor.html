Tankor (RotF)

Allegiance : Decepticon
Size: Legend
Difficulty of Transformation : Very Easy
Color Scheme: Light sky blue and some pale yellow, silver, moderately dark red, and metallic gunmetal gray
Rating : 7.7

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the TF Movie Legends class Brawl toy. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the TF Movie Legends class Braw toy here .)

Well, here's something that doesn't happen often-- a Beast Machines homage! Yessir, Tankor here is a repaint homage to the BM character of the same name. It's not exact due to the small scale, but there's some definite similarities. The main color is light blue here, whereas BM Tankor's colors were white and dark blue, so barring that this is a nice compromise. The main giveaway (beyond the name) that' this is an homage to the BW character are the robot mode details, however. The yellow "caution"-esque lines on the legs, similar lines on the left side of his chest, the red X on the right side of his chest, the silver paint apps on his face and chest, the color of his treads-- all are copied pretty closely from BM Tankor. (Tankor actually had missile pods in the shape of an "X" on that side of his chest, but at this scale it's close enough, really.) My favorite paint job nod, though, is that a red line is painted not only on the eyes, but on the "nose ridge" between them as well. At first this might seem like an odd oversight, but it the nose ridge poking out through the eyes, when looked at from a head-on angle, actually looks like Tankor's single "roaming eye" due to the lighting! One of the most ingenious paint apps I've ever seen on an homage toy when the original mold clearly had no intention of being this character. Unfortunately, all this attention to this robot mode does have a downside-- Tankor's pretty sparsely painted in tank mode, with no paint apps at all on his turret.
No mold changes have been made to RotF Tankor.
RotF Tankor is a fantastic repaint homage with some nicely done paint apps in robot mode, but his vehicle mode suffers from very few paint apps and it desperately needs some. Recommended if you like Beast Machines (or Tankor), otherwise it's roughly even with Legends class Brawl in terms of the colors, in my opinion. Oh, what they could have done with this homage on the deluxe scale...
