Sideswipe (Scan Series) [DotM]

Allegiance: Autobot
Size : Deluxe
Price : $15 (U.S.)
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Silver, clear plastic, blue, dark brownish gray, and some light electric blue, moderately light milky gray, grayish white, white, and light red
Rating : 9.7

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the original Revenge of the Fallen deluxe Sideswipe toy. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original RotF deluxe Sideswipe toy here .)

Like other figures in the "Scan" exclusive subline, Sideswipe's vehicle mode is half "normal", while the back half is mostly clear plastic with some really cool electric blue "cyber lines" clustered around a white line that's supposed to represent a scanned vehicle mode-- this is Sideswipe in mid-reformatting, essentially. The "scan" effect is really sweet, and the clear plastic parts used for the rear of his vehicle mode and most of the upper half of his robot mode look pretty cool-- you can see all the little plastic parts inside, though this does have the side effect of making Sideswipe's robot head visible in vehicle mode, and with no tinted plastic whatsoever for the windows, it's considerably easier to see his robot legs inside, where the drivers' seat should be. The clear plastic isn't the only plastic change for this version of Sideswipe, though; he also has a pretty good amount of blue in his robot mode now, which is a GREAT shade and helps add some color to a scheme that was pretty monochromatic originally. There's also a bit of light electric blue plastic at the waist, which looks good. On the other hand, the main body is painted a shade of silvery white that doesn't quite match the silver paint used on most of his vehicle parts. Because of all the paint used for the "scan" paint apps, his front bumper is also completely undetailed when it comes to paint.
No mold changes have been made to DotM Scan Series Sideswipe.
Scan Series Sideswipe has the same awesome "scan line" paint apps and clear plastic that the other toys in the subline have, but the blue added to the robot mode is an even better addition, in my opinion. Recommended over the original version of the mold, though I still think the "Hunters' Rumble" Target 2-pack beats this version out slightly as having the best color scheme overall.
