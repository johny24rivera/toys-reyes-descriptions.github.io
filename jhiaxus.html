Jhiaxus (KB Toys Exclusive)

Allegiance : Decepticon
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Hard
Japanese Name : N/A; RiD-only release
Color Scheme : Bright orange, light gray, shiny black, flat black, and some transparent light red
Mold History : Jhiaxus' mold was originally used for the deluxe version of Beast Machines Jetstorm, which came out back in 2000.
Rating : 8.4

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to deluxe Beast Machines Jetstorm. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of deluxe Beast Machines Jetstorm here .)

The fourth iteration of the Beast Machines deluxe Jetstorm mold is probably the worst. For starters, he's Jhiaxus has got the same problem here that his previous mold buddy Storm Jet had- he's only got a few colors on him. Granted, they're spaced out over the toy so it's not as obvious, but still... more colors should have been added on a deluxe toy. He's also got the exact same paint apps as Storm Jet (just in different colors), so there's not much imagination that went into his design there. He's also got the same exact colors of black and transparent red that were on Storm Jet in their respective places. The relationship between the two is undeniable. However, whereas Storm Jet at least has a good color scheme, Jhiaxus really doesn't. The bright orange and gray really don't work well together- instead of the orange, maybe gold, with the gray a bit darker? He's also prone to the same "sticky elbows" situation that the Storm Jet version of the Deluxe Jetstorm mold had. What makes it even worse is that Jhiaxus' name is taken from a popular G2 comic book character, but his color scheme couldn't be FARTHER from Jhiaxus' G2 color scheme. (Which was green, gold and red, if I remember correctly.) Why give him the brand-new name if he doesn't resemble the character? At least he has some nice Decepticon symbols tampoed on his cockpit and his spark crystal.
No mold changes have been made to Jhiaxus.
Jhiaxus- or, as I like to call him, Halloweenie- is definitely the Jetstorm repaint that has had the least thought put into him. Same paint apps as Storm Jet, but with an ugly orange dominating his color scheme. It's a shame, because the mold itself is great. Not recommended unless you can't find any other versions of this mold at a reasonable price.
