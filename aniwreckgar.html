Wreck-Gar (Animated)

Allegiance : Autobot
Size : Voyager
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Light milky gray, light orange, black, and some dark gray, dull red, pale yellowish white, metallic gunmetal gray, light yellow, light red, and transparent light greenish blue
Rating : 7.3

Hi there, fellow Internet-traveler! I am Wreck-Gar! I am a hero! I am also a transforming garbage truck-- after all, I am good for only one thing, GARBAGE! In my alternate mode, I am pretty small for a Voyager class toy, but I am also pretty compact! Even for an Animated toy, though, I don't have much mold detailing-- wait, is that a bad thing? *listens to Beastbot's whispers* Ah, okay. It is a bad thing, as I look rather "kiddy" in this mode, almost like I am a toy from Fisher-Price (whatever that is)! However, I have no robot extras showing at all in this mode, though I don't have much clearance for my tires! My main "gimmick" in this mode involves pulling back on the black tab on the back of each of my garbage can levers, which will then make the front halves of the levers rotate upwards to "throw trash" into my back compartment! My back compartment isn't open for trash, though-- it houses my robot mode weapons-- so instead I just throw trash all down my sides! It's awesome! My color scheme consists mostly of orange, red, and gray, with some black, and is very reminiscent of my G1 version! (I have a G1 version?!) My colors go together alright, but they're nothing to stare at! I have a fair number of paint applications, though the top of my back half has no paint at all, which makes it look rather bland!
As is evident from my transformation, I, Wreck-Gar, am a total shell-former in robot mode! Almost my entire robot mode is hidden under or in the back half of my vehicle mode, and almost my entire vehicle mode is on my back in robot mode! Only four of my wheels and my front bumper are shared between both of my modes (and the bumper makes for a swell chest, I must say-- or must I?)! Having almost my entire alt mode on my back causes me to be rather back-heavy in robot mode, and makes me hard to stand up in this mode-- plus, my garbage-lifters stick out from below my waist and look really unsightly! (Alternatively, if you fold them up, it makes me even more back-heavy, so Beastbot does not recommend that!) My general robot mode proportions are very good, though, and fit Animated's style perfectly-- particularly in my legs, which have the trademark Animated "angular curve" to them! My face sculpt is also top-notch, with the expression fitting my hilariously nieve personality perfectly and homaging my G1 self at the same time! Plus, it looks like I have an old TV for a head! How awesome is that? Beastbot tells me it is very awesome, which makes me wonder why he just told me to ask you! My articulation is also pretty good, as I am able to move at the neck (at two points), shoulders (at three points), elbows, at the base of the four fingers of each of my hands (with all four fingers on each hand on one hinge), and at the hips (at three points) and knees (at two points)! Unfortunately, as I said earlier, I am so back-heavy this limits my poses a lot in spite of this! My main gimmick in this mode involves blade-like weapons that pop out of my back and attach to my lower arms so I can RUN WITH SCISSORS! Beastbot isn't sure if that homage to this "Weird Al" person was intentional or not, but he loves it regardless!
I am Wreck-Gar! I am a mediocre Transformer! Though I am a great homage to the G1 toy and this "Weird Al" fellow, I also have very little mold detailing, am sort of small for a Voyager, am backheavy, and am a total shellformer! Still, if you are a Weird Al fan or just a fan of my character, Beastbot still mildly recommends me because of my personality! YAY ! Let's go play in the landfill to celebrate! *runs off*


Wreck-Gar Character Bio :
AUTOBOT ROOKIE
"Anything I can do to help?"
Wreck-Gar means well. All he really wants to do is help folks in need. Unfortunately, he's a terrible judge of character, and happily helps anyone who asks without thinking about what he's doing. He has accidentally robbed a few banks, and blasted an Autobot here and there. His Spark is in the right place anyway. Now that he's clear on the difference between the Autobots and the Decepticons, misunderstandings like that should happen less often.
GALACTIC POWERS AND ABILITIES:
- Loves to collect things other folks throw away
- Watches even more TV than Bumblebee
- Believes everything he reads on the internet
