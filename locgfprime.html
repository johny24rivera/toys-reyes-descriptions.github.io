Optimus Prime (Galaxy Force Redeco; Legends of Cybertron)

Allegiance : Autobot
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme : Dull red, silver, charcoal black, and some white, light milky gray, and moderately light blue
Rating : 6.5

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the original Legends of Cybertron Optimus Prime. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original Legends of Cybertron Optimus Prime here .)

(For a review on the larger Galaxy Force Optimus Prime toy that this is based on, check out the review here .)

Just like the larger Galaxy Force Optimus Prime redeco, this Legends of Cybertron repaint is not nearly different enough from the original. It does look slightly better than the original LoC Prime when it comes to the color scheme, though. I especially like that the blue has been changed to black, but other than a few other minor paint app changes and the addition of the planet symbols on Prime's shoulders, there's really pretty much nothing else to differentiate this repaint of Optimus Prime from the original LoC toy. It's a shame, and quite frankly a waste of plastic to crank out a repaint that's so similar to the original.
No mold changes have been made to LoC Galaxy Force Optimus Prime.
LoC Galaxy Force Optimus Prime does look a little better than the original use of the mold, but the blue to black is the only big color change on the toy. Just like on the larger Galaxy Force redeco, Hasbro put too much effort into making him still look like Optimus Prime and not enough effort into making him look different enough color-wise from the original toy. If you don't have the original LoC Prime toy and really want this pretty mediocre mold, then by all means pick up this one, but otherwise skip it.
