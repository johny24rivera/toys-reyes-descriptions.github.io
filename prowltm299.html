Prowl Transmetal 2 (Black Variant)

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Transparent dark red, dark blue, silver, chrome silver, chrome dark blue, black, and some gold, neon orange, and light green
Rating : 8.1

Beast mode is a transmetal 2 owl. This mode looks very nice, and has a better color scheme than the original Transmetal 2 Prowl , in my opinion. In fact, the color scheme is so different, this could be considered a "dark" clone of the original Prowl Transmetal 2. The detailing is awesome in this mode, and there are a few neat features, especially on the head. The head can rotate all the way around by pressing a button on Prowl's back, and if you "unhook" the wings from their slots and lift them up, he can do a "slicing attack" by having his wings rotate around his body! Another neat feature is the transparent red half-orb on top of Prowl's beast head. There are little pieces of shiny silver plastic crafted to look like "silicon chips" inside! Flip the orb over to reveal the Maximal allegiance orb. Yet another nice additon to this toy is the nice, transparent red right eye that Prowl has, and the crafted plastic on the inside of the eye looks very good, giving Prowl a "cyborg" look. The only major downside to this mode is the very obvious robot arm extras between Prowl's two "rocket jet packs" hanging from his waist. These tend to get in the way some and look very odd. However, this is not enough to override the other neat features this toy has.
Robot mode is a bit of a disappointment when compared to the beast mode, but still looks decent. The detailing, excellent color scheme, and the see-through orbs, as well as the robot mode face, are still very cool. The robot mode face has transparent red plastic from the optics to the upper back of the head, and if you hold his face up to light, it makes it appears as if his optics are "glowing". The silver-painted "rune" on Prowl's forehead, which the original Prowl Transmetal 2 did not have, adds to the "wise" look of this 'bot. One of the eyes is circular, while the other is more in the shape of a human eye, and this gives Prowl a bit of a "cyborg" look as well. There are a few more downsides to this mode than there were in the last one, however. First, the arms in this mode are very small when compared to other parts of Prowl's body. Second, the robot chest (the owl head) sticks out a bit too far, and the combination of the "rotating chest" (left over from the owl head) and the two ears of the owl make his chest look very odd indeed. The last problem is probably the most annoying one. Despite this being a repaint, Hasbro still didn't fix the problem of the wings jiggling on Prowl shoulder's when you move the Maximal. This is a little noisy and gets a bit annoying after a while.
A very nice transformer, with a COOL color scheme. Recommended over the original Prowl Transmetal 2.


Prowl Transmetal 2 Bio :
FUNCTION: Military Strategist
MOTTO: "Logic is the ultimate weapon."
Prowl strives to find logic and reason in everything. A listener, not a talker. Has most sophisticated logic center of the Maximals. Able to analyze and advise on complex combat situations almost instantaneously. Fires highly corrosive acid pellets. Cybernetic eye and frontal lobe interact with wing-mounted ion orbs, supplying limited telekinetic power. Afflicted with ironic, dry sense of humor. Believes himself to have been a great military strategist in a former life.
