Sky Shadow

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Hard
Color Scheme : Translucent gold, green, and dark blue, with spots of red and yellow
Rating : 8.4

Beast mode is an iguana with dragonfly wings and dragonfly eyes on his back, with a dragonfly tail. Kind of cool-looking, but sophisticated. The tip of his tail looks weird, though, because its his head in robot mode. As usual, nice detailing and color scheme throughout. To activate his action feature, you just push the knob on top of the dragonfly eyes up and down, activating a "wing-buzzing" action. Pretty neat.
Robot mode is kind of cool, and very organic-looking. His face resembles that of Transquitos , including the face with a missile that can shoot out of it. However, this missle doesnt shoot very far because you have to push it. His tail folds up to become his head, but connects to his body well. There are a moderate amount of extras, though, including his front iguana legs and his iguana neck pieces. His right arm is a claw made from two of his dragonfly wings and his right hand is a claw with his iguana head on the bottom, making this arm look odd.
This is a difficult figure to transform, so give yourself a good 7 minutes or so the first few times you transform his guy. A great transformer overall; in my opinion, the best of the fuzors, although he has a bit of extras in his robot mode.


Sky Shadow Bio :
FUNCTION: Camouflage Expert
An intelligent, brilliantly colored robot, Sky Shadow is one of the most charismatic and popular of the Predacons. A good statesman and leader, he acts as one of Megatrons most trusted counselors. In actuality however, he despises everyone and wants nothing more than to rule the Predacons with his own, "more imaginative techniques." He is an expert in concealment and surprise-assault tactics; with reptilian patience he can lie for days in wait and then suddenly attack with a powerful turbo-missile, or convert to robot form and overwhelm unexpecting Maximals with his devastating saw-edged pincer. Powerful wings allow him to hover over targets, giving him a distinct advantage with targeting accuracy and in reconnaissance missions.
