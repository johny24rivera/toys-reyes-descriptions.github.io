Jungle Bonecrusher

Allegiance : Decepticon
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Dark jungle green, dull greenish tan, charcoal gray, and some electric light blue, light red, silver, moderately light jungle green, and light metallic silvery blue
Rating : 9.7

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to the original Movie deluxe Bonecrusher toy. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of the original Movie deluxe Bonecrusher toy here .)

Jungle Bonecrusher is certainly jungle-themed, that's for sure. The dark green and light green colors are particularly close representations of foilage colors, and the tan parts on him look a bit like dirt while still hearkening back to his original colors. That said, the sudden change in colors from the dark green to the tan does, admittedly, make it look like Bonecrusher is wearing a green sweater, as has often been said in the Transfandom. Using light green on the legs would have helped to alleviate this a bit, I think. The color accents on Jungle Bonecrusher are great, particularly the silver-- it REALLY fits nicely against the dark green on his chest. Jungle Bonecrusher's prerequisite electric "Allspark Power" blue also fits in well with the overall color scheme, again particularly well with the green (but not so much with the tan). In vehicle mode, Jungle Bonecrusher is entirely dark green-- I'm not sure if this is particularly realistic for a minesweeper vehicle, but I think it looks nice regardless, and the vehicle mode being almost entirely one color at least attempts to make the color scheme look more realistic. Oh, and he still has his famous "dog with huge teeth" Bonecrusher insignia visible in vehicle mode. Hooray!
No mold changes have been made to Jungle Bonecrusher.
Jungle Bonecrusher is a great repaint, giving completely new, complimentary colors to Bonecrusher while still retaining the essence of the character's colors in robot mode. That said, his color scheme isn't quite as realistic (though more exciting-looking) than the original Bonecrusher toy's, and having dark green arms but tan legs makes it look like he's wearing a green sweater. A bit less of the tan would have made him look slightly better, I think. Recommended slightly over the original Bonecrusher, if only because the color scheme is a bit more varied.


Jungle Bonecrusher Bio:
Barely functional after his battle with Optimus Prime, Bonecrusher crawled away from the scene of his defeat and hid. Effecting what few repairs he could on his own, he also removed his locator beacon and communications hardware, cutting himself off entirely from the other surviving Decepticons. Fleeing south, farther and farther, he eventually found himself deep in the jungles of South America. Hidden in the most remote area on Earth, he is free to indulge his rage on the rainforest without interruption.
