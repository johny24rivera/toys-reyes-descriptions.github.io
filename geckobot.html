Geckobot

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Basic
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme : Dark turquoise, transparent purple, gold, red, greenish yellow, and light red
Rating : 6.7

Beast mode is some type of flying lizard. This mode looks extremely cool- in fact, I'd go so far as to say that it's the best beast mode of the basic Beast Machines Maximals (excluding the Battle for the Spark line). It has a very fierce look to it- especially the beast face. The detailing is exquisite on this toy, which is another plus. Here's an interesting thing to note- the spark crystal, which is located under a panel on the top of Geckobot's beast head, actually serves as her eyes when the panel is closed, which was very creative on the designer's part. The action gimmick is rather weak, however- by pushing the front fin of each wing forward, you make the other three move backward. Wow, like that serves a purpose. I suppose it could be interpreted as some kind of "wing-flapping action", but it really doesn't look like it. Thee other, albeit minor, downsides to this mode are the lack of jaw movement, the easily visible robot head on Geckobot's lower neck, and the fact that this toy is rather small for a basic.
Robot mode is...well...to put it bluntly, horrid. With the exception of great posability and detailing, there are no redeeming features to this mode. The color scheme on the main body is mostly limited to dark turquoise in this mode- it would have been nice if there was a bit more color variation, as it makes this look a bit more like a prototype in this mode than the final product. The beast head and tail sort of hang off the back, and although they don't get in the way, they look rather odd- most Beast Machines toys have few, if any, extras in either mode. Most obvious, however, is the incredibly odd articulation of the arms. The arms can bend in five different places each, and, to add to this oddity, the back part of the arms is lodged behind the front part. I tried for a long while to pose Geckobot's large arms in a position that would have made them look normal for the picture above, but it was nigh impossible. An interesting thing to note about Geckobot's head and body is that is decidedly neutral in gender- the tech spec never refers to Geckobot as a "he" or "she" either, making the owner responsible for deciding Geckobot's gender.
An excellent beast mode, but this is offset by a very bad robot mode.


Geckobot Bio :
FUNCTION: Aerial Combat Specialist
MOTTO: "I will blind you with my brilliance!"
One of the few Maximals capable of aerial operations, Geckobot is extremely swift and maneuverable when flying. Equipped with storage cell on back that powers a variety of energy pulse weapons. Wings and tail act as super-sensitive energy receptors, giving Geckobot a high level of endurance during long-range reconnaissance or attack missions. Very intelligent and create - enjoys constructing 'garbage art' sculpture out of destroyed Vehicons.
