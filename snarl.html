Snarl

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Basic
Difficulty of Transformation : Easy
Color Scheme : Dark grey and brown, with some white, light blue, and red
Rating : 5.9

Beast mode is a tasmanian devil. Very fierce-looking, and nicely crafted. His sides are sort of loose, though, and can flip back easily. His robot extras are only visible if you look at him fron the rear, so this beast mode is pretty nicely done. The mouth and the four legs can all move, so this beast mode is a little more poseable than its other basic counterparts.
Robot mode has a lot of extras. His tasmanian devil sides flip back and look rather awkward. His tasmanian devil face becomes his feet, and looks even more awkward than his back. His legs are a little skinny, and he tends to lean backward in this mode because of it. However, he has a cool tailgun and a nicely crafted face, arms, and chest.
A decent transformer, but I've seen better.


Snarl Bio :
FUNCTION: Surprise Attack
Snarl is seldom seen until the moment he attacks. His camoflage coloring allows him to sneak into Predacon basecamps undetected. Powerful legs give him the ability to lunge forward to attack startled enemies, and in robot mode he can jump straight up to escape incoming missile fire. Snarl is a fearless fighter whose gnashing, serrated teeth and razor-sharp claws are enough to fend off would-be attackers...if they know what's good for them!
