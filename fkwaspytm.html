Waspinator Transmetal (Fox Kids)

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Yellow, bright yellow, transmetal black, black, translucent light green, pale brown-orange, and some copper and bright pinkish red
Rating : 7.1

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Waspinator Transmetal. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Waspinator Transmetal here .)

The Fox Kids' repaint of Waspinator Transmetal is very nice. The color scheme fits that of a wasp quite a bit better than the black-and-gold color combo of the original Waspinator Transmetal. The yellows and blacks fit together extremely nicely. I especially love the transmetal black, it has an "oily" look to it. Because he looks so much like the original Beast Wars toy Buzzsaw in this mode, many Transfans have dubbed this repaint "Transmetal Buzzsaw", and it fits. However, the improved color scheme still does not make this a very good toy. No mold changes were made to the repaint, although the ball joints on the insect legs seem a bit tighter then the original's. But this could just be my toy. This toy is still a rather mediocre mold, with quite a bit of extras, especially in robot mode.
Still, because of the improved color scheme, I recommend this toy over the original paint job of Waspinator Transmetal. Just remember it's a mediocre toy to begin with, and you won't be disappointed.
