Toxitron (Botcon 2011 Attendee-Only Exclusive)

Allegiance : Decepticon
Size : Voyager (comes in a 2-pack with Sideswipe )
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Black, dull flat yellow, moderately dark purple, dull purplish gray, and some transparent cherry red, silver, light orange, light red, and bright chalky green
Rating: 9.2

(NOTE: Because this is a repaint, this is not a full-blown review. This mainly covers any changes made to the mold and the color scheme, and merely compares it to Animated voyager Optimus Prime. For a review on the mold itself, read the review of Animated voyager Optimus Prime here .)

Toxitron has a rather interesting history-- originally planned as a redeco of G2 "Laser Rod" Optimus Prime, the famously horrendously-colored redeco was cancelled. The idea was loved, though, and here it's revived, with Toxitron being the Bizarro to Animated Optimus' Superman. The color scheme is obviously not for everyone, but it fits an Animated character especially well-- the purple, black, and dull purplish gray serve as nice "Decepticon-y" base colors, with the yellow having a distinctly toxic shade to it and contrasting well with other main colors (and this coming from someone who normally doesn't like yellow as a main color). The transparent red plastic used for the sirens and windows is also a great shade, and contrasts fantastically against the yellow (same with the red paint, to boot). The light orange "toxic drips" around Toxitron's gun nozzle, left shoulder, and on the sides of his vehicle mode are well done, though against the yellow it's light enough where it's hard to see. To top it all off, Toxitron's face is a horrendously sick-looking color of green, which just goes great with the rest of his theme.
No mold changes have been made to Toxitron.
Toxitron is definitely for a rather specific audience (hence why he's a Botcon exclusive)-- if you like Animated, liked Toxitron's so-bad-it's-good color scheme, and "themed" redecos like this one, than this one is definitely for you. If you like your Transformers more realistic and subdued, though, this is one to avoid at all costs. (Compared with Motormaster, I think this one's colors are SLIGHTLY worse, but it's still surprisingly good-looking given the colors that it works with, with care obviously put into the details.)


Toxitron Bio :
FUNCTION: Decoy
MOTTO: "Subjugation am not right of all insentient beings."
Toxitron was the first attempt by Doctor Scalpel and Oil Slick to protoform a warrior in an Autobot shell. The failed experiment produced a bizarre duplicate of Optimus Prime with immense strength, corrosive toxic sludge cannons, a hopelessly stunted intellect, and a penchant for driving backwards in vehicle mode. Fortunately for the Decepticons, the data from this mishap led to greater success with Projects Stunticon and Nemesis Prime.
