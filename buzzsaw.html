Buzz Saw

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Yellow, black, and dark green and light green, with some creamy cyan and purple
Rating : 6.5

Beast mode is a hornet. This beast mode is nice, except his back insect legs are his robot legs folded out, so they look like mutated hooves. His hands are also kind of visible in this mode too from the underside. The articulation in this mode is very limited, since only the wings and back legs can move. The detailing isn't very good on this toy, either.
Robot mode is nice, but with some extras. His front two insect legs stick out a lot and get in the way some, and his insect abdomen also gets in the way a little. His hornet face halves are nicely placed and he has two heads: a "mutant" head like Waspinators , and a face that looks like an Autobots. His stinger becomes a missle launcher and parts of his wings can become missles.
A nicely done deluxe in robot mode, but the beast mode isn't all that great. A repaint of Waspinator, although the coloring isn't quite as nice.


Buzz Saw Bio :
FUNCTION: Aerial Surveillance
Hunts enemy Maximals from altitudes of 20,000 feet. Uses infrared scanning sensors wired within its eyes to detect movement below, then zeroes in and launches powerful air-to-ground missiles to induce temporary paralysis. Buzz Saw can exit Earth's atmosphere at any time, but prefers hunting land-based enemies where it has he advantage of flight. Known to gang-up on its victims with its evil partner, the wicked Waspinator.
