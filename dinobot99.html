Dinobot (Fox Kids)

Allegiance : Maximal
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Red, tan-gold, dark brown, and some black, silver, and neon green
Rating : 7.6

Beast mode is a velociraptor. This mode looks VERY cool, and is a nice Dinobot repaint. It looks like Dinobot was trapped in mud during a storm that was raining blood. One of the nice features in this mode is the green eyes- they have no pupils! This makes Dinobot look very much like a cold-blooded killer- much more Predacon than Maximal. However, this mode suffers from the same big complaint as the other original Dinobot and Grimlock; the robot legs are clearly visible from any angle, and make Dinobot look big, bulky, and awkward.
Robot mode looks very nice as well. His color scheme has become more brown than red now. There are a few extras in this mode, such as the velociraptor arms and a section of the tail on the back, but these do not get in the way much. His spinning shield that is silver on the inside is very cool. One of the major problems I have with this mode is his "mutant head"- it gets in the way a lot, and it would have been a better toy if Hasbro had just forgot the darn thing.
A very nice toy, with few overall extras and a nice color scheme. Recommended over the original, as this toy seems to be of higher quality than Grimlock and the original Dinobot.
