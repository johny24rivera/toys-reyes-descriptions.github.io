Blackarachnia

Allegiance : Predacon
Size : Deluxe
Difficulty of Transformation : Medium
Color Scheme : Light brown, transparent black, gold, purple, green, and some creamy cyan
Rating : 7.3

Beast mode is a black widow spider. This beast mode is extremely well-crafted, except for her feet showing on her butt, and her grappling hook stuck on it also. The detailing is exquisite, and all eight of her legs can move, although the articulation ends there. The fangs seem a bit big proportionally, though, and the abdomen seems a bit small.
Robot mode is also nice, with few extras. Although this robot mode doesnt look all that much like Blackarachnia on the TV show, it does have the same basic body structure, with her spider legs sticking out of her arms, that also can double as missles for a gun with a large grappling hook. The spider legs do not get in the way much, though. However, the string attached to the hook may get in the way and get tangled a lot. Her claws arent nearly as big as those on the TV show, and her legs are somewhat short. She is a repaint of Tarantulas , and although Tarantulas colors are, in my opinion, better, these colors are a little more realistic in beast mode.
A nice transformer with few extras, and an extremely nice beast mode.


Blackarachnia Bio :
FUNCTION: Double Agent
Moving like a venomous shadow under a midnight moon, Blackarachnia is an evil temptress posing as a Predacon double agent! Capable of converting thermal energy into poisonous cybervenom, she lures Maximals into her darkened den and then stuns them by firing a poison-tipped missile! After restraining them in her powerful legs, she then sinks her fangs into their armor to suck their life-force dry.
